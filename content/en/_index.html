We are Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant.

author: >- Manifesto job: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: accent_background text: >-

Distinguished by the French government, we are proud to carry the colors of French artisanal and industrial excellence around the world.

True to our shared values of respect, high standards and innovation, we strive to cultivate and pass on the rare know-how of our elders. As players in responsible, respectful manufacturing, we are as committed to the quality and durability of our products as we are to the well-being and training of the talents who shape them.

As creative and passionate ambassadors, the men and women of our companies are always on the lookout for trends and developments that will enable them to stay at the cutting edge of their professions and meet new expectations.
