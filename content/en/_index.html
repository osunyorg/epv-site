Réseau Excellence is a regional network of companies awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label.

body: >-

The association
Réseau Excellence federates EPV associations that wish to work together to promote exceptional know-how. Through targeted actions, Réseau Excellence supports and develops a dynamic community driven by shared values.
Find out more


Our network spans every region and draws its strength from various committed, open-minded and dynamic businesses. A national office federates these local networks of excellence for optimum coordination and impact, and represents them in the French and international craft and industrial ecosystems. 500 EPV-labelled member companies in 12 regions of mainland and overseas France

We are Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant.
Manifesto


Distinguished by the French government, we are proud to carry the colors of French artisanal and industrial excellence around the world. True to our shared values of respect, high standards and innovation, we strive to cultivate and pass on the rare know-how of our elders. As players in responsible, respectful manufacturing, we are as committed to the quality and durability of our products as we are to the well-being and training of the talents who shape them . As creative and passionate ambassadors, the men and women of our companies are always on the lookout for trends and developments that will enable them to stay at the cutting edge of their professions and meet new expectations.


To be awarded the label is to support a project for the future in our territories, with the young people we train: that of anchoring French manufacturing excellence in a heritage that is our shared pride . Our commitments
