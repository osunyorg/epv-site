Our company has been producing cotton, linen and synthetic yarns for many years for its high-end textile customers.

Our company is proud to be one of the few French companies still manufacturing a very diverse range of yarns in its own workshops.

Our company has been producing cotton, linen and synthetic yarns for many years for its high-end textile customers.

These threads are intended for the weaving, lace, embroidery, clothing industries and many other specific areas: quilting, trimmings, ribbons, leather goods, bookbinding ...

Part of the production is intended for the textile industries ... The other part is intended for the general public through our brands: Fil au Chinois, Le Baufil, La Laine Saint Pierre, Decouvit  

These products are distributed in a large number of haberdashery shops in France, but also abroad. Our goal is to consolidate this acquired know-how and to offer always optimum choice, quality and service, to allow our customers to give free rein to their creations.

In order to respect the environment and people, we have differents labels: Okeo-Tex, EPV (Réseau Excellence), GOTS FRANCE, TERRE TEXTILE, MASTERS OF LINEN.
