Atelier François Pouenat is a workshop of ironwork and metalwork of French art specialized in the creation of custom parts in iron, brass, copper and aluminum. A heir to a family know-how passed on for more than 130 years, he collaborates with international architects and designers to create high-end furniture, decorative elements and interior fittings. The workshop combines traditional techniques and contemporary tools to design unique projects, ranging from pieces of furniture to architectural achievements for prestigious places such as hotels or private residences.
