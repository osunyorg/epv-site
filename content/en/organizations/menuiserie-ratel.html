Since 1954, the RATEL Carpentry has been engaged in the restoration of heritage, both in external carpentry (making doors, windows) and in interior carpentry (honeys to preach, confessionals, retables, etc.). We intervene in the restoration of churches, mansions and castles.

We are also wood sculptors and shoot for small and medium series production of staircase balusters or umbrella handles.
