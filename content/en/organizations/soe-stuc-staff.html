Our expertise is recognized in various fields:

▪ Staff work: cornices, moldings, traditional wall and ceiling coverings

▪ Stucco: decorative stone and marble stucco finishes

▪ Plasterwork: partitions, ceilings, wall coverings, and stair cladding

▪ Gypsum work: plaster ornaments

▪ Decorative elements: lighting fixtures, cladding, and interior design

The company, with nearly 50 employees, operates in France, Europe, and internationally, on contemporary projects as well as heritage restoration projects:

▪ Apartments and private mansions

▪ Shops and showrooms

▪ Offices and public spaces

▪ Hotels

▪ Historical monuments

▪ Condominiums (stucco stairwells and lobbies)
