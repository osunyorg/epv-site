SOCOTEX, SOciété de COnfection TEXtile dédiée à la protection solaire.
For over 50 years, this family-run business in Normandy has built its expertise around canvas roofing solutions for protection from the sun and rain. Combining craftsmanship and new technology, the company custom-makes canvas coverings and manufactures its own ranges of innovative products: awnings, pergolas, parasols, shade sails... Aesthetic, long-lasting fittings to extend your living spaces and bring comfort with Made in France expertise (Labellisée Entreprise du patrimoine vivant en 2020). Socotex's expertise in shade sails and pergolas has been honored at the Palais de l'Elysée during the Grande Exposition du Fabriqué en France in 2021.
