Since 1961, LUM'ART has been designing and handcrafting lighting fixtures for both indoor and outdoor use. The workshop works with noble alloys such as solid brass, zinc, and copper which, beyond their beautiful appearance, ensure the highest durability-especially outdoors. Today, the collections include more than 500 models, each available in a dozen in-house patinas. Each lighting fixture is fully handcrafted by our craftsmen in France and is therefore unique.
