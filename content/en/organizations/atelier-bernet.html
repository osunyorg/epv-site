Catherine Bernet, a tapestry weaver, set up her workshop in her home in the heart of Felletin. She is reviving the Creusian tradition where each family sheltered the loom, which strengthened the region's ties. The workshop produces a type of smooth bass weaving, where the work is worn horizontally by the cotton chain stretched between two looms. With the help of three other weavers, she is able to create large tapestries.
