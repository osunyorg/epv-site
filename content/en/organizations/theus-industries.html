Theus manufactures the all of the Focus collection - about twenty models - including the famous Gyrofocus: an organically shaped chimney made of black steel, suspended and 360 degree swivel.

With an R&D team, Théus innovates continuously to adapt collector models to current standards (wood Ecodesign) and new energies (gas, bioethanol, electricity without ever touching the design that made the brand's success Focus.

In his factory, it's the sum of unique know-how that is implemented to achieve parts of exception.

In the workshops, boilermakers shape steel with gestures as precise as rare.

Labelled « Enterprise of Heritage Living", the Manufacture Focus embodies the excellence of innovation and French craftsmanship.
