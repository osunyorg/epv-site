Founded in 1935, Marigaux is a French manufacturer recognized for the excellence of its wind instruments. Specializing in the manufacture of high-end oboe and English horns, the company perpetuates artisanal know-how combining precision, innovation and acoustic quality. His instruments are appreciated by professional musicians and orchestras around the world.
