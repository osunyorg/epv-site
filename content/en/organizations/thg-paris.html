Founded in 1956, THG Paris are specialists in the design and manufacturing of ornate luxury brassware and accessories.

Spanning 60 countries, with subsidiaries in London, Paris, Moscow, New York, Shanghai, Milan and Dubai, THG Paris can manage projects globally and offer a bespoke service to its clients.

By mastering all processes from conception to production, THG Paris can offer innovative and turn-key solutions. This is complemented by our extensive collaborations with renowned designers including Jean-Michel Wilmotte, Jamie Drake, Gilles & Boissier, Martin Kemp and Charles Zana to name a few.

Based along the Normandie coastline, our factory utilises local resources and our employee's expansive knowledge to manufacture luxury brassware in one place, culminating in recognition from French label EPV.

Long-standing relationships with partner companies such as Lalique, Baccarat, Christofle and Haviland have enabled THG Paris to offer a vast array of brassware solutions with their respective coordinated accessories and finishes throughout some of the extravagant residential and commercial projects in the world.

In recent times, THG Paris has expanded their library by offering complementary products ranging from kitchenware, electronic solutions, heated towel rails and door handles.
