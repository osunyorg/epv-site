Manufacture de Cuir de Réalmont is a French craft company specializing in the manufacture of leather clothing for the luxury industry, founded in 1982.

It develops expertise of excellence in the manufacture of custom made leather parts for prestigious houses, ensuring a high level of quality, precision and finish.

The company combines craft traditions and contemporary requirements of the luxury sector, and has obtained the Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant label in recognition of its expertise and the transmission of its know-how.
