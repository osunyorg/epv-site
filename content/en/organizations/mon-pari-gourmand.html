Since 1957, Mon Pari Gourmand has used its extensive experience to create classic gourmet treats, as well as original and bold recipes.

Mon Pari Gourmand makes high quality, highly prized and recognized caramels, fruit pastes and chocolates.

Thanks to know-how inspired by craft methods, Mon Pari Gourmand products offer delicious taste experiences with classic confections and bold creations.

Chocolates decorated by hand, one by one, caramels with toppings, and fresh chocolates (no thawing): Mon Pari Gourmand makes products with great care and professionalism to ensure you receive only the best.

Since 1957, Mon Pari Gourmand has used its extensive experience to create classic gourmet treats, as well as original and bold recipes.

Mon Pari Gourmand makes high quality, highly prized and recognized caramels, fruit pastes and chocolates.

Thanks to know-how inspired by craft methods, Mon Pari Gourmand products offer delicious taste experiences with classic confections and bold creations.

Chocolates decorated by hand, one by one, caramels with toppings, and fresh chocolates (no thawing): Mon Pari Gourmand makes products with great care and professionalism to ensure you receive only the best.
