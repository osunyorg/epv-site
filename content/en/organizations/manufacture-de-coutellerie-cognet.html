We have been designing and manufacturing the world-famous Douk-Douk folding knife since 1929.

In our workshops in Thiers, we manufacture all our Douk-Douk, Le Thiers, Le Capucin and Le Montpellier knives entirely in-house, using traditional craftsmanship and expertise dating back to 1835.
