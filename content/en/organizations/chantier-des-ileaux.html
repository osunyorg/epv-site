Chantier des Ileaux specializes in the restoration of wooden boats, the construction of modern composite wood units as well as in the maintenance, winterization and preparation of boats for the season. The team masters both traditional naval carpentry techniques and contemporary processes, allowing them to work on classic sailboats, heritage units or custom creations.

The workshop is also equipped with a paint booth for boats up to 16 meters, with suction wall and filtration of harmful vapors. The Chantier des Ileaux has set up part of its workshop for the Composite Wood activity. With an area of 150 m2, this space is fully thermally insulated and heated to optimize the manufacturing of composite wood boats.
