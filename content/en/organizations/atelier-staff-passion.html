We a company specializing in the manufacture and installation of components STAFF, customised, in the decoration of the habitat intended to enhance a staircase, magnify a ceiling ...(Interior or exterior layout, mouldings decorative and acoustic insulation).

We work with clients private as professionals, throughout France, but mainly in the Alpes Maritimes, Var and Monaco The texture of plaster, combined with the unique know-how of our artisans, allows us create, shape and volume decorations of various shapes, traditional as innovative, original as classic... and so, meet every particular request of our customers.

STAFF adapts to everyone styles (contemporaneous, Baroque, classical, etc.)

After having passed his CAP in 1987 at the high school of Gué in Tresmes, Mr. Goldberg learned alongside Mr. Rondeau Gérard, former medallist of the MOF title. In 1997, he founded his business in Cagnes Sur Mer. In December 2017, Mr. Goldberg received the title "Maître Artisan des Crafts of Art", in 2019, that of "One of the best workers of France", in the category "Métiers du plastere, sculpture decorative, Ornemanist staffer option". Strong of his experience, he wishes to continue to share his passion, transmit his know-how and evolve with the digital tools that offer to this beautiful trade.
