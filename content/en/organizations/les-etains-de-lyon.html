We are Master Craftsmen in pewter craftsmanship, 4 times EPV certified, and we make pewter bar tops, counters and kitchens.

The expertise of Les Étains de Lyon's master pewterers is a precious heritage, rooted in a family history rich in metalworking traditions. Founded by enthusiasts who grew up in an environment steeped in the art of metallurgy, our company perpetuates this expertise with dedication and meticulousness. Pewter, that noble and versatile material, reveals all its beauty in the expert hands of our craftsmen.

There is no training dedicated to pewter work, which makes each recruitment at Les Étains de Lyon unique and crucial to the preservation of this rare craft. Each new craftsman undergoes a rigorous six-month in-house training course, ensuring that ancestral techniques are passed on and adapted to contemporary requirements.

In this way, we not only contribute to the preservation of this exceptional know-how, we also ensure that it continues to embellish your interiors with the same elegance and quality for which our company is renowned.
