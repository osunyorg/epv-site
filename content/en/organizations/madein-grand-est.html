MADEiN Grand Est acts as a visibility accelerator and a commercial lever:

1. A Showcase of Prestige and Trust

  • Targeted Referencing: Our members join a platform that guarantees the origin of their products. For the association's businesses, this serves as a mark of transparency that reassures both B2B and B2C buyers.
  • Local Branding: Utilizing the MADEiN Grand Est image allows companies to transform technical expertise into a "short-circuit" selling point, which is highly valued in today's market.

2. Access to a Business Network (Networking)

  • Facilitated Connections: As a partner, we facilitate meetings between our association members and other industrial players or distributors across the region.
  • Synergies: The network fosters collaboration opportunities between local businesses (for example, connecting a knife manufacturer with a local timber producer for their handles).
  • Trade Fairs and Exhibitions: MADEiN Grand Est frequently organizes collective pavilions at major trade shows. As a partner, it provides our members with easier access to these prestigious events at a reduced cost.
