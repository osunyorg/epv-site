Since 1908, Charles Paris has perpetuated the unique French ancestral know-how of bronze lighting.

For more than a century, designers and artisans combine tradition and innovation to constantly enrich the inimitable Charles style with wonderful creations, to the international reputation in the universe of interior decoration.

The models are all made by hand in workshops located at the gates of Paris by skilled chiselers, tourners and passionate assemblers as well as by the best outside technicians (founders, decorators, glassmakers, etc.).

Today, the light sculptures that adorn homes, hotels and even the most elegant yachts around the world are often signed "Charles Paris".

The raison d'être of the house is always to explore the matter, the luminous object and its possible functions in order to extract an emotion and magic that make each Charles creation much more than a lamp: a rare and precious art object.
