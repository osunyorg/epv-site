When metal becomes passion, innovation and heritage. At METAL360, we are reinventing the metal industry by merging artisanal know-how with modern technologies. From design to implementation, we bring your custom projects to life, with an absolute requirement of excellence. Each piece is unique and shaped in our workshops, combining precision, durability and aesthetics. We constantly innovate for efficient and environmentally friendly solutions and share our passion with young people through demonstrations, exchanges and a dedicated training area.

METAL360 remains by your side for your projects of taking sides, drawing, manufacturing and installation. We transform metal into timeless works, while anticipating the challenges of tomorrow and respecting your heritage.

A project ? Let’s talk about it! Together, let’s create something exceptional.
