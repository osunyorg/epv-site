Found by Eugene Weiss in 1882 in Saint-Étienne, French chocolate capital, the Chocolate shop Weiss is one of the oldest chocolate shops in France.

Sound Eugene Weiss is inspired by the tradition of blending scale varieties to develop the art of assembling cocoa beans.

The House developers chocolates, pralines and special manufactureery by mastering the whole of the manufacture, from the selection of beans born from encounters with the cooperation.

Its expertise is based on a requesting selection of materials first: French sugar and milk and by packaging manually manufactured in France, at the service of the sustainability of the savoir-faire ligeriens.

In 2025, the Chocolaterie received the Palme d'Or de l'Artisatat de Tradition, Evaluating the commitment and expertise of Weiss women and men For over 140 years.
