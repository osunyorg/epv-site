Installed at Bourg-en-Bresse Since 1936, the Maison Jeanvoine is today the only owner of the know-how of Bressans, an iconic jewellery art of the Bresse.

In 1998, the family resumed the last historical manufacturing workshop to ensure the transmission of this centuries-old tradition. Each piece is made entirely by hand: the enamel is deposited and baked on a silver base, then decorated with delicate golden paillons and enamel beads before being mounted in jewel.
