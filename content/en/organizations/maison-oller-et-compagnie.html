Founded in 1947, Oller et Compagnie is a renowned player in the manufacturing of cork stoppers for the wine and spirits industry. The company is based in Reims, within the Farman-Pompelle Industrial Zone.

Core Activities

  • Cork Stopper Manufacturing: Production of corks for wines and spirits, meeting the most stringent quality standards
  • Innovation & Research: Development of technical solutions to enhance product preservation and guarantee authenticity
  • Environmental Commitment: Implementation of sustainable practices throughout the entire production process
