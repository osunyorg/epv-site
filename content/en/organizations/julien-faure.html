Ribbon designer since 1864

Our ribbons are woven from dyed yarns in France, at St Just St Rambert near St Etienne. Yarn preparation (milling, dyeing) is carried out by our local partners; design, warping, weaving and finishing are carried out in our workshops.

Between modernity and tradition, Julien Faure makes the most of experience handed down from generation to generation. We use shuttle looms with jacquard mechanics,
