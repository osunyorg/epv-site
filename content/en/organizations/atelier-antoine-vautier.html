Atelier Antoine Vautier is a company located in Nantes, in the heart of the Pays de la Loire. Located in a neighborhood conducive to the promotion of craftsmanship, the workshop enjoys a dynamic environment, focused on heritage and innovation. Specialized in the restoration of antique furniture and objets d'art, the workshop implements a traditional know-how, transmitted and perfected over the years.

The company is structured around a main workshop, equipped to accommodate complex and varied projects. Antoine Vautier, master craftsman, works in collaboration with qualified professionals and specialized partners according to the needs of the projects. The workshop also welcomes trainees and young artisans in the process of transmission and training. Our clientele is composed of individuals, collectors, heritage institutions and decoration professionals.

The workshop works on local, national and international projects, with a constant demand for quality and authenticity. Labeled Living Heritage Company, the Atelier Antoine Vautier embodies the excellence of French know-how and is actively engaged in the preservation of movable heritage.

The workshop is also committed to the transmission of crafts and in a responsible approach, promoting restoration as a sustainable alternative.
