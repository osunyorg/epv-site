We are still working on homemade cooked sugar, and we are probably the last to produce laminated candies, pralines by hand.

We offer a wide range of cooked sugar candies, laminated, pralines, stuffed fruit or honey, wrapped or naked, packed in bulk, in bags, glass jars or metal boxes. With this know-how, we make tailor-made recipes to the tastes and colors desired by our customers.
