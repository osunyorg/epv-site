Courtois Paris is one of the last Hats House in France, with the essence of preserving and transmitting its know-how.
It ownstwo chapelries in Paris and two workshops in FranceOne in Paris in the 20th (workshop and showroom) and one in Honfleur (14600, hat press workshop). We took over three workshops that closed to rehabilitate them. We are very proud of our team of about twenty employees, to be recognized by the prestigious label Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant and above all, to safeguard, on our scale, the precious know-how of the French chapelry.

We manufacture both for our brand, Courtois, and also for other French luxury houses in white label.

Our know-how is multipleWe also work on felt, on moulds made of wood or aluminium, straw, panama, sisal, parasisal, Buntal, but also woolly skin and cut-cousus.

Our clientele is both men and women, for hats to wear every day or for more special occasions. We work in the piece for very special orders, but also in BtB for companies, for orders from 10 pieces. Sort of or finished product! We're at heart.to initiate all possible partnershipsin the middle of the hat to be able to sustain this exceptional know-how.
Do you have a project? Do you want to work with us?Contact us.We will put a hat on all heads and save the French chapelry! Our values to perfect this learned mix between modernity and tradition:excellence, trust and service.
