Our events & solutions :

Exclusive Scene Immersive and avant-garde experiences with Premiere Classe, Matter and Shape, JILL, Interfilière Preview and DRP, which reveal talents, independent creations and emerging trends. Industry Pillars Essential events such as Whos Next, the International Lingerie Fair, Shoppe Object Paris, Interfilière Paris (operated for Eurovet) and Bijorhca (operated for the BOCI), which federated upstream and downstream of major industries, bringing together creators, manufacturers, brands and distributors to shape the future of industries. Tailored Solutions Concrete tools such as RUN, our turnkey hybrid offer for emerging creators, or initiatives such as IMPACT and Neonyt Paris, WSN Academy, PagesMode, Fashop, WSN x Ulule and Ankorstore, which meet the strategic challenges of professionals.
