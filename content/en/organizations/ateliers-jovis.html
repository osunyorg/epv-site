Creative cabinetmaking since 1986 at the service of hospitality and sharing professionals, and individuals wishing to benefit from a professional quality. Historically master billarder, our workshops aim to rely on the expertise of making billiards to design a new offer of themed game tables and traditional luxury gaming tables, always with this desire to contribute to the creation of unique experiences of welcome and sharing.

Our positioning: A certain idea of hospitality and sharing for demanding professionals and individuals.

Our value proposition: An exceptional know-how for unique pieces made with a requirement combining the beautiful work and the ideal use experience.
