Beauvillé is an iconic French House specializing in premium textile printing, located in Ribeauvillé, Alsace. With artisanal expertise passed down for over 180 years, the company designs and manufactures exceptional home linens locally, blending tradition, creativity, and excellence.

Awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (Living Heritage Company) label, Beauvillé embodies French elegance through refined collections, meticulously printed in its own workshops.

Core Activities

  • Artisanal manufacturing of high-quality printed fabrics in Ribeauvillé, Alsace, based on century-old expertise
  • Creation of original patterns for interior design (fabrics, curtains, cushions, home linens), featured in prestigious locations such as castles, museums, and private residences

Values and Commitment

  • Tradition and Heritage : Over 180 years of craftsmanship passed down through generations, combining artisanal tradition with innovation
  • Made in France : Local production, short supply chains, French suppliers, and "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" certification since 2009
  • Passion & Excellence : A team dedicated to the ideal of beauty, impeccable quality, and meticulous attention to detail in every creation
