Panyol has been a French manufacturer specialized in the design and manufacture of refractory terracotta wood ovens since 1840.

The brand perpetuates artisanal know-how around refractory ceramics, developing traditional and contemporary ovens for wood burning. These facilities are used by both individuals and catering and bakery professionals, with a focus on cooking quality, material durability and thermal performance.
