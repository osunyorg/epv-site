Vitrail Saint-Georges is a workshop specialized in the creation and restoration of stained glass windows. His expertise is expressed in:

  • the restoration of stained glass of historical monuments
  • making stained glass windows for interior decoration and architecture
  • design of custom-made contemporary creations
  • collaborations with artists, architects and designers

The workshop combines traditional stained glass techniques with artistic creation to showcase heritage and contemporary projects.
