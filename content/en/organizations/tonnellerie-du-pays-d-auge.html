Tonnellerie du Pays d'Auge is a French company specialized in the manufacture of wooden barrels and drums. It produces wooden containers intended primarily for:

  • breeding and conservation wines and spirits
  • manufacturing traditional oak barrels
  • tailored to the needs of producers

The company perpetuates the know-how of Handcrafted cooperage, based on the work of wood and traditional techniques of manufacture of barrels.
