Implemented in Savoy since 1821, the Dolin House has always remained faithful to its values, and a certain vision of craftsmanship. Manufactured according to the rules of art, its vermouths and liqueurs to plants wear high colors of France worldwide. Savoyard above allThe House Dolin is very attached to its mountain terroir :née in the duchy of Savoie, it now belongs to a dynamic territory, the crossroads between winter sports, tourism and a dense economic and artisanal fabric and Creative. Dolin is one of the 3 oldest companies in Savoy in and the company is expanding its manufacturing plants to meet ever-increasing demand.

200 years of expertiseThe products of the Dolin House, including the most emblematic- Vermouth of Chambéry and genepi- liquor are always made on site by a small team of enthusiasts, following preciously preserved historical recipes. And if the tanks and the production tool are modernizing, the methods remain and the copper alembic still lies at the heart of the workshop.

Dolin worldwideSince Marie Dolin's legendary journey to the Universal Philadelphia Exhibition in 1876, and the medal it brought, the attractiveness of the United States for the Chambéry's vermouth never denied it. But they're not the only ones: Today, Dolin products are sold in more than 60 countries. Synonyms of Excellence to French, they are particularly appreciated in cocktails trend all over the world.
