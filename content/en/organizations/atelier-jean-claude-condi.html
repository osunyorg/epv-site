Based in Mirecourt, the historic cradle of French violin-making, Jean-Claude Condi was the first artisan to craft a Nyckelharpa in France.

Drawing on a unique expertise that blends artisanal tradition with acoustic excellence, he designs every instrument with passion, precision, and meticulous attention to detail.

The workshop upholds values of authenticity, the preservation of musical heritage, and bespoke quality. It offers limited-edition, fully customizable instruments designed to meet the needs of discerning musicians drawn to this rare and poetic instrument.

*The nyckelharpa is a traditional bowed string instrument originating from Sweden.

Core Activities

  • Bespoke Crafting : Custom creation of various models, including the TenorHarpa, OctaveHarpa, and CelloHarpa
  • Accessories : A selection of high-quality bows, strings, rosin, and cases
  • Maintenance and Repair: Professional servicing, acoustic setup, and restoration of instruments

Each instrument is carefully crafted using techniques that balance ancestral tradition, rigorous acoustic standards, and a keen eye for detail.
