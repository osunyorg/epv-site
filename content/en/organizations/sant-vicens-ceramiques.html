Labeled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV), the house has for more than 80 years perpetuated exceptional Catalan ceramic know-how, combining artisanal manufacture, enamelling and creation of unique pieces and small series.

Sant Vicens, a top artistic creation site, has welcomed such great names as Pablo Picasso, Salvador Dalí and Jean Lurçat, and today remains a space for the production, exhibition and transmission of French ceramic heritage.
