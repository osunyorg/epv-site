Région Nouvelle-Aquitaine - Filière Cuir, Luxe, Textile et Métiers d'Art is a regional initiative focused on developing and structuring the leather, luxury, textile, and fine crafts sectors. It supports businesses, artisans, and creators by fostering innovation, training, knowledge transfer, and the promotion of local production. It also helps strengthen the visibility and attractiveness of these excellence-driven industries at both national and international levels.
