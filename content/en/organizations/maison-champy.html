Founded in 1720, Maison Champy is the oldest wine house in Burgundy still in operation. Located in Beaune, it has for more than three centuries perpetuated excellent winemaking know-how, combining respect for Burgundy traditions and a commitment to sustainable viticulture. Renowned for the quality of its wines and its anchor in the regional heritage, Maison Champy embodies the art of living and the excellence of the great wines of Burgundy.
