Antan Ferries and Serres is a French company of Art ironwork specialized in greenhouses and winter gardens in steel and glass, inspired by the 19th century style.

The company designs and restores high-end works such as:

  • Garden greenhouses and old greenhouses
  • winter gardens and glass windows
  • verandas and custom metal structures
  • restoration of architectural heritage in iron and glass

The company perpetuates a traditional know-how of lock and metalwork, with a strong heritage dimension, notably on projects to restore historic greenhouses and create unique pieces.
