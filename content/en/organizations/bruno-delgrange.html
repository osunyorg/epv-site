Bruno Delgrange Society is a French house specialized in the Handcrafted manufacture of high-end riding saddles and tailor-made riding accessories.

Expert in:

  • the design of Custom luxury stools suitable for demanding professional and amateur riders
  • manufacturingtailored equestrian equipment (stools, quarters, arches, specific finishes)
  • artisanal leather work with special attention to the precision, balance and comfort of horse and rider
  • collaboration with high-level riders to optimize performance

The company embodies French excellence in the prestige saddlery, combining craft tradition and technical innovation in the equestrian field.
