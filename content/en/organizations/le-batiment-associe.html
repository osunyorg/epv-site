Le Bâtiment Associé is a French family-owned company founded in 1979, specializing in both construction and renovation. Based in Muizon, near Reims, the firm employs 192 people and completes approximately 400 projects annually across regions including Lille, Metz, Nancy, and Paris.

Core Activities

The company provides a comprehensive range of services, including :

  • Structural Works & Traditional Masonry : New construction, rehabilitation, facades, core drilling, and diamond cutting
  • Timber Construction : Timber frames, cladding, terraces, and vertical extensions
  • Stonemasonry : Restoration of historic monuments, facades, and decorative elements
  • Carpentry : Both traditional and industrial roofing
  • Design-Build : Full project management from initial design to turnkey delivery, ensuring a single point of contact
