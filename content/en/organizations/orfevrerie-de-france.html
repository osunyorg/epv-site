Orfèvrerie de France is the guardian of a historic legacy. The company perpetuates an industrial and artisanal tradition dating back to the 19th century, while seamlessly meeting the contemporary demands of the global market.

Deeply rooted in a region historically renowned for its expertise in cutlery, the company prides itself on a production process primarily carried out in France. Its reputation is built on mastered technical processes and an uncompromising commitment to quality, finish, and durability.  

  The heart of Orfèvrerie de France's craft lies in the creation of exquisite cutlery and silver hollowware. Each piece is the result of a rigorous manufacturing process involving numerous stages-from metalwork and silver-plating to the final polishing.The company designs collections in silver plate, stainless steel, and solid sterling silver, striking a perfect balance between aesthetics, balance, and resilience. This unique expertise allows for the creation of products that are both functional and elegant, designed to elevate the art of dining.

  Main activities :  

  • Manufacture of Cutlery and Silverware Production of forks, knives, spoons, and various accessories designed for both everyday use and professional excellence
  • Fine Tableware (Arts de la Table) Design of complementary serving pieces such as trays, tureens, gravy boats, and various service accessories
  • Distribution for Individuals and Professionals The company serves a diverse clientele, including private collectors as well as the luxury hotel and restaurant sectors, with ranges tailored to every specific need
  • Restoration and Re-silvering Services Orfèvrerie de France also offers expert repair and re-silvering services, extending the lifespan of cherished items and restoring their original brilliance
