MDB Bourges intervenes on public buildings such as from the cathedral to the private residence.

Projects may concern windows, shutters, stairs, doors, parquet floors, woodwork, Structures, ... in restoration or renovation.

All our work concerns public or private heritage and historical monuments.

This work is remarkable by the necessary know-how to implement them, balancing traditional and modern techniques, in order never to question the historical value of the building.

Companions must have perfect control of the and the importance of conservation of its original appearance, both in appearance and in essence. All construction sites require a high level of technical skills, both in the framework and in the development of protocols and implementation. Deposit existing, gold renovation replica to nine of the elements deposited, installation of restored or replicated elements, intervention on unfiled items. In each stage, the quality of implementation must be optimal in order to meet the expectations of Masters of particular demanding works.
