Today, we are still honored to practice this demanding and fascinating profession: the restoration of ancient and historical heritage.

Main activities: masonry, stonework, facade restoration, plastering, timber framing.

Secondary activities: insulation using bio-based materials, woodworking.

From its inception, ARPB Arc'had has implemented best practices on construction sites, in the workshop, and in the office to reduce the company's environmental impact. This environmental approach is being progressively integrated into our operations and is subject to continuous improvement.

Company objectives:

- To prioritize local and bio-based materials.

- To promote the circular economy through the reuse of wood, earth, and stone.

The company has the capacity to work simultaneously on several construction sites throughout the Brittany region, including on the Breton islands, such as Bréhat and Belle-Île-en-Mer. Our headquarters are located in Central Brittany, between Saint-Brieuc, Carhaix and Pontivy, approximately one hour from both the North and South coasts.
