body: >-

Our services

Restoration of graphic arts Drawings, letters, prints, posters, maps, fans, newspapers... These artifacts have a variety of supports and techniques, and the workshop ensures conservation and restoration. Book restoration Manuscripts or printed, broached or bound, books and other materials (paper, leather, fabric, canvas, parchment, etc.) require special observations and appropriate treatments. Binding In paper, canvas, parchment or leather, with corners, nerves, tinted or marbled papers, the workshop makes bindings to protect, but also to enhance. Packaging and luterin Boxes, boxes, storage shirts, case... The workshop manufactures any type of packaging that protects the works and proposes the manufacture of custom-made lutrins for their presentation. Council for Preventive Conservation Physical studies, state records, health records, preservation plans... The workshop advises and accompanies to improve the conservation of collections. Training The Workshop takes part in initial and continuing training for professionals in museums, libraries and archives.
