In the heart of the Massif du Sancy, at the foot of the Auvergne volcanoes, the hot springs will lead you to the discovery of a strange curiosity. Whether you're a family or a group, the petrifying grotto and its volcanic waters welcome you for a subterranean immersion. Éric Papon, Master Petrification Craftsman, will then open the doors to his workshops. The secrets of his rare and ancestral know-how will be revealed during this fun and educational tour. Just 45 minutes from Clermont-Ferrand, discover a unique French savoir-faire: the craft of Petrification. An unforgettable company visit for young and old!
