Atelier Fey is an art sheath shop that has perpetuated since 1910 an exceptional family know-how around leather. Specializing in the creation of decorative coatings, custom sheathing, as well as the design and restoration of furniture and art objects, the workshop combines traditional techniques and innovation to carry out unique projects for interior architecture, hospitality, yachting and prestigious residences.
