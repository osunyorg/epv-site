Founded in 1935, Maison Tréca is the flagship of premium bedding in France. Based in its historical workshops in Reichshoffen and Chambord, it preserves rare artisanal expertise, particularly hand-tufting.

By using the finest natural materials and advanced spring technologies, Tréca transforms the bed into an object of design and absolute comfort. Present in the world's most beautiful palaces, the brand embodies a vision of luxury where each piece is a unique creation, custom-made to provide an exceptional sleeping experience.

Core Activities

  • Luxury Mattress & Base Manufacturing : Artisanal design featuring pocket spring suspensions and rare natural materials
  • Designer Bed & Headboard Creation : Collaborating with designers to offer elegant and customizable bedroom furniture
  • Topper & Accessory Development : Comfort enhancements (duvets, pillows) to perfect the sleeping experience
  • Prestige Hospitality Solutions : Tailor-made bedding solutions for the world's most demanding luxury hotels
