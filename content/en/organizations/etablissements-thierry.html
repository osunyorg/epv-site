LE SAVOIR-FAIRE DES ÉTABLISSEMENTS THIERRY
Efficient production Our organization (methods department, scheduling/launching, design office, material and finished-product quality control, "Collection" groups) enables us to meet our customers' quality, price and lead-time requirements. A wide range of productsWhether sleeved (jackets, coats, coats, trench coats) or loose-fitting (blouses, blouses, evening dresses), not to mention accessories, we are able to respond to requests for a wide range of products and materials (double-sided, recognized expertise in working with leather, silk and organza, etc.). Loyal customers Our know-how, attentiveness and ability to adapt are the reasons behind the trust placed in us by the major French luxury brands with whom we have had the privilege of collaborating for several decades. Looking to the future With the solid confidence of our customers, a modern machine park and a young, passionate team, our companies perpetuate know-how and look confidently and ambitiously to the future.
