Ralph M creates and manufactures in his workshop in France, high-end customizable sofas and armchairs. A success perfectly mastered for more than 30 years. The Ralph M team meets the essential challenge of Made in France: to guarantee the quality of models by combining noble materials, expertise and flexibility.

The craftsmen work with materials chosen to combine refinement and robustness. The structures of the sofas and armchairs are made of beech wood machined in France and the fabrics are selected with finesse from well-known publishers.
