Since 1905, the Moulins de Versailles has maintained the profession of miller with passion and demand. Located a few steps from Versailles Castle, they are part of a rich local tradition, while integrating the latest technologies.

Specializing in the manufacture of high-quality flours, intended for chefs, bakers, pastry makers and restaurateurs, the Moulins favour cereals without storage insecticides or organic from local and sustainable fields.

At the Moulins de Versailles, innovation and environmental commitment join each other. Personal lovers of good flours, discover more than 50 references of flours and preparations directly at the mill shop where our team will advise you best. Possibility of having you delivered to home by ordering online.

Find us also in farms, grocery stores and large local areas. Actors engaged in the territory, the Moulins de Versailles embody a responsible and forward-looking mill, highlighting the importance of short circuits.
