The company was founded in January 1998, after fifteen years of professional training.

Eager to put my expertise and sensitivity at the service of my own clients, I created Ferronnerie d'Art, dedicated to architecture and historical and artistic heritage. I am also committed to supporting young people in their training programmes until they are ready to spread their wings, flourish and enrich their experience through new encounters.

Our website, www.ferronnerie-art-tavernier.com, features a wealth of photographs. It strives to convey our unique style and expertise, which we use to meet the diverse needs of our clients.
