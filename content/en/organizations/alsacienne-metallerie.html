Alsacienne de Métallerie is an artisanal company located in Furdenheim (Bas-Rhin), specialized in artistic ironwork and traditional metalworking. The firm combines time-honored expertise centered on working with noble metals (pure iron, steel, stainless steel, copper, bronze, and brass) while respecting ancestral techniques. The team of approximately 11 experienced metalworkers and locksmiths ensures meticulous craftsmanship for every project.

Core Activities

  • Bespoke Metalwork: Stair railings, guardrails, balustrades, and metal staircases (straight, spiral, mono-stringer, ...)
  • Decorative Ironwork: Forged scrolls, foliage patterns, decorative screens (claustras), lanterns, and gates
  • Heritage Restoration: Restoration of period metal elements for historical buildings, churches, castles, and listed monuments (Strasbourg City Hall, the citadels of Belfort and Besançon, and Haut-Kœnigsbourg Castle)
  • Industrial Services & Subcontracting: Plasma cutting, bending, surface treatment, spray-booth painting, sandblasting, and plate rolling. Every project is managed entirely in-house, from initial design to final finish
  • Designer Metal Staircases: Manufacturing contemporary or industrial stairs combining metal with wood or glass, often created in partnership with master carpenters for high-end results
