HORUS is a French manufacturer specializing in luxury faucets and high-end sanitary ceramics. The brand operates two manufacturing sites in France, both awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label, ensuring full control over the entire value chain-from design to production.Their collections combine expert metalwork, mastery of ceramic processes, and exceptional finishes with close collaborations with designers and architects, serving prestigious residential and hospitality projects in France and worldwide.

Product Range

  • Bathroom and kitchen faucets
  • Sanitary ceramics: washbasins, basins, and bathroom fixtures
  • Design and heritage collections
  • Bathroom accessories
  • Bespoke solutions for contract and hospitality projects
