Cousins associés - upholstery - is a exceptional house established for more than twenty years in the 7th arrondissement of Paris, with workshops located at 141 University Street and a showroom at 9 rue de la Comete.

body: >-


His team of fifteen highly qualified artisans
master the creation and restoration of seats, sofas, wall hangings and
windows, in traditional and contemporary techniques.

Combining heritage and innovation, the workshop repulsed
continuously technical limits to meet the most
in a spirit of tailor-madeness and excellence. Cousins Associés coordinates for each project a
know-how chain involving partners - mostly certified EPV - including cabinetmakers, carpenters, couriers, suppliers and
publishers of prestigious fabrics, all guided by the same values: requirement,
listen and creativity.

At the head of the company, Guillaume Viel, trained
of a renowned master upholsterer and initiated very early into the universe of beautiful
houses and historic monuments, has a deep attachment to the
tradition, to the rigour and taste of perfect work. He soon founded his
own structure, then take over the Sosegh House when Patrick Seghpossian,
Best Worker of France, retires.

carried by the love of the profession, it develops the activity along
exceptional projects for prestigious interiors. Large
international decoration, public and private heritage institutions,
designers as well as private collectors grant him their
trust.

Transmission is also at the heart of its commitments:
training young people, accompanying conversions and encouraging swarming.
Former apprentice of the CFA-École de la Bonne Graine, he now sits at his
Board of Directors, actively contributing to sustainability and
the evolution of the profession.

While many workshops have left Paris for
Cousins Associés makes the strong choice to stay there in order to
stay close to its customers and preserve a living connection to the heritage
and those who make him live.

Combining heritage and innovation, the workshop repulsed continuously technical limits to meet the most in a spirit of tailor-madeness and excellence.


Cousins Associés coordinates for each project a know-how chain involving partners - mostly certified EPV - including cabinetmakers, carpenters, couriers, suppliers and publishers of prestigious fabrics, all guided by the same values: requirement, listen and creativity.

At the head of the company, Guillaume Viel, trained of a renowned master upholsterer and initiated very early into the universe of beautiful houses and historic monuments, has a deep attachment to the tradition, to the rigour and taste of perfect work. He soon founded his own structure, then take over the Sosegh House when Patrick Seghpossian, Best Worker of France, retires. carried by the love of the profession, it develops the activity along exceptional projects for prestigious interiors. Large international decoration, public and private heritage institutions, designers as well as private collectors grant him their trust.

Transmission is also at the heart of its commitments: training young people, accompanying conversions and encouraging swarming. Former apprentice of the CFA-École de la Bonne Graine, he now sits at his Board of Directors, actively contributing to sustainability and the evolution of the profession.
