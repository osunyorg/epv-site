A major figure of the 18th and 19th centuries, she was a supplier of royal families and Emperor Napoleon I.

Recognized for its exceptional know-how, ODIOT creates pieces of silverware and cutlery in solid silver, vermeil and savings, perpetuating a craft of excellence transmitted for more than three centuries.

The methods and techniques remain faithful to those inherited from the 18th century. Each piece is handcrafted by highly specialized artisans - from the foundry to the polisher - and may require more than a hundred hours of work. The inspiration of the workshop is based in particular on a fonds of historical drawings sometimes over two centuries old.

Beyond technical virtuosity, each ODIOT creation is designed as a heritage object intended to pass through time and to transmit itself from generation to generation.
