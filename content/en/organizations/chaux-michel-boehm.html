Chaux Michel Boehm & Cie is an Alsatian company specializing in the manufacturing and marketing of natural lime and related products. Located in Dahlenheim, the company combines traditional expertise with modern processes, allowing it to meet the specific needs of building professionals, heritage restoration specialists, and discerning private clients.

Core Activities

  • Natural Lime Production: Manufacturing of aerial and hydraulic lime for construction, renovation, and restoration projects
  • Distribution of Related Products: Marketing of mortars, plasters, whitewashes, and other materials specifically compatible with natural lime
  • Technical Advice & Support: Providing assistance to professionals and individuals in selecting the right products for their projects, with a focus on heritage restoration sites
  • Training and Education: Organizing workshops and training sessions on the application of natural lime and traditional building techniques
