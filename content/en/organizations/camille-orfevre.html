Camille Gras is a creative goldsmith among the last in Paris, trained at École Boulle and in Italy. He created his workshop in 2012 in Paris. He is a specialist in the restoration and manufacture of silver silver and silver silver goldsmiths, and he also makes custom-made creations for designers and large luxury houses. His workshop perpetuates traditional craft techniques while meeting contemporary requirements.

It is recognized by several distinctions, including the title of Master Artisan and the label Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant. And he just acquired the one from Made in Paris.

His creations include caviar doors, candlesticks, silver cutlery and tea services. Passionate about the transmission of his knowledge, he participates in the European Days of Crafts of Art. Her work is entirely done by hand in her Parisian workshop, guaranteeing 100% French production.

It also offers a customization service, including custom engravings. To learn more, it is possible to visit its sitewww.camilleorfevre.fr (external link)
