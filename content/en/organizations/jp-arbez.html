JP ARBEZ is a family business and independent created in 1973 by Jean-Pierre ARBEZ.
It manufactures lead sealing accessories as well special works for the renovation of the roofs of monuments historical.
In synergy with its foundry trade, JP ARBEZ will resume the company P.R.E.V.O.S.T VITROPLOMB and its stained glass business created in 1929 to continue manufacturing conventional or custom-made lead profiles for Master glassmakers.

The Workshop has facilities and extrusion machines, for some centennials, which are maintained and modernized, according to our quality requirements.
Our expertise in lead processing under various forms allow the restoration of works in accordance with the original techniques. For example, renovations classified as historical monuments preserve their splendor and soul; for more tradition allows the creation of lasting and unique works.

In concrete terms, our various collaborations on prestigious and historic sites (Coupole des Galeries Lafayette, Cité Internationale of the French language, Le Louvre, Les Écuries de Chantilly, Notre Cathedral Dame de Boulogne, château de Vaux le Vicomte ...) allow to promote know-how Indisputable and well known nationally and JP ARBEZ stands at your disposition for your projects in France and abroad.
