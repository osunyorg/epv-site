Atelier de la Boiserie is a house founded in 1963 by the grouping of several pre-existing Parisian and provençal workshops. This merger allowed everyone to bring their own specialties and skills, and to form a successful company, rich in knowledge and capacity. Specialized in the manufacture and restoration of style carpentry, in arrangement.
