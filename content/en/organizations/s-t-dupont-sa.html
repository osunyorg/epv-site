S.T. Dupont SA is a luxury French house founded in 1872, recognized for its know-how in the prestige objects and high-end accessories. 

  • Luxury lighters, considered as iconic objects (with the famous "cling" sound)
  • High-end leather goods (bags, small maroquineries, leather accessories)
  • Prestige pens used as luxury writing instruments
  • Fashion accessories and custom objects

The company embodies a blending know-how traditional French crafts and industrial precisionwith close attention to noble materials (leather, lacquer, precious metals) and finishes. Historically associated with the Paris luxury world, the brand is now distributed internationally and remains positioned on a segment very high-end, at the crossroads of the fashion accessory and the collection object.
