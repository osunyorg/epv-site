Heritage and Territory

Since 1946, Maison D'Ennery is in line with French textile excellence. It is at the heart of our workshops in Saint-Germain-Laprade, Haute-Loire, that we perpetuate the art of quilting and making high precision. Combining tradition and innovation, we support the most In addition, it is important to ensure that the textile sector is able to carry out its textile projects.

Bed, bedroom decoration

We transform the material into comfort. Our know-how is expressed through a wide range of customised products: and bed scarves, refined plaids, base and bed heads Structured. We also shape cushions, duvets and covers of sofas that bring an elegant and warm signature to the interiors of exception.

Architecture and layout

Partner D'Ennery's preference for designers, publishers and architects is to reject the decorative and technical limits of textiles. For CHRs and shops, we design efficient decorative acoustic solutions, benches and backrests in fabric or leather with original stitchings, covers and seats elaborate forms. Commitment Recognized

This requirement of quality and this territorial anchor we deserve to be distinguished by the State label Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV). More than one award, this label demonstrates our commitment to preserve know-how rare and a resolutely French manufacture.
