La Fonderie d'Art Macheret has been working for 40 years to create exceptional bronze and brass pieces for events with the creation of trophies and medals, but also heritage, architecture and decoration. Recognized for its artistic and technical challenges supported by its design office, this family business supports its clients in turnkey solutions with noble and sustainable materials. Established 180 km west of Paris since 1982 in the Sarthe, there are more than twenty bronze workers and chiselers, modelers and moulders, founders, designers who exercise their recognized know-how.

The company is currently carrying out an expansion project that will double its operating area while reducing its carbon impact on its activity. The company has received the EPV label - Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant - awarded by the French government since 2013.

La Fonderie d'Art Macheret has been working for 40 years to create exceptional bronze and brass pieces for events with the creation of trophies and medals, but also heritage, architecture and decoration. Recognized for its artistic and technical challenges supported by its design office, this family business supports its clients in turnkey solutions with noble and sustainable materials. Established 180 km west of Paris since 1982 in the Sarthe, there are more than twenty bronze workers and chiselers, modelers and moulders, founders, designers who exercise their recognized know-how.

The company is currently carrying out an expansion project that will double its operating area while reducing its carbon impact on its activity. The company has received the EPV label - Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant - awarded by the French government since 2013.
