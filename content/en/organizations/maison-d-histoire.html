Restoration of historic monuments in masonry and stonework
MAISONS D'HISTOIRE is qualified in the restoration of historic monuments in masonry and stonemasonry. Our craftsmen are just as skilled in the ancestral techniques of vernacular earthen heritage as they are in the modern techniques of insulating lime-hemp rendering and repairing post-war concrete.
