The Gilles Grangeon cabinetmaking workshop is located in Velay, a region in Haute Loire known for its traditions and human values. After travelling around France, Gilles Grangeon returned to his native land.

For forty years, the workshop has combined passion and expertise to provide the very best to customers seeking quality.
