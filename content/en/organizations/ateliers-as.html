Les Ateliers AS produces prints on Lyonnaise tables for high-end accessories, ready-to-wear clothing, luggage and furnishings.

Equipped with state-of-the-art long tables, the site also houses an independent prototyping, sampling and small series production workshop (PEPS). Ateliers AS processes all types of textile fibres and fabric weights, on widths of up to 160 cm, for both large and small series.
