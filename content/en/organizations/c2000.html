The C2000 workshop works for the fashion industry’s leading clients, for whom it applies its rare expertise: cutting, sewing, gathering, and hand-embroidery of rare fabrics such as lace, silk, linen, cotton and wool, with the involvement in the manufacturing process of craftspeople such as jewellers, enamellers, embroiderers, pleaters, re-linkers and silk painters, to name but a few of C2000’s key suppliers.

C2000’s expertise can be seen within the Lou Kasatché fashion house, founded in 2011 by the CEO of C2000.
