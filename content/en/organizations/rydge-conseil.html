Rydge distinguishes itself through a modern, tech-driven approach to accounting, with a specialized focus on strategic executive advisory.Here are their core areas of expertise:

1. Accounting and Digital Solutions

While accounting is their core business, Rydge places a strong emphasis on digital transformation.

They leverage advanced technological tools to automate data entry, providing business leaders with real-time oversight of their operations.

2. Executive Advisory and Strategic

ConsultingBeyond the numbers, Rydge positions itself as a strategic partner :

  • Business Startup Support: Guidance on legal structures, business plan development, and fundraising.
  • Performance Dashboards: Implementation of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to facilitate informed decision-making.
  • Tax and Social Optimization: Strategies designed to optimize the tax burden for both the executive and the company.

3. Payroll and HR Management

The firm also handles the "human" aspect of their clients' businesses:

  • Payroll Processing: Preparation of payslips and regulatory filings.
  • Employment Contracts: Drafting of legal labor agreements.
  • Labor Law Advisory: Guidance on termination procedures, mutual separation agreements (ruptures conventionnelles), and compliance.

4. Audit and Statutory Auditing

They provide certification of financial statements for larger entities and conduct specialized audit missions (e.g., during mergers and acquisitions).
