Founded in 1924, Labonal is a French brand specializing in the manufacture of high-quality socks, produced in its own factory located in Alsace. The company prides itself on artisanal craftsmanship and a steadfast "Made in France" commitment, ensuring every pair of socks is meticulously designed in the heart of the region.

Core Activities

  • Design & Production: Labonal offers an extensive range of socks for men, women, and children, spanning various styles (solid colors, novelty patterns, sport & nature, safety) and premium materials (cotton, merino wool, alpaca, cashmere, silk, bamboo, Lisle thread, etc.)
  • Diverse Collections: The brand organizes its products into themed collections-such as "Made in Alsace," "Bivouac," and "Retro Games"-to offer unique styles that align with current trends
  • Customization & Gift Sets: Beyond its standard collections, Labonal provides customization options and curated gift sets tailored for various occasions (newborn gifts, "day-of-the-week" sets, etc.)
  • Factory Tours & Commitment to Materials: The company invites the public to discover its Alsace factory to gain a deeper insight into its production process and expertise. It also places a high priority on the rigorous selection of high-quality fibers and raw materials
