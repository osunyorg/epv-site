Luxury clothing and accessories for men Since its inception, ZILLI has remained a discreet yet powerful player in the world of men's luxury fashion. Founded in Lyon in 1965, the House quickly established itself as an icon of authentic and discreet sophistication, building on solid foundations: undeniable quality, creative rigour and unique craftsmanship.

By preserving our heritage, we offer excellence by creating extraordinary garments for extraordinary men. With a single goal: to remain faithful to the culture of quality.
