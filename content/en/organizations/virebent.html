The Virebent factory is once again a French flagship and design.

The collections are based on a mastery joint, unique in France, techniques of porcelain, faience, and stucco, associated with those of enamelling and gilding.

True laboratory of experimentation, Virebent perpetuates its collaborations with renowned designers such as Olivier Gagnère, Christian Tortu, Fabrice Hybert, Matali Crasset, or Tse Tse associated and new collaborations with Diptyque, Cartier, Edmond de Rothschild.

On the wire between conservatory methods and innovation Virebent has been awarded the label since 2007 "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant".

For over 100 years, we have Let us accompany our clients during all phases of their project: creation, design, development, production, search for specific enamels, textures and materials.
