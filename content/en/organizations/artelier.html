Based in Reims, L'Artelier was founded by Geoffrey Martin to bring a modern impulse to traditional cabinetmaking. Serving as a bridge between tradition and futurism, the workshop specializes in bespoke support for leaders in the luxury, gastronomy, and international hospitality sectors.

Thanks to its efficient design office and rigorous artisanal expertise, L'Artelier creates unique pieces-from furniture to tableware accessories-where every detail is designed to enhance the material and the client experience.

Core Activities

  • Bespoke Cabinetmaking : Integrated design and manufacturing of contemporary furniture for prestigious interiors
  • High-End Gastronomy Objects : Design and production of service accessories (baskets, caskets) in solid wood for world-renowned chefs
  • Design & Engineering Office : Creative and technical study of complex projects, from conceptualization to modeling
  • Luxury Interior Fitting : Specific interior design for boutiques, luxury hotels, and high-end private spaces
