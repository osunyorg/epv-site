Recognized in France and internationally, we have a unique and unparalleled expertise in research on materials finishing. The company embodies Craft 4.0 by combining the excellence of manual gesture with the precision of the digital tool.

Our expertise is based on a fine knowledge of moulding, machining, prototyping and finishing techniques, complemented by the use of advanced digital tools.(Machining robot, 3D printing etc.) These technologies push the boundaries of creation while ensuring a high degree of precision in the implementation of exceptional projects. Between technical rigour and creative sensitivity, we develop tailor-made solutions.

Every detail is designed to highlight the beauty of the material and the poetry of the object. The dialogue, between the designer who has a vision and our artisan engineers who know the material and the techniques, allows us to find innovative solutions. We accompany our customers in all stages, from design to logistics, prototyping, production and custom finishing.

By integrating a study office and a laboratory of experimentation, Cogitech ensures constant innovation, making its hybrid approach the key to its high precision and poetry of its exceptional achievements. Thanks to our intimate knowledge of matter and our sophisticated expertise, we can turn technical challenges into creative opportunities.
