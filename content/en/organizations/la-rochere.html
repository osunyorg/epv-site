La Rochère is a glass factory founded in 1475 and recognized as the oldest glass factory still in operation in Europe. Specializing in the manufacture of pressed and blown glass, the house perpetuates a unique craftsmanship combining traditional techniques and contemporary design. It produces a wide range of glass objects - table arts, decorative glassware and architectural collections - intended for individuals and professionals alike. Each piece is designed and shaped in its workshops, in Burgundy-Franche-Comté, respecting historical glass gestures.
