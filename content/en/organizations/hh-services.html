Based in Strasbourg for over 30 years, Carrosserie HH Services is an international benchmark in the restoration and conservation of vintage and prestige vehicles.

Founded by Master of Art Hubert Haberbusch, the company perpetuates exceptional craftsmanship combining traditional skills with historical rigor.

As a certified "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (Living Heritage Company), HH Services does more than just repair automobiles: it breathes new life into exceptional pieces by scrupulously respecting period construction techniques (steel, aluminum, wood).

Core Activities

  • Complete restoration of collector vehicles: Full management of reconstruction projects for prestige automobiles and exceptional coachwork
  • Artistic panel beating and metal shaping: Manual shaping of metals (steel, aluminum) to reproduce missing bodywork components to original specifications
  • Ash wood framing: Design and restoration of traditional wooden structures, specific to vintage vehicles
  • Historical painting and finishing: High-precision paintwork including historical color matching and concours-level varnish application
  • Rolling heritage conservation: Technical stabilization and cryogenic cleaning to preserve the original condition and historical patina of museum pieces
  • Expertise and consultancy: Technical support for the authentication and assessment of the conservation status of vintage bodywork
