It was in 1947 that the Audouin family took their first steps into the world of leather goods, with a bag made from leather scraps from shoe workshops. Over the years, the collection has expanded, asserting a recognized craftsmanship. In the 1990s, Laurent Audouin expanded his business to a new field: luxury automotive accessories. In the 2000s, the company takes on a new challenge: leather sheathing for exceptional projects such as prestige boats, palaces or high-end shops.

Company certified ISO 9001 since 2004, and compliant with the 2015 version since 2017, our company is committed to meeting the most demanding quality standards.
