Shipyard Naval Bernard is an artisanal shipyard located at Saint-Vaast-la-Hougue (Manche, Normandy), at the entrance of the port, in an area well known for pleasure and boat repair.

Main activity

This site specialises in the construction and restoration of boats with recognized traditional know-how, including:

  • classic and traditional sailboats
  • wooden and polyester boats
  • complete restoration of pleasure craft
  • naval repairs (maintenance, overhaul, etc.)
