HomeVolpon, art cabinet in Lyon, was founded byErnest VOLPON1946. His ambition was to design haute couture interior furniture and furnishings. Mr Volpon's ancestral know-how has been passed on from generation to generation. Over time, the Maison VOLPON has been able to adapt to new technologies in order to propose a perfect marriage between tradition and modernity.

In 2000,Frédéric VOLPONbecame the leader of the VOLPON House, succeeding his father. Strong soundunique know-how, the VOLPON workshop was awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label of excellence in 2016.
