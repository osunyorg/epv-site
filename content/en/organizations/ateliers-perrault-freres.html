Les Ateliers Perrault is 265 years of history and heritage through the maintenance, transformation and creation of built heritage in France and around the world. This work is thus recognized by its Living Heritage Company Label. Their strength therefore lies in the excellence of their companions, the mastery of their know-how and the heritage of the past.

Les Ateliers Perrault thus ensure the restoration of heritage buildings, the reproduction of works of art and the creation of traditional creations. These operations therefore vary around the trades of window work, carpentry, carpentry and cabinetmaking. Passionate about their professions in the respect of tradition.
