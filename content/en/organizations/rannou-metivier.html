For five generations, Rannou-Métivier has been renowned for its Montmorillon macarons with a tender almond filling. The company offers a range of gourmet delicacies, including macarons with seasonal flavours, thistles and nougatines, as well as many other treats to share. Selected as one of the best chocolate makers in France by the Club des Croqueurs de Chocolat, Rannou-Métivier has reinvented the art of tasting with a range of fine chocolates, crafted with respect for tradition and expertise passed down through the generations.
