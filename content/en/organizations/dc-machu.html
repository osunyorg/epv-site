Founded in 1964, the company specialises in "visiting" and the lace finish: the scraping, after "visit" (control) of the fallen "trap" cutting, i.e. cutting of the intermediate tulle area to the knife circular.

The company implements all finishing operations. It treats the know-how of preparation and weaving at the company V Machu. It has rare and complex finishing know-how: This is a time-consuming visit, i.e. control of the "trap" fallen from trades and repairs to the needle hooks and defects. Then comes fencing: the junction wires are pulled to leave only the Lace. Lace, can then be twisted with threads more or less precious (rubans, sons of gold or silver, lurex, pearls).

The company has the Solstiss group, brand distribution.
