Based in the Vosges region, the company continues to be an expert in the manufacture of exceptional seats and furniture, working in collaboration with international designers for projects for luxury hotels, restaurants, yachts, shops and private residences. Labelled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV), it brings together all the furniture trades within its workshops in order to guarantee complete control of production and a crafts and industrial quality recognized internationally.
