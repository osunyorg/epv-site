Located in the heart of the Vosges Mountains in Liffol-le-Grand since 1892, Maison Laval (Laval Collection) is a true French institution dedicated to the creation and manufacture of luxury furniture.

Awarded the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label, this manufacturer embodies the excellence and tradition of "Made in France," a legacy passed down from generation to generation.

The company specializes in the creation of bespoke furniture and exquisitely crafted seating. While upholstery and cabinetmaking remain its historical core business, Maison Laval has evolved by integrating new skills into its workshops: working with metal, resin, and stone, and developing exclusive finishes. This complete mastery of the production chain guarantees impeccable artisanal and industrial quality.

Today, Laval Collection collaborates with leading international designers (Philippe Starck, Christian Liaigre, Gilles & Boissier, Rémi Tessier) to bring exceptional pieces to life. Its creations furnish the most prestigious locations worldwide: Parisian palaces (Hôtel de Crillon, Le Meurice) and international hotels (Baccarat Hotel NYC), Michelin-starred restaurants, luxury boutiques, yachts (Satori, Grace E), and private residences. With its headquarters in France and a subsidiary in Shanghai, the brand combines the prestige of its reputation with the quality of its expertise to meet the most ambitious interior design projects.
