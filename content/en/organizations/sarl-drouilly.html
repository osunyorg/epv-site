Based in the Aube region since the late 19th century, Poterie d'Amance - Tuilerie Drouilly preserves a rare artisanal expertise in terracotta. Recognized as a Living Heritage Company (EPV), this family business blends tradition and innovation to produce functional, decorative, and heritage-focused objects.

Artisanal Pottery

Handcrafted terracotta items created using traditional methods :

  • Ollas (Oyas): Eco-friendly irrigation systems for sustainable gardening
  • Decorative Objects: Lamps, candle holders, vases, and more
  • Functional Items: Wine racks, lighting fixtures, pots, and jars
  • Techniques: Modeling, molding, stamping, and sizing

Traditional Tile-makingProduction of terracotta building materials, fired at high temperatures :

  • Materials: Roof tiles, bricks, floor tiles, and brick slips
  • Old-fashioned Craftsmanship: Manufactured without chemical additives
  • Quality: Durable and resistant products, perfectly suited for heritage renovations or high-character new builds

Architectural Ceramics and Restoration

Reproduction and restoration of terracotta elements for architectural heritage :

  • Decorative Facade Elements: Friezes, pediments, cornices, and more
  • Custom Work: Special tiles and bespoke architectural pieces
  • Prestigious Projects: Expertise provided for historical monuments, listed buildings, and exceptional construction sites in France and abroad
