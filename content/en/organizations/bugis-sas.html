Bugis is a French company founded in 1956 near Troyes, specializing in the creation and manufacturing of knitted fabrics for the fashion industry and technical applications. Building on recognized expertise, the company designs and produces high-end, innovative, and eco-responsible fashion knits locally, combining tradition, performance, and sustainable commitment with high-tech materials.

Activities & Services

  • Creation, innovation, and manufacturing of fashion fabrics: a recognized expertise in developing exceptional textiles that combine style and comfort to support the most demanding brands
  • Knitted fabrics made from natural and noble materials: a comprehensive range dedicated to ready-to-wear, including jersey, fancy jersey, piqué, fleece, loop, velvet, milano, jacquard, rib, and padded fabrics
  • High-performance technical knits: the design of high-tech solutions meeting the requirements of advanced industries, specifically for the protection of people and equipment
