Founded in 1878 in Nancy, Daum is one of the most prestigious French crystal works, born from a family tradition started by Jean Daum and carried forward by his sons, Antonin and Auguste.

The House played a major role in the École de Nancy as well as the Art Nouveau and Art Deco movements. Daum holds the "Living Heritage Company" (EPV) label, recognizing its exceptional craftsmanship-unique in Europe in the fields of crystal and glass paste.

Core Activities

  • Artisanal Creation & Manufacturing : Crafting crystal and glass paste objects, including vases, sculptures, lighting, and jewelry
  • Artist Collaborations : Partnering with renowned artists to produce limited-edition works
  • High-End Decorative Art: Production of luxury decorative objects for the global high-end market
  • Preservation of Traditional Expertise : Mastery of ancestral techniques such as lost-wax casting and mass coloring
  • International Distribution : Commercialization through dedicated boutiques, art galleries, and international art fairs
  • CSR Commitment : Focusing on sustainable innovation and the transition toward lead-free glass paste
