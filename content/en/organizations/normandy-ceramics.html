Manufacture of tiles, ceramic objects and architectural ceramics.
For over 60 years, the company has been developing 100% French artisanal production, based on the shaping of the clay and the development and application of the glazes. It offers the flexibility of an artisanal factory (choice of formats, 2D/3D shapes, glazes: satin, gloss, metallic...) with the quality of an industrial tool.
