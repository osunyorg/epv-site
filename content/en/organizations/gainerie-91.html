With almost 60 years The Gainerie 91 Group is the leader in dressing and putting in value of the creations of the most prestigious luxury houses.

He intervenes in various universes such as watchmaking, jewellery, perfumes & cosmetics, Wines & Spirits, Cigars or Scripture, by designing boxes, boxes and custom displays.

Based in Île-de-France, Lieusaint, Gainerie 91 embodies know-how French artisanal, transmitted and perfected for several decades. This level of requirement and mastery has earned him recognition by the EPV label, a distinction awarded to the houses of excellence that cultivate rare and exceptional know-how.

The group relies on a complete mastery of trades such as sheathing, design, marquetry, carpentry, painting etc. combined with a constant search for performance, innovation and aesthetics.

Aware of the environmental challenges, the G91 Group is also developing integrated eco-design solutions, with a reasoned approach to production and a strong commitment to sustainable, recycled or bioourced.

Thanks to this alliance between craft tradition, responsible innovation and French excellence, Gainerie 91 is now a trusted partner for large luxury houses in search of authenticity, durability and distinction.
