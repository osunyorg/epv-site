Le Bois de Lutherie is a company specializing in the manufacture and selection of wood for lutherie, serving professional and amateur luthiers since 1992. It provides carefully selected and prepared wood essences for the production of musical instruments, guaranteeing materials adapted to the acoustic and structural requirements of the music industry. The company supports artisans in their projects, combining technical know-how, rigorous selection of raw materials and passion for musical instruments.
