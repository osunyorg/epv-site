JEWELRY

Gold and platinum are worked by highly skilled jewelers. Most of our collections are forged.

SETTING

Our gem-setter plays a key role in our workshop. Gem-setting is a profession in its own right, rarely integrated into a jewelry workshop...

POLISHING

The polishing and treatment of precious metals, satin-finished or polished, is normally the final stage in the creation of a piece of jewelry. However, some pieces require the intervention of a polisher during the manufacturing process. Our integrated polishing station ensures optimum finishing quality.
