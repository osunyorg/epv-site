Expertise in soil tools

Jourdant offers you a range of tools for soil work. All our manufactures are made in our workshops, entirely by hand from steel tube and sheet metal. Step by step, the material is bitten, bent, folded, welded, painted and packed. A small structure... great flexibility! We can listen, study, design, manufacture all your particular demands and meet your greatest demands.

A successful team in an innovative workshop in the heart of Berry
