A house founded in 1922...For 3 generations, the FOCHESATO marble factory has built with you your project and for each of the main rooms of the house, bringing its know-how and the quality of the materials used for your service.

A privileged partner of the offices of demanding interior architects, we bring our know-how to guarantee the quality of the most daring projects.

In love with beautiful materials, we select our blocks all over the world, then realize in our Lyon workshops your wishes for decoration: furniture, bathroom, paving... for residential, hotels, shops.

The strength of our marble works rests on the alliance of traditional know-how with new technologies. Our passion for stone and our creativity reveal the nobility of marble.
