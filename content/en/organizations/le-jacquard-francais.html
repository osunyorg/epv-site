Le Jacquard Français is an iconic French House, founded in 1888 in Gérardmer, specializing in the design and manufacture of premium home linens. For over 130 years, it has preserved a unique artisanal heritage, blending tradition with innovation.

Core Activities

  • Production of home textiles: tablecloths, napkins, tea towels, aprons, hand towels, and bed linen
  • Artisanal jacquard weaving: combining traditional expertise with textile innovation
  • Design and creation: stylish and colorful collections highlighting high-quality materials (combed cotton, linen)
  • Global distribution: products available in France and internationally through boutiques, authorized retailers, and the online store

The brand focuses on artisanal excellence, product durability, and authentic French style.
