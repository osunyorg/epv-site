Ardelaine is a French worker cooperative (SCOP) specializing in the processing of local wool and the production of eco-friendly textile goods. It promotes traditional and sustainable craftsmanship through the manufacturing of wool mattresses, clothing, and bedding products, while also developing educational and cultural activities around the wool industry.
