Founded in 1955 in Besançon, in the Franche-Comté region of France, Literie Bonnet has, over the years, preserved and carried forward the artisanal tradition of bed making. Its scale and craft-based structure allow the company the independence to carefully select the finest materials and dedicate the necessary time to ensure meticulous craftsmanship.

From the outset, the excellence of its collections has resulted from a blend of cutting-edge technologies and traditional artisanal techniques.

As a result, the assembly and finishing of each piece are always carried out in France, in accordance with the standards of high-end bedding manufacturing.

Each mattress, bed base, headboard, and furnishing element is made to order, according to the requirements of both private and professional clients. Mattress makers produce each item from start to finish, cabinetmakers craft solid wood bed bases, which are then upholstered by skilled craftsmen. All of this is driven by a single goal: delivering an unforgettable sleeping experience with Literie Bonnet.

Made-to-measure solutions, high-quality materials, precision finishing, and attention to detail define the brand's identity. Today, this expertise allows the company to furnish both private residences and luxury hotels in France and internationally.
