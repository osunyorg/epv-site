NV Equipment is a French company founded in 1977 and based in Saint-Gilles-Croix-de-Vie.

It is specialized in the design and manufacture of protective and comfort equipment for boats (biminis, hoods, tauds, cockpit protections, etc.).

Recognized for its technical know-how and tailor-made approach, the company works with leading sailboat, motorboat and multihull manufacturers to offer solutions adapted to each model.

Labelled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV), NV Equipment combines textile crafts, innovation and 100% French production to improve comfort and protection on board.
