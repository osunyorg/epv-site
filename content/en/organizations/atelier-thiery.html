Founded in 1971 by Patrick Thiery, Master Artisan, the Thiery Workshop embodies the excellence of leaf gilding and decorative painting.

Today led by Sébastien, Clément and Céline Thiery, this family-owned company labeled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant", collaborates with architects, designers, decorators and artists, both in France and internationally.

The Atelier Thiery works on classical and contemporary decor projects in private residences, luxury hotels and shops, restaurants, yachts, always providing tailor-made services.

Experienced and always sensitive to the effects of the material combined with the gestures and techniques of its know-how, Atelier Thiery continues to innovate to inspire creatives and professionals who seek it, whatever their universes.
