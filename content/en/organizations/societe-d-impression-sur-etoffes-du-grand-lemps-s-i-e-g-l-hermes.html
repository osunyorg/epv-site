As a versatile HTH printer, SIEGL is able to meet all technical requirements for all types of textile fiber, from warp and weft to knitwear. Its modern, high-performance printing tools, at the cutting edge of technology, enable it to print customized fabrics from 20 g/m2 to 800 g/m2, for widths up to 160 cm, in both large and small runs.
