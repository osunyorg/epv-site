Grav'Or transforms glass according to demand, cutting, sandblasting, engraving, lacquering, gilding, painting. Specialized in various fields in order to offer the best possible quality, the equipment allows us to combine industrial production and design with quality craftsmanship. Whether it is a unique creation or tackling a large-scale project.

The company notably holds the Ateliers d'Art de France label and the art profession certification. In January 2020, Grav’Or was selected for the "Made in France" event at the Palais de l'Élysée to present its expertise in Églocolor finishing, and is also the 2021 laureates of the trophies "Pays de la Loire excellence artisanale mention métier d'art", as well as the prize "Sarthoise de Talent 2018" for Isabelle Ratiskol, executive of the company, in the "Audace" category.
