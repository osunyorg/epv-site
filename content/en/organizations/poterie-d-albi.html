Since 1891, The Poteries of Albi perpetuate family know-how transmitted since 7 generations. Deeply attached to the handmade in France, the house creates collections inspired by the art of living and French traditions.

Achieved by our masters potters and empirants according to ancestral techniques, our parts reflect requirement and quality.

Turn the earth, the enamelling, the cooking... Each step requires precision and precision. expertise acquired only with time and passion. Our potters, real artisans of the gesture, shape each piece by hand.

Labelled Heritage Company Living (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) since 2013, the house embodies a heritage live craftsman.
