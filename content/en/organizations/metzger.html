Metzger is a company specializing in the design and custom manufacturing of aluminum joinery and extensions since 1970, with local production carried out in its workshops in Metz, Lorraine. The company's excellence also relies on a unique installation expertise that has been passed down from craftsman to craftsman since its founding, serving private clients in Lorraine and Luxembourg, particularly in historic or listed buildings. Metzger works exclusively with bespoke solutions, with an eco-responsible approach (using recyclable and certified materials) and draws on its unique know-how to guarantee the quality and durability of its products.      

 Main Activities

  • Home extensions and conservatories: Metzger offers a range of flat-roof extensions (such as the Transition and Villa Véranda models) and aluminum conservatories (Tradition, Evolution), as well as pool houses. All products are designed to deliver high thermal insulation performance.
  • Pergolas and winter gardens: the company manufactures traditional pergolas (Mythique), flat-roof pergolas (Graphique), modular pergolas (Dynamique), and bioclimatic pergolas with adjustable louvers, as well as winter gardens (Botanique).
  • Carports and garages: design and manufacturing of aluminum carports for vehicle protection, as well as garage doors (sectional, side-sliding, roller, and swing doors) combining security and thermal performance.

External joinery:

  • Windows and shutters: tilt-and-turn and sliding aluminum and PVC windows, aluminum roller shutters and hinged shutters.
  • Entrance doors and entry vestibules: aluminum front doors (Decodora, Classica) and the custom design of entrance vestibules and canopies.
  • Outdoor fittings: manufacturing of aluminum gates, fencing, privacy screens, and guardrails.
  • Indoor fittings: creation of artist-style or contemporary workshop-style glass partitions, aluminum interior doors, and interior blinds.
