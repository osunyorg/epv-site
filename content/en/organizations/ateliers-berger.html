Three words represent the very essence of the studio's creations :

Movement, Light, Emotion

For over 20 years, Ateliers Berger has been imagining and creating exceptional hand-made decors for the biggest names in architecture and luxury goods.

Our know-how is based on a team of talented painters, experts in textures and colours and trained in the workshop's distinctive gestural techniques.

Our reputation is built on our creative and innovative practices:

Architectural Paper Marquetry® and Agile Decors® on flexible supports, both developed in our workshops.
