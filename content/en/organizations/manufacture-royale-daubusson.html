Created in 1751, the ROYAL MANUFACTURE OF AUBUSSON was first known for its soap carpets. Today, she specializes in weaving carpets, stairways and custom-made top-of-the-range carpets. It is the last French manufacturer to use the Wilton technique. This singularity distinguishes it in the national industrial heritage landscape.
