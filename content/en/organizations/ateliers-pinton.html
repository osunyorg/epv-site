Pinton has always had a keen sense of the times. To capture the spirit of the age is also to stay one step ahead... Applying its expertise to yachting and aviation, or to the carpet market as early as the 1940s, collaborating with contemporary artists on highly modern designs, introducing unusual materials into its weaves – particularly for its Haute Couture collection – and employing new techniques such as gun tufting, Pinton has never ceased to innovate.
