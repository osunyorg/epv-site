In his workshops
Tully, Picardie, each piece is hand-shaped with precision
a combination of traditional know-how and technical innovation. This
craft mastery allows to give birth to unique creations, where
the elegance of the forms accords to a contemporary functionality.

Through her
iconic collections, Bistrot, Carpe & Lyre, Piet, Nemo, Heritage,
Grand Hotel or Jonas, the House affirms a strong identity, enriched by
art creations made on order, such as Marie-Antoinette,
Versailles, Birds of Paradise or Kerylos.

Designed for
As a demanding international clientele, these works equip prestigious venues:
Private residences, palaces, museums, yachts and jets. The artisans of the
Manufacture, sculptors, chiselers, polishers, work with demand
noble materials such as brass, rock crystal or porcelain of
Limoges.

Labelled
Living Heritage Company since 2006, the Volevatch House illustrates the
vitality of French expertise of excellence. Under the direction of Igor and
Nathalie Volevatch, she pursues an approach combining transmission, creation
and durability, serving an authentic and timeless luxury.

Creator and The House, which is the publisher of exceptional taps, draws its inspiration from the rich heritage of the Decorative Arts, perpetuating heritage and demand transmitted by its founder, Serge Volevatch.

In his workshops Tully, Picardie, each piece is hand-shaped with precision a combination of traditional know-how and technical innovation.This craft mastery allows to give birth to unique creations, where the elegance of the forms accords to a contemporary functionality.

Through her iconic collections, Bistrot, Carpe & Lyre, Piet, Nemo, Heritage, Grand Hotel or Jonas, the House affirms a strong identity, enriched by art creations made on order, such as Marie-Antoinette, Versailles, Birds of Paradise or Kerylos.

Designed for As a demanding international clientele, these works equip prestigious venues: Private residences, palaces, museums, yachts and jets.

The artisans of the Manufacture, sculptors, chiselers, polishers, work with demand noble materials such as brass, rock crystal or porcelain of Limoges.

Labelled Living Heritage Company since 2006, the Volevatch House illustrates the vitality of French expertise of excellence. Under the direction of Igor and Nathalie Volevatch, she pursues an approach combining transmission, creation and durability, serving an authentic and timeless luxury.
