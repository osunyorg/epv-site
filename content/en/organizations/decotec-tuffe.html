DECOTEC is the only French manufacturer of bathroom furniture and accessories whose excellence in know-how and research in R&D are recognized EPV. Its 5 workshops - carpentry, painting, composite, metallurgy, assembly-assembling, in the Sarthe allow it a careful control of all its production line in an approach respectful of both human and natural environment. DECOTEC works in eco-design of materials from the circular economy, bio-based materials and PEFC wood from eco-managed forests.

Combining refinement and ethics, DECOTEC offers inspiring, practical solutions that are both contemporary and timeless with their original and elegant design. Every detail is thought to offer a unique user experience. DECOTEC manufactures to order, both custom-made and for collective contract projects, with dedicated collections.
