Bodet Campanaire is a family business with 100% French know-how, whose core activity is to maintain and renovate buildings for the preservation of heritage.

Restoration of bells, dials of buildings, automation of bell ringing, woodworking, lightning and lightning protection, access security, maintenance and maintenance are part of the varied skills related to its field of activity.

Today, Bodet Campanaire is present throughout the French territory and also has agencies in the overseas departments and regions.
