These houses delegate the production of their women’s ready-to-wear projects to the company. This unique craftsmanship is divided into four divisions, the Tailor Division, the Leather Goods Division, the Fuzzy Division and the Design & Collections Division.

In addition, the workshops are labeled E.P.V (Living Heritage Company), recognition awarded by the Ministry of Economy and Finance as a company with knowledge of excellence.
