Leblon-Delienne is a French workshop of pop sculptures recognized for its expertise in creating figurines and art objects inspired by the world of comics and popular culture. Combining creativity, craftsmanship and finishing requirements, the house collaborates with iconic artists and licenses to bring unique and limited-edition pieces to life. Through his creations, Leblon-Delienne embodies the alliance between art crafts and contemporary culture, contributing to the international reach of French design and manufacturing.
