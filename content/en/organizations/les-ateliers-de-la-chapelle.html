The Workshops group 5 complementary activities: The Manufacturing Workshop, specialized in the design and realization of woodwork, exceptional interior and exterior carpentry and wood floors, The Restoration Workshop, specialized in restoration-conservation of large historical decorations, parquet floors, church and civil furniture, The Woodworking shop, specialized in the creation of contemporary furniture, Sculpture Workshops, specialized in restoration and copying ornaments, from the 15th century to Art Deco, The design and research office, specialized in carrying out preliminary studies for the restoration of joinery complexes, particularly on woodwork decorations, religious or civil furniture.

Les Ateliers de la Chapelle employs around fifty highly qualified professionals who form a multidisciplinary team combining the expertise of carpenters, cabinetmakers, restorers and passionate sculptors, the expertise of art historians and heritage specialists, the creativity of designers and draftsmen.
