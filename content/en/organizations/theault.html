Bodywork specialized in equine transport
For nearly 100 years, THEAULT has been working passionately to enhance every detail of its horse trucks. To offer innovative transport solutions with sleek design tailored to the needs of its users and the horses being transported, THEAULT combines cutting-edge technologies with artisanal craftsmanship, new materials, and high-quality materials. These alliances have made THEAULT the benchmark for equine transport worldwide. Leveraging its renowned French expertise recognized by the EPV excellence label, the brand has developed a range of light and heavy vehicles, available as carriers or with living quarters.
