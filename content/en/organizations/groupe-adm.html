The ADM Group can rely on the skills of its companions to create unique or custom-made pieces. They are qualified to respond to the most creative ideas, whether modern, technical or specific. The ADM Group has a workforce of 76 employees, workshops with a fleet of latest-generation machines (2 CNC machining centres in 3 & 5 axes) and a design office equipped with the latest 2D and 3D tools.

The ADM Group has been PEFC certified since 2015 and uses materials from sustainably managed forests and controlled sources. Through this commitment, it promotes a local partnership with FSC and/or PEFC certified suppliers to promote the legal origin of our wood-based raw materials.
