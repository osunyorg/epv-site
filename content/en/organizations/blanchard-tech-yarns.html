Blanchard Tech & Yarns is a company specialized in the design and manufacture of technical yarns and innovative textile solutions. It develops materials for various uses, including industrial and decorative, combining traditional textile know-how with modern technologies. The company is distinguished by its ability to offer technical wires adapted to demanding performance and quality requirements.
