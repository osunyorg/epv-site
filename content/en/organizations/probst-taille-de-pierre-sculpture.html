An exceptional workshop specializing in stone cutting and sculpture in the heart of Alsace, Atelier Probst cultivates ancestral expertise dedicated to the preservation of architectural heritage.

Driven by high standards and a passion for the craft, the company performs highly technical interventions-restoring old houses, religious buildings, and historical monuments-for public institutions, architects, and private clients.Within the workshop, traditional techniques are perpetuated with absolute rigor: each stone is shaped exclusively by hand, without the use of pneumatic hammers, to respect the integrity of noble materials such as Vosges sandstone.

This commitment to excellence is embodied by Thomas Probst, a former "Compagnon du Devoir" and gold medalist "Master Stone Cutter" at the European Stone Festival, making the company an essential reference in French fine craftsmanship.

Core Activities

  • Restoration of Historic Buildings: Interventions on traditional Alsatian houses, facades, window frames, and stone facing
  • Conservation of Historical Monuments: Restoration of religious edifices, cathedrals, vaults, and complex architectural elements
  • Sculpture and Ornamentation: Creation and restoration of statues, mascarons, capitals, and custom engravings
  • Stone Fittings: Design of bespoke fireplaces, staircases, fountain bases, and flooring
  • Funerary Art: Rehabilitation of memorial sites, headstones, and funeral monuments

The company's DNA is rooted in the transmission of skills and respect for architectural history. Recognized for its transparency and quality of execution by the "Meilleures Entreprises de France" (Best Companies of France) network, Probst is committed daily to breathing new life into the stones that constitute the cultural wealth of our territory.
