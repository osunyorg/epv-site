An exceptional workshop specializing in stone cutting and sculpture in the heart of Alsace, Probst cultivates ancestral craftsmanship dedicated to preserving built heritage.
Driven by high standards and a passion for the trade, the company performs high-technicality interventions-restoring old houses, religious buildings, and historical monuments-for public institutions, architects, and private clients.Within the workshop, traditional techniques are perpetuated with absolute rigor: every stone is shaped exclusively by hand, without the use of pneumatic hammers, to respect the integrity of noble materials such as Vosges sandstone.

This commitment to excellence, embodied by Thomas Probst-a former Compagnon du Devoir and gold medalist "Master Stone Cutter" at the European Stone Festival-makes the company an essential reference in French fine craftsmanship.

Core Activities:

  • Restoration of Historic Buildings: Interventions on traditional Alsatian houses, facades, window frames, and stone cladding.
  • Conservation of Historical Monuments: Restoration of religious buildings, cathedrals, vaults, and complex architectural elements.
  • Sculpture and Ornamentation: Creation and restoration of statues, mascarons, capitals, and personalized engravings.
  • Stone Fittings and Features: Design of fireplaces, staircases, fountain bases, and stone flooring.
  • Funerary Art: Rehabilitation of memorial sites, steles, and funerary monuments.

The company's DNA is rooted in the transmission of traditional gestures and respect for the history of architectural heritage. Recognized for its transparency and quality of execution by the "Best Companies of France" network, the brand of Probst is committed daily to breathing new life into the stones that form the cultural wealth of our territory.
