Labelled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) since 2016, the company Staff Espaces Volumes has specialized since its creation in 1988 in the restoration and manufacture of decorative elements in staff; cornices, mouldings, low reliefs, architectural ornaments, ceilings, fireplaces, stairs, furniture, fireplaces and laying on construction sites (apartments, houses, hotels, offices, shops, restaurants, museums, private hotels, historic monuments...).
Our mastery of staff techniques allows us to intervene on prestigious, classified sites, to restore their lustre to architectural ornaments and elements of style.

Our work has been part of a wide palette since the study, drawing, execution plans, the manufacture of earth, resin elements, the taking of elastomer prints, the manufacture of models, prototyping, the creation of moulds, the manufacture of decorative architectural elements in staff and the laying in France and abroad.

Our clients are architects, designers, decorators, individuals, private and public institutions and building professionals.

Under the direction of Bruno PORTIER since 2014, the company has been developing and opening up to contemporary creations with renowned architects and designers.
