Since 1884, the Piganiol House creates umbrellas in Aurillac that tell a story family history, that of a know-how transmitted from generation to generation.

From Cantals heart, between textile tradition and attachment to the territory, the family Piganiol perpetuates a rare artisanal gesture: that of manual manufacture of umbrellas. Each piece from the manufacture is the result of a requirement, a passion, a history.

Next of time, the House has been able to remain faithful to its values: quality, sustainability, respect for an art of making French. She was able to evolve by creating collections that combine elegance and functionality, while preserving the essential: the sense of detail and the beautiful gesture.

House Piganiol has been one of the last family for more than 140 years. umbrella factories in France. A living house that honors its heritage while looking at the future with faith and creativity.
