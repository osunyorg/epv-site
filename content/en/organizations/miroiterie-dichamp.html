Heir to a craftsmanship born in 1893, Dichamp Techniverrier is a landmark glasswork institution based in Troyes. Renowned for its unique expertise in glass bending (curved glass), the company tackles the most complex challenges in modern architecture and decoration. Whether restoring elements of French heritage or designing contemporary glass structures that combine purity and safety, the company relies on rare technical mastery to transform material into unique pieces, blending aesthetics with performance.

Core Activities

  • Curved Glass Manufacturing: Production of bent glazing for architecture, furniture, and industry
  • Precision Glasswork & Processing: Cutting, beveling, and polishing of all types of glass products
  • Prestige Restoration: Working on historical and artistic heritage renovation projects
  • Bespoke Interior Fitting: Design and installation of joinery (aluminum, steel), railings, and custom glass solutions for residential and commercial spaces
