Based in Savoie, she designs and produces exhibitions, decors, models, interactive installations and stage design for museums, cultural sites, exhibition spaces or event stands.

She works from the design phase (design 3D, technical studies) to manufacturing and installation, integrating different materials (wood, metal, plastics, composites) as well as multimedia and interactive elements. The company operates in France and internationally and is recognized for its technical know-how, craft precision and tailor-made achievements in the cultural and scenographic field.
