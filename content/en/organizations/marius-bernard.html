Specializing in the manufacture of cooked dishes and fine grocery products, the company carries out the profession of cook and preserver by preparing traditional recipes inspired by Provencal and Mediterranean cuisine.

It is distinguished by craftsmanship, recipes without additives or preservatives, responsible production and a strong commitment to the fight against food waste and solidarity actions.
