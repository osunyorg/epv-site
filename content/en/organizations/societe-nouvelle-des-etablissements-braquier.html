Dragées Braquier is an artisanal House based in Verdun, founded in 1783, specializing in the traditional production of dragées (sugar-coated sweets) and confectionery. Recognized for its expertise passed down through generations, the company offers a wide range of iconic products, including prestigious Avola almond dragées and chocolate dragées.

The company also invites the public to discover its manufacturing process through guided tours of its factory.

Core Activities

  • Artisanal manufacturing of dragées (almonds, chocolate, giant dragées, etc.) and fine confectionery
  • Commercialization of these products, particularly for life events such as weddings, baptisms, and other celebrations
  • Industrial tourism: Welcoming visitors for guided or audio-guided factory tours to showcase their traditional craftsmanship and heritage
