Lambert Manufil offers a comprehensive range of steel wires, available in various forms and adapted to the diverse needs of the agricultural, industrial, and construction sectors:

• Plain, galvanized, annealed, smooth, twisted, or ribbed wire,

• Coils, cut bars, and custom-formed parts,

• Staples and horticultural hoops,

• Nails.

This diversity allows Lambert Manufil to meet demanding technical requirements and offer solutions tailored to each application.

Without compromising on quality or precision, the company uses 100% recycled, European, and low-carbon steel.

The sales, production, and logistics teams work in close coordination to build lasting and trusting relationships with each customer. This organization guarantees a high level of service, where responsiveness and customization are the priority.

In Couëron (44), on the banks of the Loire River, Lambert Manufil is certified by EcoVadis, Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company), La French Fab, and Made in France.
