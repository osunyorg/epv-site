Lambert Manufil Industries is a family industrial company founded in 1924 and established in Couëron, near Nantes.

Specializing in metallurgy, it designs and manufactures steel wires and wired products (bobins, bars, folded parts, tips, nails, etc.) for use in many sectors, including construction, agriculture and industry.

With more than 100 years of expertise, the company is distinguished by its expertise in drawing and shaping steel wire, its French production, its tailor-made service and its commitment to sustainable development, with more than 90% of its steel supply from recycling.
