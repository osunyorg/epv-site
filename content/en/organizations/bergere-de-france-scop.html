A Historical and Creative House

Founded in 1946, Bergère de France has established itself as a leading name in the world of wool and knitting. A true French institution, the House has successfully navigated the decades by blending artisanal tradition with contemporary creativity.

A Unique Industrial Model in Bar-le-Duc

The company is built on an integrated spinning mill model, located at its historic site in Bar-le-Duc. Within this 40,000 m² production facility, Bergère de France manages the entire product lifecycle, from the selection of raw materials to commercialization.This exceptional industrial expertise is showcased through every stage of manufacturing:

  • Dyeing and cracking
  • Preparation and spinning
  • Winding and assembly
  • Twisting, steaming, and balling

The Alliance of Man and Machine

At the heart of the workshops, cutting-edge technology meets craftsmanship. Operators regularly intervene manually on precision mechanical machines, ensuring impeccable yarn quality.This human expertise is the foundation for creating the famous yarn swatch catalog. A distinctive hallmark of the company, this globally unique resource is produced using custom-made equipment, requiring a level of dexterity that only the human hand can provide.

Core Activities

  • Manufacturing high-quality knitting yarns-including wool, cashmere, alpaca, mohair, merino, and bamboo-entirely produced in France at its spinning mill in Lorraine.
  • Designing and distributing patterns for knitting, crochet, and embroidery for women, men, children, and babies, including clothing, accessories, DIY items, kits, and haberdashery.
  • Providing a yarn swatch catalog alongside an extensive network of retailers and boutiques, as well as an online store offering international shipping.
  • Comprehensive customer support from purchase to project completion: restocking options, creative hobby advice, technical assistance for patterns, and access to archives of vintage designs.
