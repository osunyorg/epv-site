Since 1908, Cruard Menuiserie creates unique joinery, in the rules of art with current and old techniques. The company directs its activity towards larger-scale and high-end projects in Pays de Loire, Paris and the Parisian region: luxury hotel located in the Arcachon basin, private villas in London or Paris or even an organ gallery for the church of Etampes.

For 20 years, Cruard Menuiserie has held the highest Qualibat qualification in restoration of carpentry of Historical Monuments. It also holds the RGE designation as a wood joinery installer manufacturer and the prestigious Living Heritage Company label.
