Founded in 2008 by Patrick Pugin, a precision mechanics enthusiast, the company has built an international reputation.

It holds the prestigious EPV label (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant), a mark of recognition from the French State that distinguishes companies with excellence in artisanal craftsmanship.

Core Activities


  • Full or Partial Restoration: The workshop carries out competitions restorations on iconic models (356, all 911 generations, 914, etc.). Each project follows a rigorous process: stripping, fine metalwork, anti-corrosion treatment, and reassembly in strict compliance with original standards
  • Mechanics and Maintenance: Specialized maintenance for both air-cooled and water-cooled engines, gearbox overhauls, chassis tuning, and performance optimization
  • Bodywork and Painting: Mastery of hand-forming techniques and application of high-quality finishes. The company is particularly renowned for its specific expertise in restoring Fuchs wheels
  • Artisanal Upholstery: Complete interior refurbishment (seats, dashboards, headliners) using period-correct materials (leathers, fabrics, carpets)
  • Pre-owned Porsche Sales: Art Restoration offers a selection of rigorously chosen, serviced, or restored vehicles, providing collectors with a guarantee of quality and transparency
