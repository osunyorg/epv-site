Textile du Maine, a living heritage company, is a human-sized company. Located in the heart of Anjou, our workshop of 55 highly qualified enthusiasts, is specialized in women’s ready-to-wear at the service of major fashion houses. The clothes that leave the workshop are the expression of a heritage of precious know-how.

The application of this knowledge relies on the talents of our teams. Our hand encompasses blur, large blur, cut-sewn jersey, lingerie/corsetry, swimwear, leather as well as great technical mastery of the material mix. We provide development, standardization and production.

The diversity of our hand serves the variety of our clients' collections but requires multiple skills to preserve and maximum flexibility.
