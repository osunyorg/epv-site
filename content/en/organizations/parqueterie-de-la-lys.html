Since 1875, La Parquèterie de la Lys has been putting its expertise into practice while adapting to the evolution of techniques and materials. Our teams, with many years of experience, pass on their skills from generation to generation and master all the installation techniques for solid, engineered, or laminate flooring: floating floors, glued floors, on screed, on joists, on insulation, indoors and outdoors.

Our workshop also allows us to create custom pieces or bespoke flooring, and to renovate and repair old floor coverings, or parts of parquet that have been damaged. Our team members, who are passionate about their craft, all come from the woodworking field: colorists, cabinetmakers, carpenters, fitters...

Their attention to detail guarantees you perfect finishes and a clean, on-time construction site. Their technical expertise allows them to find solutions to even the most specific challenges, minimizing costs while respecting the aesthetics of the premises.

Our facilities, very easy to access, cover more than 2,000 square meters and include:
• a 300-square-meter showroom,
• offices of nearly 200 square meters,
• an 800-square-meter warehouse for storing equipment and the vehicle fleet,
• an 800-square-meter heated workshop for storing materials and carrying out carpentry work,
• a storage area for sensitive consumables, with controlled temperature. 


All our products are fully traceable, and our suppliers comply with all European standards and ecological labels required to ensure forest sustainability and sustainable development (FSC, PEFC, CE, NF, etc.).
