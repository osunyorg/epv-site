Fontes & Traditions is a French art foundry specializing in the design, manufacturing, and restoration of cast iron elements for architectural heritage. Founded in 2003 and based in Chaumesnil, the company promotes traditional craftsmanship by offering a wide range of products, such as railings, staircases, grates, gates, entrance doors, fountains, and statues.Recognized as an "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (Living Heritage Company), Fontes & Traditions-part of the VHM Groupe, is committed to combining quality, durability, and respect for French artisanal techniques.  

Activities and Expertise

Fontes & Tradition specializes in the design, manufacturing, restoration, and customization of cast iron components for architectural heritage. The company offers a wide range of products, including:

  • Railings and Balustrades
  • Staircases
  • Entrance doors
  • Grates and Gates
  • Fountains and Statues

The company utilizes various types of cast iron, such as lamellar graphite iron (grey iron), spheroidal graphite iron (ductile iron), chrome-alloyed lamellar graphite iron, and Ni-Hard iron, to guarantee the premium quality and durability of its creations.
