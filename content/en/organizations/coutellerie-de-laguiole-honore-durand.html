Master craftsman knife maker from Laguiole to Laguiole which has been in existence for 35 years. The company manufactures different models: folding knives, tableware, custom and collectible knives, Damascus steel manufacturing. It is an artisanal production made up of 2 trades: the Blacksmith profession (making blades, springs, etc.) as well as the Cutler profession (adjusting, assembling, shaping, etc.). from raw parts to the finished Laguiole)
