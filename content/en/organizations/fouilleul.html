We have thus moved from traditional tannery to leather sealing joints to support major drinking water installation projects. Leather working is deeply part of our DNA. Then we learned to work on all the supports of joints: rubbers, fibers, plastics, felts and foams thus developing an innovative and agile company culture.

Increasing our technological expertise and productivity and thus opening our expertise to various sectors and new applications. In a world increasingly exposed to the consequences of global warming, companies are transforming to absorb this transformation shock. The preservation of essential resources such as water reinforces the requirement for effective waterproofing solutions for safety and well-being in workplaces.

We regularly invest in new powerful tools.
