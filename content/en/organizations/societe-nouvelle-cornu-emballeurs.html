New Cornu Packers Company is a French company specialized in the design and manufacture of light wooden packaging.

It intervenes mainly in:

  • manufacturing Custom wooden boxes and packagings
  • production of solutionslight packaging for industry and transport
  • protection and packaging of fragile or bulky products
  • packaging design adapted to logistical and export constraints

The company is part of the Industrial carpentry and technical packaging, offering solutions adapted to the protection, storage and shipping needs of companies.
