A French Maison of Excellence specialized in decorative and furniture pieces in hide and fur, Norki cultivates rare artisanal craftsmanship in the service of contemporary creation.


The Norki workshop produces unique and bespoke pieces - rugs, throws, cushions, armchairs, sofas, and furniture - for designers, architects, private clients, and major luxury houses, in France and internationally. At the heart of the Maison, several artistic crafts are perpetuated with passion and exacting standards: upholstery, fur sewing, and Lunéville embroidery.

This excellence in craftsmanship, passed down through generations and reinterpreted to meet modern requirements, earned Norki the Living Heritage Company (EPV) label in 2023 - a prestigious recognition of its commitment to French craftsmanship.


The transmission and promotion of artisanal trades are inseparable from Norki's DNA.The Maison is actively involved in events dedicated to craftsmanship, notably the European Days of Crafts, as well as in numerous design and innovation fairs.        

Activities & Expertise

  • Bespoke design and manufacturing of rugs, throws, cushions, furniture, and wall coverings made from natural materials (shearling, leather, fur, wool, and wood).
  • The House releases two annual collections (Spring-Summer / Autumn-Winter), as well as capsule collections and artistic collaborations.

 Through its design office and interior styling studio, Norki designs and transforms exceptional chalets, hotels, and residences worldwide. Turnkey interior design projects, bespoke creations, and the curation and restoration of vintage furniture make Norki an essential reference in luxury decoration, particularly renowned in alpine and mountain environments.
