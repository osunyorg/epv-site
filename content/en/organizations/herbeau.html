The HERBEAU House, founded in 1857, is proud to maintain an original design in the world of health: the quality and charms of the "FAIT MAIN".

This commitment, to the genuine nature of the products and the know-how it involves, has been rooted in our society for five generations.

Since the "Salons de Bains" designed by the founder Louis HERBEAU at the end of the 19th century, passing through the original creations of Désiré HERBEAU at the beginning of the 20th century and the inventiveness of Jean HERBEAU at the end of the 19th century, the family tradition has always allowed us to preserve the singular place of our society in the universe of Bath, Vestiaire and Cuisine.

Despite a commitment to tradition, the company HERBEAU seeks to be in line with its time. It thus vogues according to trends. Thus, even today, Lionel HERBEAU, as a worthy heir to the family, continues to draw new lines of taps or kitchen sinks, demonstrating that the trend "RETRO" can marry with all the interior atmospheres.

The HERBEAU House, founded in 1857, is proud to maintain an original design in the world of health: the quality and charms of the "FAIT MAIN".

This commitment, to the genuine nature of the products and the know-how it involves, has been rooted in our society for five generations.

Since the "Salons de Bains" designed by the founder Louis HERBEAU at the end of the 19th century, passing through the original creations of Désiré HERBEAU at the beginning of the 20th century and the inventiveness of Jean HERBEAU at the end of the 19th century, the family tradition has always allowed us to preserve the singular place of our society in the universe of Bath, Vestiaire and Cuisine.

Despite a commitment to tradition, the company HERBEAU seeks to be in line with its time. It thus vogues according to trends. Thus, even today, Lionel HERBEAU, as a worthy heir to the family, continues to draw new lines of taps or kitchen sinks, demonstrating that the trend "RETRO" can marry with all the interior atmospheres.
