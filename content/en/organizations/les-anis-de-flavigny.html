Les Anis de Flavigny is a historic French confectionery that has been making anise candy since 1591.

Located in the former abbey of Flavigny-sur-Ozerain, the house perpetuates a unique artisanal know-how consisting of covering a seed of anise with successive layers of flavoured sugar, according to a traditional method unchanged for centuries. The Anis de Flavigny are today an emblematic product of French gourmet heritage, combining history, authenticity and artisanal manufacture.
