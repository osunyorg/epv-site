Stoelzle Perfumery & Cosmetics is a key business unit of the Stoelzle Glass Group, specializing in luxury packaging for the Perfumery, Beauty, and Home Care industries.

Our commitment to excellence is embodied by Stoelzle Masnières Parfumerie, our French glassworks established in 1818 and recognized as an Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant.

With over 200 years of glassmaking expertise, this facility specializes in luxury perfumery, skin, and hair care, serving as the primary producer of high-quality containers for these markets. In addition, some of our beauty packaging is expertly crafted and decorated at our Polish site in Czestochowa.

We proudly partner with world-leading brands, offering comprehensive expertise in design, decoration, and full-service packaging to support their success. Leveraging the extensive resources and knowledge of the Group, we continue to set new standards in this industry, delivering innovative products that elevate each brand's appeal and ensure top quality.

Stoelzle Perfumery & Cosmetics is a key business unit of the Stoelzle Glass Group, specializing in luxury packaging for the Perfumery, Beauty, and Home Care industries.

Our commitment to excellence is embodied by Stoelzle Masnières Parfumerie, our French glassworks established in 1818 and recognized as an Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant.

With over 200 years of glassmaking expertise, this facility specializes in luxury perfumery, skin, and hair care, serving as the primary producer of high-quality containers for these markets. In addition, some of our beauty packaging is expertly crafted and decorated at our Polish site in Czestochowa.

We proudly partner with world-leading brands, offering comprehensive expertise in design, decoration, and full-service packaging to support their success. Leveraging the extensive resources and knowledge of the Group, we continue to set new standards in this industry, delivering innovative products that elevate each brand's appeal and ensure top quality.
