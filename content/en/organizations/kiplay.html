Adapting the work clothes produced in our factories into everyday clothing
In existence since 1921, Kiplay Vintage is a family business handed down from generation to generation, specializing in workwear. Kiplay Vintage, ambassador for Normandy at the United Nations Global Compact, is also certified Entreprise de Patrimoine Vivant, ISO14001, Ecocert and ISO9001. Our flagship products include jeans, work jackets, overalls and pants. Kiplay Vintage works with cotton, moleskin and gabardine.
