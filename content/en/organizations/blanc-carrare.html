Founded in 1998 in Niderviller, Lorraine, by Jacqueline Lestingi-Faure, Blanc Carrare is a specialist stone-crafting manufacture dedicated to the transformation of exceptional natural stones. Drawing on over 70 years of expertise, the company is recognized as a premier partner for the most discerning architects and designers.

Core Activities & Services

  • Full Project Management: Comprehensive support from initial study to final installation, including consultancy, sourcing, design, crafting, implementation, and post-project follow-up
  • Global Sourcing: Procurement of unique natural stones (marbles, onyx, quartzites, Auvergne lava stones) from a network of over 200 quarries selected for their rarity and character
  • Technical Design: Precise site surveys, executive plans, 3D renderings, photo-composition, and technical specifications, all executed with a rigorous architectural approach
  • Artisanal & Industrial Manufacturing: Production at the Niderviller (France) and Farvagny (Switzerland) workshops, combining high-precision CNC machinery with traditional hand-finishing and unique patinas
  • Expert Installation: Carried out by highly qualified stonemasons following a "dry lay" (pre-assembly) in the workshop to ensure perfect alignment and quality control at every stage
  • After-Sales & Maintenance: Specialized services including polishing, crystallization, repairs, maintenance contracts, and tailored stone-care products

Values & Positioning


  • Tradition & Innovation: A fusion of heritage craftsmanship-certified as an Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV)-and modern technical requirements
  • Excellence & Uniqueness: Every project is treated as a masterpiece, utilizing materials chosen for their unique character and refined finishes
  • A Creative Vision of Stone: Matter is elevated into a form of artistic and technical expression, embodying the distinctive signature of the House
