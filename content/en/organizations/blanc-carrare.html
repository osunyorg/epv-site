Blanc Carrare, founded in 1998 in Niderviller (Lorraine), by Jacqueline Lestingi-Faure, is a stone fabricator specializing in the transformation of exceptional natural stone.  

On a stoneworking site with nearly 70 years of history, Blanc Carrare has become a leading partner to architects and interior designers.   Awarded the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label in 2010, Blanc Carrare has preserved its traditional craftsmanship in the shaping and transformation of natural stone while continually modernizing its operations. Its ongoing pursuit of innovation, surface treatments (finishes and patinas), and creativity allows it to deliver ever more ambitious architectural projects.  

Blanc Carrare brings a distinctive and exacting perspective to stone, shaping it from its very core. From lava stone to marble, onyx, and quartzite, each block is meticulously sourced from a global network of suppliers before being transformed and refined in its workshops in France.
