Historically a master soap-maker specializing in the manufacture of liquid Marseille soap, Laboratoire Provendi is now also a cosmetics manufacturer, offering a complete range of products. 

Added to this is its expertise as a master alum craftsman, specializing in the custom manufacture of products based on natural alum stone.
