Implemented since 1931 Comines (59), the Marbrerie Vandermarlière is specialized in engraving, marble building and classical decoration and Stone cutting.

Thanks to our creativity design office, we offer our clients innovative solutions and help them choose the most suitable material for their project: stone, marble or granite. This material can itself be associated with metal (iron, stainless steel, bronze, brass, etc.), wood or glass. Intervention on monumental engravings to create unique objects, our assets are the expertise and creativity.

Our areas of intervention: restoration, maintenance and copying of ancient works with cabinetmakers and antiques. We also provide, live or as a subcontractor, work of engraving and inlaying of large marble companies. Different artists, designers and architects also use our services.

Thanks to our expertise especially in engraving and inlays (marking, mosaic and scagliole) as well as in forgotten techniques (scenes on buckwheat vaults), we have realized the fronton of Lille Flanders station, the court Human Rights in Lille, the tribute to Jacques Brel in Anderlecht (Belgium) and also participated in exhibitions at the FIAC in Paris (Foire International Contemporary Art) with works for artists contemporary.

Our creed: giving life to the stone by combining our ancestral know-how with modern and innovative techniques.
