LOUIS GENESTE is the heritage of more than 155 years of family and entrepreneurial spirit, a world of excellence, where the culture of a job well done is deployed with respect for men and women.  

Based in Clermont-Ferrand, Rosiers d'Egletons, Murat and Paris, the company offers masonry, stonemasonry, sculpture, roofing and zinc work. LOUIS GENESTE is a masonry company specializing in the restoration of old buildings. Established in the Auvergne capital for four generations, it has built up a reputation for high-quality craftsmanship, which has been expressed in numerous regional heritage buildings. A specialist in the restoration of old buildings, Louis Geneste stands out for its use of traditional methods, which have made the company a benchmark in its field in Auvergne, Centre France and Paris.
