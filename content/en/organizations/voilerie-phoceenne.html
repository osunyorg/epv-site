Known for its traditional know-how, specialized in the craft manufacture of hand-made sails and their interviews for sailing yachts and regattas. It manufactures dormant and current riggings, offers mast refit services and mating services as well as the manufacture and installation of accasting. The Phocéenne Sailing is committed to the preservation of all sailboats of all ages, it is distinguished by its quality, its tradition and the service provided, so it is labeled Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant for more than 10 years.
