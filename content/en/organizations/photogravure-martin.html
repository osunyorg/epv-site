Created in 1989 by Mr MARTIN Pierre and his son Jean-Pierre, PHOTOGRAVURE MARTIN specializes in textile engraving. Over the years, the company has extended its activity to the manufacture of glass screen printing screens. Since 2016, it has been expanding into the advertising digital printing sector.
