Since 1970, Atelier ARCOA is dedicated to the restoration of the heritage and the realization exceptional construction sites, be they buildings classified as monuments historic buildings, private homes or prestigious hotels.

Among our references include the Palace of Versailles, Notre Dame Cathedral of Paris, the Senate, the Popes' Palace, the Elysée Palace and the Navy Hotel, To mention just a few.

Strong of their complementary expertise, our curators-restaurateurs, painters decorators, gilders and painters create and restore the most beautiful painted decorations, canvases and polychrome furniture objects. Between tradition and modernity, rigour and creativity, they transmit ancestral know-how from generation to generation, while integrating scientific and contemporary techniques.
