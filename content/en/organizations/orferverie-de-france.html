Located in Mandres-la-Côte in the Haute-Marne region, Orfèvrerie de France is the direct heir to the craftsmanship and expertise of the Deetjen and Chambly manufactures.

Certified as a Living Heritage Company (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant - EPV), the firm perpetuates 100% French artisanal manufacturing, blending ancestral traditions with environmentally friendly processes.

The workshop shapes nickel silver, sterling silver, and stainless steel to create exceptional pieces.Renowned for the finesse of its finishes, the manufacture supplies the most prestigious tables in fine dining and luxury hospitality, while offering timeless collections for private individuals.

Core Activities

  • Exceptional Table Cutlery: Design and manufacture of over 20 cutlery patterns (Louis XV, Régence, Art Deco styles) in sterling silver, silver-plated nickel silver, or 18/10 stainless steel
  • Tableware & Silverware: Production of prestigious service pieces such as caviar bowls, tureens, sauce boats, and trays with meticulous finishes
  • Re-silvering & Restoration: Specialized refurbishment service for antique cutlery and silverware, restoring brilliance and protection to family heirlooms or collector's items
  • Mastery of Rare Techniques: Application of closed-circuit silver-plating processes, artisanal polishing, and exclusive finishes such as matte "Vintage Silver" or mirror-polish
  • Luxury Hospitality Solutions: Bespoke support for culinary professionals in creating tableware tailored to the rigorous demands of haute gastronomie
