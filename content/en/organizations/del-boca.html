Since 1931, Atelier Louis Del Boca has been recognized for the excellence of its achievements and know-how in the fields of decoration, staff, stucco (orphan know-how), ornamentation, painting, decorative painting.

With its multiplicity of know-how, interior decor can be created or restored by the Louis Del Boca workshop alone. The staff can be painted, the bas-reliefs are made of stucco stone, the stucco secular marble adorns itself with very contemporary colors, the painting mixes with plaster for new effects.

Labelled EPV, member of the Groupement des Monuments Historiques, with a workshop manager MOF, the workshop is one of the key actors of French Heritage His companions perpetuate and transmit the traditions of traditional French styles and techniques.

Our exceptional workshop and our construction sites in the most prestigious places are the best example, from Place de la Concorde with the famous Hôtel de la Marine, to the cornices of the room of the Sacre du Château de Versailles, at the top of the Samaritaine at the suites of George V. Recently, we had the chance to create a table in marble stucco, bringing back to the taste of this centuries-old technique.
