 Founded in 1996, Toffolo & Fils is a family-owned business located in Witry‑lès‑Reims, in the Marne department. Carrying a renowned artisanal expertise in the fine crafts, the company blends tradition with modernity through a team of highly skilled professionals.

Core Activities

    • Artistic Ironwork : Creation, repair, and restoration of gates, doors, handrails, railings, canopies, pergolas, and bespoke metal furniture-combining functionality with refined style
    • Bespoke Metalwork : Fabrication of metal structures tailored to contemporary or industrial projects (gates, staircases, terraces, privacy screens, ...)
    • Metal Joinery and Glazing : Design and installation of glass partition, sliding doors, glass walls, glass floor tiles, and other structures combining glass and metalwork
    • Custom Staircases : Design and installation of interior and exterior staircases (spiral, sawtooth stringer, double stringer, etc.), entirely custom-made to suit your space
