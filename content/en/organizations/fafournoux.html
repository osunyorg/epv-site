Ets Fafournoux, located in the Thiers industrial area, is renowned for its expertise in cutting and stamping parts for cutlery and tableware. With over 90 years of experience, the company has also expanded into technical markets such as aeronautics, parachuting and the medical sector. Always open to new projects, Ets Fafournoux can support you from design to manufacture of all unique parts thanks to its expertise, commitment and reliability.
