Manufacturer of custom stainless steel parts Made in France, we offer a quality and precision solution for kitchen manufacturers, pharmaceutical industries, food processing, refrigeration, equipment and other sectors. Specialist in the custom manufacturing of stainless steel parts, our company offers a reliable and quality solution for various sectors.

With more than 30 years of experience, we are able to satisfy the most complex requests thanks to our know-how labeled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" and our commitment to quality. Our workshop is committed to a "Social and Environmental Responsibility" approach; our EcoVadis label, the global standard for CSR assessments, is underway.
