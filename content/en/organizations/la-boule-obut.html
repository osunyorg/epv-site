OBUT, a passion for pétanqueMore than just a manufacturer of boules, OBUT is a company with a passion for pétanque. For many years, OBUT has been committed to promoting pétanque in France and abroad by supporting players, taking part in competitions and offering innovative products. OBUT's aim is to make pétanque a sport that is accessible to all, friendly and inter-generational.
