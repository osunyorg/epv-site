Domaine Les Crayères, located in the heart of Reims, is an iconic five-star hotel. Built in 1904, the Château is nestled within a peaceful seven-hectare park, surrounded by prestigious neighbors such as Ruinart, Veuve-Clicquot, Pommery, and Taittinger.

Core Offerings

  • Luxury Accommodation: 20 elegantly decorated rooms and suites that seamlessly blend classic French style with timeless refinement
  • Gastronomic Restaurant "Le Parc":** Led by Chef Christophe Moret, this two-Michelin-starred restaurant offers modern and creative cuisine, highlighting local "terroir" and expert champagne pairings
  • Brasserie "Le Jardin": Featuring seasonal cuisine in a contemporary setting within the park. Awarded a Bib Gourmand, it offers shared platters and a unique selection of wines by the glass served from magnums

Experiences & Exclusive Services

  • Curated Stays: Exceptional gift sets and stays including overnight accommodation, fine dining, champagne, and breakfast
  • Events & Producers' Market: An annual regional producers' market held every September, alongside exclusive themed evenings
  • World-Class Champagne List: Boasting over 1,000 references, it is widely regarded as one of the most prestigious wine lists in the world
