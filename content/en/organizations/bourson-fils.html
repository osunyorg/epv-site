Bourson & Fils is a family business that opened its doors in 1901.

Today, we, the 5th generation of marble masons and sculptors, welcome you to our workshop in Gouvieux (between Senlis and Sarcelles).

Our ancestral know-how is combined with the technological innovations of our century.

We are proud to uphold our company's slogan, which has been in force for over a century, with the same passion:

“A stone in every building”.

Bourson & Fils is a family business that opened its doors in 1901.

Today, we, the 5th generation of marble masons and sculptors, welcome you to our workshop in Gouvieux (between Senlis and Sarcelles).

Our ancestral know-how is combined with the technological innovations of our century.

We are proud to uphold our company's slogan, which has been in force for over a century, with the same passion:

“A stone in every building”.
