Over 70 years of staff

Rouveure Marquez offers top-of-the-range services based on proven expertise. Maison Rouveure Marquez has the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company) label, Qualibat certification and is a member of the Comité Bellecour.  

For modern modifications of interior volumes, made-to-measure creations, innovative shapes and volumes. Thanks to its ancestral know-how, the Rouveure Marquez workshop can produce your own staff creations for original decors with a contemporary edge.

Traditional art made from natural plaster of Paris. Decorative elements from the 17th to the 20th century and custom-made motifs. The craftsmen of the Rouveure Marquez workshop focus on making cornices and moldings in the tradition of traditional craftsmanship.

Over 70 years of staff

Rouveure Marquez offers top-of-the-range services based on proven expertise. Maison Rouveure Marquez has the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company) label, Qualibat certification and is a member of the Comité Bellecour.  

For modern modifications of interior volumes, made-to-measure creations, innovative shapes and volumes. Thanks to its ancestral know-how, the Rouveure Marquez workshop can produce your own staff creations for original decors with a contemporary edge.

Traditional art made from natural plaster of Paris. Decorative elements from the 17th to the 20th century and custom-made motifs. The craftsmen of the Rouveure Marquez workshop focus on making cornices and moldings in the tradition of traditional craftsmanship.
