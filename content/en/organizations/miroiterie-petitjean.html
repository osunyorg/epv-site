Located in Houdemont, near Nancy, Miroiterie Petitjean is a benchmark in artistic glasswork. Certified as an Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) - a label recognizing French excellence in traditional and industrial skills - the company stands out for its mastery of rare techniques such as thermoforming, silvering, and engraving.

The workshop transforms glass into designer furniture and high-end interior fittings, blending the material's inherent transparency with boundless creativity for both public and private sectors.

Core Activities

  • Contemporary Furniture Design : Conceptualization and manufacturing of tables, desks, and consoles that merge glass with artistic design
  • High-End Interior Fittings : Creation of bespoke glass doors, display cases, and partitions for residential and commercial spaces
  • Decoration and Sculptures : Production of art mirrors, glass paintings, and personalized trophies
  • Mastery of Rare Techniques : Application of artisanal thermoforming, engraving, and silvering processes to achieve exclusive finishes
  • Specialized Technologies : Utilization of advanced processes, including digital printing, lacquering, and thermoforming, to deliver unique and personalized creations
