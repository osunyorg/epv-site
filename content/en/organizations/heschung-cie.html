Founded in 1934 in Steinbourg (Bas-Rhin) by Eugène Heschung, the brand remains deeply rooted in Alsace, combining family tradition with artisanal excellence. As a certified Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV), the house has perpetuated master heritage techniques since its inception, most notably Norwegian welted and Goodyear welted constructions.

Products and Positioning

  • Premium Leather Footwear: A range of derbies, boots, and loafers designed for longevity, characterized by their sturdiness, high-quality materials, and the ability to be resoled
  • Discerning Clientele: Targeted toward enthusiasts of durable, elegant, and French-made products

High-End Manufacturing

  • Men's and Women's Collections: Crafted for durability with a distinct high-end positioning
  • Noble Materials: Exclusive use of premium leathers, primarily sourced from European tanneries
  • Artisanal Production: Entirely manufactured in its historical Alsatian workshop in Steinbourg
