Founded by Adrien Jaminet, the company carries on a long-standing tradition of craftsmanship that has long been renowned for brass instrument making in France.

While the French trumpet style had almost disappeared since the 1950s, A.JAMINET has made it its mission to bring it back to prominence by developing high-end instruments entirely made in France.

Each trumpet is the result of meticulous craftsmanship, combining acoustic precision, traditional manufacturing techniques, and a focus on sound.

The company designs and manufactures its instruments respecting historical methods while incorporating the contemporary demands of professional musicians.
