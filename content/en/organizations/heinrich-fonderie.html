For over a century, Vhm FONDERIE has perpetuated the art of foundry in Alsace, blending ancestral craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. The company embodies both industrial and artisanal excellence, ensuring the transmission of rare expertise while integrating contemporary innovations to provide high-quality cast iron for both highly technical parts and decorative creations.  

Industrial Competencies

The iron foundry specializes in sand casting for parts weighing from 1 kg to 800 kg, produced in small to medium series using Grey Iron (EN-GJL), Ductile Iron (EN-GJS), and alloyed iron. Its strengths include:  

  • Co-development and engineering: its strength resides in part optimization, core integration, and conversion of welded assemblies into compact castings
  • Rapid prototyping : 3D printing
  • Filling and solidification simulation : CAD/CAM (SolidWorks / SolidCAM)
  • Electric induction melting : two furnaces with a capacity of 1 ton each
  • Machining and premium finishing : robotic deburring, heat treatments, and coatings

  Activity Segments

  • Water & infrastructure : fittings, flanges, valves, branch collars, manholes, and drainage grates
  • Industry & mechanics : pump bodies, centrifugal impellers, motor housings, and transmission parts
  • Urban furniture: lampposts, bollards, tree grates, benches, and fountains

  Values

  • Tradition & innovation :  mastery of historical sand casting processes, enhanced by CAD, digital simulation, and 3D printing
  • French excellence : technical parts for the leading industrial companies or decorative pieces for iconic projects (lampposts at "Jardin des Tuileries", "Carrousel du Louvre", "Place Vendôme", "Pont d'Iéna, and Place de Varsovie" in Paris)
  • Sustainable commitment : electric induction melting, ISO 9001 & ISO 50001 certification, and reduced carbon footprint
