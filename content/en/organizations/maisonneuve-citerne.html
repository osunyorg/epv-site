Manufacture of tank trucks and water treatment plants
Founded in 1939, MAISONNEUVE CITERNE is a major player in the design, manufacture and marketing of tankers. The company distinguishes itself by its continuous adaptation to market evolutions, supported by the know-how of its experienced teams. MAISONNEUVE CITERNE relies on officially certified resources and techniques. Its commitment to quality is reflected in a management system that complies with the EN ISO 9001 standard, guaranteeing high-quality products and services for its customers.
