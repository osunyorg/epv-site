L'Atelier Luxe is an independent French manufacturer specialising in the design and manufacture of made-to-measure pieces, cases, boxes and accessories for leading luxury houses. Housed in a listed building, our workshop combines a rare diversity of skills, mainly in sheathing, but also in woodwork, leatherwork, metalwork, ceramics, marquetry, lacquerwork and the integration of technology. At the crossroads of arts and crafts and innovation, we create unique, lasting and meaningful objects.

L'Atelier Luxe is an independent French manufacturer specialising in the design and manufacture of made-to-measure pieces, cases, boxes and accessories for leading luxury houses. Housed in a listed building, our workshop combines a rare diversity of skills, mainly in sheathing, but also in woodwork, leatherwork, metalwork, ceramics, marquetry, lacquerwork and the integration of technology. At the crossroads of arts and crafts and innovation, we create unique, lasting and meaningful objects.
