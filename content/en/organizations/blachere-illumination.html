Blachère Illumination is a French company specialized in the design, manufacture and installation of festive lighting and decorative lighting. Based in Provence, it is known for its know-how in Christmas illuminations and highlights of urban spaces, monuments and events around the world. The company combines creativity, technological innovation and environmental commitment, notably through the development of more energy-efficient LED solutions.
