Precious heritage passed on for 4 generations of carpenters, Bertin et Fils was founded in 1904 in Clermont Ferrand. Today linked to the restoration and conservation of heritage carpentry Built and Historic Monuments work as Atelier Bertin 1904.

We work in the restoration and creation of woodwork, tumbler restoration, monumental carpentry, cochère door, window and traditional parquet.
