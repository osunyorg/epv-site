 Bronzes Strassacker is a family-owned art foundry established in 1919 in Süßen, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Specializing in bronze casting, it is one of the oldest and largest foundries in Europe, employing over 500 people. The company is internationally renowned for its exceptional craftsmanship in creating bronze sculptures, ranging from monumental public artworks to refined decorative objects.

Activities and Services

    • Art Foundry: Production of bronze sculptures using both traditional and digital modeling. This includes monumental works, prestigious trophies (such as the Bambi Award), garden sculptures, and decorative art pieces
    • Strassacker Editions: Collaborative partnerships with contemporary artists to produce limited-edition bronze sculptures available for collectors
    • Strassacker Projects: Management of complex architectural art projects, encompassing the design, fabrication, and installation of bespoke bronze elements for both public and private buildings
    • Sacred & Memorial Art: Design and realization of bronze objects for the funerary and religious sectors, including lanterns, vases, baptismal fonts, and statues
