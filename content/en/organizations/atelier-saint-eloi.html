Antoine Legouy , Ciseur-Orfèvre for more than 40 years, has been working on the creation, restoration and reconstruction of metal ornaments in the Arts de la table , liturgical goldsmithy, luminaire, bronze d'art and jewellery. Awarded by the Minister of National Education, Alain Savary, in 1983 during his last year of the Ecole Boulle for the realization of a vegetable of the regency period, he also obtained the 2nd national prize of the Société d'encouragement des métiers d'art.
