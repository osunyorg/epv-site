The excellence of a French manufacture

Wine, a French heritage par excellence, preserved in a wine cellar manufactured in France, a commitment we have taken since our beginnings in 1976


Since its creation, EuroCave has embodied a unique vision: that of high-end wine cellars, durable, carefully designed and entirely manufactured in FranceThis choice is at the heart of our identity. It reflects our deep attachment to an industrial heritage, to rare know-how, and to the discreet elegance of French art de vivre. To manufacture in France is to meet the highest requirements, keeping total control of every detail, from the choice of materials to the final assembly in our Fourmies factory in the north of France. Producing in France also means guaranteeing constant quality, complete transparency and close proximity to our customers. Our expert teams combine technology, precision and a sense of design - to create wine cellars that respect the wine and reveal its richness, for a sublimated tasting experience. EuroCave, the French know-how at the service of wine lovers.

Certified Living Heritage Company

In 2021 EuroCave was awarded the Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label. This label, awarded by the state, marks the recognition of our know-how built and transmitted for nearly 50 years. It distinguishes our unique know-how in the design and manufacture of cellars allowing the development of the wine, once bottled. A true recognition that underscores the brand's commitment to maintaining skilled and manual trades.
