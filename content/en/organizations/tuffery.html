A responsible and timeless fashion

TUFFERY Workshop offers responsible mode through parts
timeless, sober and durable. A complete locker room (pants, skirts,
dresses, jackets, shirts, shorts... for women and men, as well as a
Workwear range consisting of iconic and robust pieces.

In line with the master tailors, the house develops
also a "size 4.0" approach, offering clothing on demand,
special or tailor-made unit. With advanced digital tools, this
approach makes the custom-made accessible to as many people as possible.

In direct connection with its clients, Atelier TUFFERY also perpetuates
the art of second life, repairing and reshaping its products for
extend their lifetime to the maximum.

Atelier TUFFERY has been designing its collections for 130 years, the oldest manufacturer of French jeans. With a concern for authenticity intact, a desire for elegance and sobriety, Atelier TUFFERY perpetuates this know-how by revisiting and making the iconic models of house, which still make its fame today.

It is in the heart of the Lozère, at the foot of the Cevennes, that the manufacture, pioneer of French jeans, imagines new creations contemporary cuts, adapted to the daily changing room as well as to the requirements of the Today's craftsmen and workers.
