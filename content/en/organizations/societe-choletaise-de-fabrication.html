Since 1969, the Société Choletaise de Fabrication (SCF) has been a creator and manufacturer of narrow textile accessories for customers around the world, a history that has lasted more than half a century. Combining tradition and innovation, SCF puts its know-how and expertise at the service of the markets for fashion, sports, packaging, household linen, interior decoration, industry, etc.

The fleet of 2,000 machines, with around 900 modern machines, 1,107 wood looms and 70 mechanical bobbin braiding machines (lace), continues to grow and reinvent itself to satisfy the requirement and creativity of its customers. Thus, SCF uses ancient machines and modern automates. The know-how of SCF brings together fraternity in human relations, automatism and digitization.

From yarn to finished products, packaging to shipments, SCF is a 100% French company, labeled EPV.
