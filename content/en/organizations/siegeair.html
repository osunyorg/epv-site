Siegeair is a French company specialized in the Custom manufacture of seats and high-end interior design.

It intervenes mainly in:

  • the creation of seats, armchairs and custom seats
  • on sheathing and coating of furniture (leather, fabric, decorative finishes)
  • on Wall and window decorations (wrinkles, hangings, blinds)
  • of projectsinterior arrangement for luxury hotels, offices, shops and residential

The company is part of a tapestry of furnishing and decoration, combining craft and custom production for complex and high-end projects.
