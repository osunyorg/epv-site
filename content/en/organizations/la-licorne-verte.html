Located in Allain, Meurthe-et-Moselle, La Licorne Verte is a unique art studio dedicated to the recreation of plant life.

Awarded the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label, the company is recognized for its rare expertise in blending nature with artifice. Its philosophy is rooted in the use of authentic trunks and branches sourced from local forests-carefully selected for their twisted and expressive shapes-which are given a "second life" through the meticulous grafting of artificial foliage.

Core Activities

  • Creation of Semi-Natural Trees: Bespoke design of large-scale trees (olive, cherry, mirabelle plum, fir, etc.) for events, interior design, and the luxury sector
  • Scenography & Theater Sets: Crafting forests and botanical elements for stage productions, including operas, the Avignon Festival, and various theatrical plays
  • Luxury & Gastronomy Design: Botanical decoration for high-end jewelry salons (such as Boucheron on Place Vendôme), prestigious restaurants, and luxury crystal manufacturer displays (such as Saint-Louis)
  • Whimsical & Artistic Creations: Production of dreamlike, non-realistic trees-golden or multicolored-or those adorned with unusual objects like crystal apples, umbrellas, or written messages
  • Bespoke Commissions: Every project is entirely customized to meet the specific architectural constraints and aesthetic visions of each client, whether they are institutions, businesses, or private individuals
