André Renault: The Excellence of Custom Sleep, Made in France

body: >-

An exceptional know-how, rewarded and recognized

Each André Renault product is made by unit and order: everything is designed to meet the specific needs of each customer. This craftsmanship of precision owes us to be labeled Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV), a distinction awarded to the French houses of excellence, as well as Origin France Garantie, proof of an authentic local manufacture. Our founding pillars:
The best sleep: We select the most noble and innovative materials to promote a repairing sleep, essential to your well-being.
Personalized comfort: Each mattress and box frame adapts to your morphology and preferences for an incomparable custom comfort.
The à la carte design: You compose your bedding according to your desires thanks to a wide range of fabrics, finishes and options.
The best user experience: From design to delivery, we place your satisfaction at the heart of our journey.
Quality commitment and respect for the environment André Renault is ISO 9001 certified, ensuring a high level of quality and continuous improvement. In order to ensure the environmental impact of our business, we focus on responsible and eco-designed materials, and our manufacturing processes meet strict environmental standards. Certified NF Environment, we attach great importance to the selection of our wood, our materials. At André Renault we recycle 98% of our waste through short circuits assisted by local companies. Choosing André Renault means choosing:
The quality and durability of a French craft manufacture,
Custom comfort designed for your well-being,
An elegant and customizable design,
A company committed to a more responsible future.
Give yourself the unique experience of a bedding designed specifically for you, and rediscover the pleasure of a repairing sleep, night after night.
