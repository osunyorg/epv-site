La restauration du Château de Villers-Coterrêts
La restauration du Château de Villers-Cotterêts met en lumière un savoir-faire d'exception mêlant patrimoine, artisanat et innovation. À travers ce chantier ambitieux, artisans, architectes et entreprises spécialisées ont uni leurs compétences pour redonner vie à ce monument historique emblématique, aujourd'hui devenu un symbole de la valorisation du patrimoine français.

Located in Paris and Burgundy, the workshops operate across the French territory. Recognized partners of public institutions, architects, and private clients, their teams support each project from the diagnostic phase through to restoration, with strict respect for the materials of sculpted heritage - stone, plaster, terracotta, staff, and concrete. Reversibility, compatibility, and traceability lie at the heart of every historic monument project. Among their notable references are Notre-Dame de Paris, Louvre Palace, Saint-Eustache Church, La Madeleine, Notre-Dame de Reims, and Château de Villers-Cotterêts. The workshops also create new sculptures - architectural ornaments, interior décor, and statuary - crafted in stone, marble, plaster, or staff. These works are designed for exceptional residences, architectural projects, and private patrons committed to preserving centuries-old craftsmanship.
