It was in Ercuis, a charming village in the Oise region, that the pantographic silversmithing manufacture was founded in 1867, now standing as an emblem of the excellence and craftsmanship of French Haute Orfèvrerie.

Our Master Silversmiths craft exceptional creations designed to adorn and elevate the world's most prestigious tables.

Each of our prestigious pieces is entirely handcrafted, in the purest respect for traditional artisanal techniques that embody the excellence and heritage of our craft.

Awarded the EPV label and a member of the "J'aime l'Oise" network, ERCUIS continues to uphold, within its historic workshops and through the excellence of its artistic professions, the most refined traditions of the Art of Tableware.

Our House also places its expertise at the service of restoring silverware and precious objects of every origin - table service pieces, sculptures, works of art, jewelry, and other precious creations - granting them renewed life while preserving their brilliance through time.
