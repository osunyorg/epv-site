Created in 1954 under the name of "Armand Barreau Furniture", Sylvaplak has been developed over decades and has risen to the top without forgetting its know-how in woodworking and veneering. Today, Sylvaplak values his unique expertise and the talent of his collaborators. All stages of manufacturing rely on coordination and communication between the different workshops. The company encourages this multidisciplinary work to improve their products every day.
