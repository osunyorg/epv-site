Refinement and quality to enhance your elegance
H2o parapluies is one of France's last remaining umbrella manufacturers. Our workshop is located in Crépon in the Calvados region. Since our creation in 1994, we have always defended French manufacturing, where the hand still has a role, a mission: to guide, sew, cut and shape. We have been a living heritage company since August 2021, and all our models come with a lifetime guarantee. Our umbrellas are top-of-the-range products.
