Pol Roger is a family-owned Champagne House founded in 1849 by Pol Roger himself. Based in Épernay, it is renowned for its high-end champagnes, most notably the famous "Cuvée Sir Winston Churchill."

The House produces approximately 1.5 million bottles per year, sourced from vineyards located in the finest Champagne terroirs. Blending tradition with modernity, Pol Roger maintains artisanal methods while innovating, with a presence in over 90 countries worldwide.

Core Activities

High-end Champagne Production

  • Approximately 1.5 million bottles produced annually from vineyards selected among the best Champagne raw

Iconic vintages 

    • Brut Réserve: The signature cuvée, balanced and elegant
    • Cuvée Sir Winston Churchill: A prestige cuvée dedicated to Winston Churchill, symbolizing ultimate excellence
    • Brut Vintage, Blanc de Blancs, Rosé Vintage, Vinothèque: Vintage ranges crafted from exceptional years

    Traditional and Artisanal Methods

    •  Winemaking that respects ancestral techniques, particularly manual riddling, ensuring finesse and superior quality
    • Innovation and Modernization: Recent investments in modern infrastructure to seamlessly combine tradition with productivity
    • International Reach: Exported to over 90 countries, with a particularly strong standing in key markets such as the United Kingdom

    Cultural Engagement and Patronage

    •  Involvement in sponsorships and the promotion of cultural events to enhance the brand's image and heritage
