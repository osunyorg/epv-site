Founded in 1979 by Jean-Pierre Besse, the Manufacture de sièges Jean-Pierre Besse has established itself as a benchmark in the creation of period furniture, ranging from classical Louis XIII styles to Art Deco seating.

As a true guardian of French tradition, this family-run business-now co-managed with his son Mickaël-has stood the test of time by balancing heritage and innovation, reimagining traditional models to create more contemporary lines.

Core Activities

  • Manufacturing of all furniture styles, 100% handmade in France: chairs, bridge chairs, armchairs, wing chairs (bergères), sofas, benches, bar stools, and ottomans
  • Creation of complementary furnishings: bed frames, headboards, tables, consoles, and hotel room furniture
  • Bespoke designs and custom orders: catering to luxury hotels, specialized boutiques, and the export market
  • Furniture restoration: refurbishment and repair of time-worn furniture, leveraging comprehensive expertise in upholstery and cabinet making
