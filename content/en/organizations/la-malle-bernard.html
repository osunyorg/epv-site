Since 1846, La Malle Bernard has maintained the excellence of the malter trade through the creation of tailor-made trunks, boxes and luxury luggage. Inheriting a rare and labeled artisanal know-how Living Heritage Company, the house combines tradition, innovation and quality requirements to design unique pieces for individuals as well as for luxury, fashion and event professionals. Each creation is made in accordance with the historical techniques that make the reputation of French craftsmanship.
