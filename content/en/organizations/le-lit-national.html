National Bedding (Le Lit National), French reference in haute literie since 1909

High bedding (Lithery, mattresses, carpentry) and high decoration (made-to-measure tapisserie, sofas and lounge beds, backrests)

body: >-


At the heart of your home, the bed is essential to your well-being. Convinced that it is more than just a piece of furniture, the National Bed has elevated it to the status of an exceptional product and maintains the confidence that generations of demanding sleepers have given it. Maison labeled EPV (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant), the National Lit founded its reputation for excellence on three pillars: - strict selection of natural raw materials; - the accuracy of traditional craftsmanship; - the perfection of advice and tailor-made service. From our workshops at the Pré-Saint-Gervais to our shop in the Place du Trocadéro, we cultivate a unique know-how and realize the order of high-end products, incomparable sleeping comfort and unrivalled decorative elegance. We now let you imagine the bed of your dreams, the one that will bring you the perfect balance to sleep as well as beautiful... The Beaufour-Lévy family and the entire National Bed team
