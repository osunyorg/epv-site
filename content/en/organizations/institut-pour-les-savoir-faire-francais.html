Promote French expertise and excellence to the general public

  • Document know-how and the companies that embody it
  • Facilitate access to visibility for companies with exceptional expertise
  • Create conditions conducive to encounters between professionals and their public
  • Contribute to the enrichment of the cultural offer linked to know-how through the development of innovative and adapted promotion tools.

Ensure the continuity of excellent know-how through its transmission and the lifelong training of professionals,

  • Make training opportunities more attractive and easier to understand
  • Encourage information sharing and meetings focused on professional training
  • Contribute to the improvement of the initial and continuing training offer for small-volume trades.

Help companies grow in all areas necessary for their development

  • Encourage companies to grow,
  • Promote the company's support offering,
  • Facilitate meetings between professionals and the players capable of supporting them.
  • Contribute to the improvement of the support offer
