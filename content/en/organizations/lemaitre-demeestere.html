Lemaitre Demeestere is a French textile company created in 1835; we are the 5th oldest textile company in France. We specialize in 100% linen weaving. We weave fabrics from 90 g to 1.5 kg per m². 

Lemaitre Demeestere's know-how is recognized throughout the world (40% of its revenue come from exports). The fabrics are used for furnishings, decoration, interior design, accessories and for the last two years in RTW and sewing. The range also includes fabrics with linen/silk, linen/alpaca and linen/cashmere blends, all of which are perfectly suited to premium brands. 

Linen for a better world!
