ADENIO is a 100% French-made clothing workshop located near Lyon, specialising in the production of high-end clothing and accessories, ranging from unique pieces to small series for luxury fashion houses, stylists and demanding designers. The workshop masters all haute couture techniques: from design to cutting, refined finishes, folding and packaging, working with both noble materials (silk, linen, cotton, cashmere, etc.) and technical textiles. ADENIO prides itself on combining craftsmanship excellence, responsiveness through its regional location, and environmental responsibility.
