Biscuiterie de l'Abbaye, discover our delicious Normandy cookies and shortbread
From its workshop in Lonlay l'Abbaye, in the heart of the Normandy bocage, Biscuiterie de l'Abbaye continues the family history that began over a century ago. Thanks to the know-how of its pastry bakers and the use of regional raw materials, the cookie factory has been able to preserve the artisanal quality of its original bakery. With its history, culture and values, Biscuiterie de l'Abbaye is naturally committed to social and environmental responsibility. Its know-how is recognized by the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant label.
