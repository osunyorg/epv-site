Since 1850
Drouault is recognized for his craftsmanship and commitment to
comfort and quality. Today's manufacture is still in place at
Mans and develops a variety of models under its own brand and for
other customers with demanding specifications. The agility of his tool
production allows it to address different markets (large stores, retail,
...), but also to seduce the hotel industry
Luxury in four corners of the world.

Company
developed its know-how on sustainability and ethics from the outset,
striving to meet the highest standards of production
responsible.

We
manufacture high-end bedding accessories in natural trimming
French or innovative synthetic fibres. We make every year
more than 1000 models of mattress pillows, duvets and overcomforts® for
our own brand or for other customers, respecting their notebooks
charges. The agility of our production tool allows us to meet demands
personalized and tailor-made with a team and service dedicated to
hotel business.
