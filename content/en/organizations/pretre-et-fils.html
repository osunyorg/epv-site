Priest & Fils is a French historical company specializing in monumental watchmaking and associated technical equipment. Based on a know-how passed on for several generations, it designs, manufactures, installs and restores clocks of complex buildings, dials, ringing systems and mechanical or electrical devices. Combining artisanal tradition and technical innovation, the company intervenes on heritage restoration projects as well as on contemporary installations, in France and internationally. It is a key player in the preservation and enhancement of heritage clocks and precision mechanisms.
