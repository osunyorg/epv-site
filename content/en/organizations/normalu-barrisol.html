Based in Alsace since 1967, Barrisol is the undisputed leader in stretch ceilings. Recognized as a "Living Heritage Company" (EPV), the brand collaborates with world-renowned architects (Jean Nouvel, Zaha Hadid) to transform spaces.

Combining cutting-edge technology and design, Barrisol offers acoustic, luminous, and eco-friendly solutions installed in the world's most prestigious locations.

Core Activities

  • Stretch Ceiling and Wall Design : Custom solutions for residential, commercial, and public spaces
  • Acoustic and Luminous Innovation : Micro-perforated membranes for sound comfort and translucent fabrics for lighting
  • Invisible Air Conditioning : Barrisol Clim system for uniform temperature without drafts
  • 3D Design and Organic Shapes : Creation of suspended structures and complex architectural forms
