DEHONDT Technologies specializes in the design and production of machines for flax processing. A world leader in the winding of long fibers for the agricultural and industrial sectors, the company has a long tradition of innovation in the mechanization of this noble fiber.

DEHONDT Technologies is a design and engineering office capable of responding to all the problems posed by the production of ecological fibers that respect the natural environment, of resolving the difficulties of preparing these fibers in an industrial environment, and of participating in the development and management of natural industrial products. Process design, mechanism design and production, automation development, packaging and handling of natural fibers, complete project management and supervision.
