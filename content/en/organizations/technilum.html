For 55 years, Technilum designs and manufactures aluminum lighting urban furniture.

Our lampposts and columns or light terminals equip public space such as private projects, for quality lighting, often tailor-made, and always singular.

Innovation, creation, design: our achievements are the result of a know-how of excellence that flourishes within an industrial site exceptional located at the gates of Béziers, in an old wine cellar rehabilitated and extended.

La Croisette beach entrances in Cannes, Museum of Champagne in Epernay, Voie des Sacres in Reims, Palais des Papes in Avignon or Liberty Park and Hudson Yards Plaza in New York: Technilum does radiate the Made in France through prestigious achievements in France and abroad.
