Les Ateliers Buguet Fils is a family-owned company specializing in timber framing, roofing, and the restoration of heritage buildings. Located in Joinville (Haute-Marne), the firm perpetuates an expertise passed down through eight generations, seamlessly blending traditional techniques with modern innovations.

Core Activities

  • Timber Framing & Roofing: Design and construction of wooden structures, including comprehensive roofing and insulation solutions
  • Heritage Restoration: Specialized work on historic buildings, utilizing traditional methods to preserve the architectural authenticity of the structures
  • Contemporary Wood Construction: Execution of modern timber-frame projects, integrating current technical standards and innovative wood-building systems
