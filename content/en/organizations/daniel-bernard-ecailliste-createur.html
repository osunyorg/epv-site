An heir to an extremely rare know-how, Daniel Bernard was trained for fifteen years with a Master of Art of the Glacier-Mountain and is now considered one of the last scailists in Europe. His workshop produces Custom eyewear frames, jewellery, accessories and restorations of old objects in scale, entirely handmade according to ancestral techniques. It also works to preserve and transmit this exceptional artisanal heritage.
