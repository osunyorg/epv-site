Specialized in the design and manufacture of custom-made furniture and quality woodwork, Ébénisterie Générale is a modern workshop with a set of processes supported by a design office. The company focuses on creating unique and sustainable pieces, while respecting the environment.

In addition, Ébénisterie Générale has had the EPV - Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant - label since 2025.
