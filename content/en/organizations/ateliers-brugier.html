It was in the 1920s, at the beginning of the Art Deco period, that André Brugier installed his studio.
One hundred years later, the Brugier workshops have demonstrated a recognised expertise in lacquering.

We arecreators,restaurateursandAntiquities. These three activities complement and feed on each other in order to continue to develop the adventure of creation through a 3000-year-old technique.

Our work is aimed at individuals - whether or not they are collectors - as well as professionals: museums, galleries, artists, etc.
