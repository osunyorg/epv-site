Carpentry, cabinetmaking & restoration of historic buildings
Les Ateliers Christophe Bénard is a joinery company based in Yerville, in the Pays de Caux region of France. Over the years, the company has specialized in the traditional manufacture and restoration of fine woodwork from the Middle Ages to the present day: doors, windows, parquet flooring, woodwork, liturgical furniture (stalls, woodwork, altarpieces, confessionals, pulpits). With strict respect for tradition, the company works to safeguard prestigious historic monuments and traditional homes.
