Installed in Vendée since 1992, is today divided into three secure sites where small, medium and large formats are restored. One of the workshops is located within a collective of heritage craftsmen, where other professionals in restoration exhibit, practice and offer their expertise.

Specialized in the conservation and restoration of paintings, Atelier Patrick Buti works with private individuals, collectors, antique dealers, gallery owners, as well as for historical monuments and museums. For everyone, the same attention is paid to the artwork, in respect of the work done according to the rules of art.
