In Isère, France, Paraboot has become one of the last emblems of "Made in France" and "sewn shoes" in the world of footwear. Already present in the four corners of the world, with boutiques opened in Antwerp (Belgium) in 1994, Tokyo (Japan) in 2001, and Brussels (Belgium) in 2003, Paraboot is consolidating its presence abroad.
