Founded in 1857, Billards Toulet is a French company specializing in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of high-end billiards and foosball tables. Blending traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation, Toulet has become a leading name in the world of gaming, both in France and internationally.

Each product is imagined and crafted in our workshops in Bondues, in the heart of northern France, where we pay special attention to materials, finishes, and aesthetic design. We work in a fully artisanal and custom-made manner with wood, metal, paint, varnish, and leather, ensuring each piece reflects a unique level of quality and personalization.

From traditional billiards to contemporary designs that convert into dining or multi-game tables, and including fully customizable foosball tables, our creations meet the expectations of private clients, architects, interior designers, and industry professionals.

Toulet is not limited to billiards: we also design custom-made games tailored to our clients' desires - including poker tables, game tables, pinball machines, dartboards, chess boards, backgammon sets, and many others. We also offer bespoke layout solutions to create unique gaming spaces, whether for private homes, clubs, or professional venues.

Guided by values of excellence, customization, and a deep passion for play, Billards Toulet continues to innovate, offering clients products that are as aesthetically refined as they are functional, designed to stand the test of time with elegance.
