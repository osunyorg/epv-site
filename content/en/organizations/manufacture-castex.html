Since 1870, Manufacture CASTEX, a family-run business specialising in feathers and down, has been making natural, high-quality duvets, edders and pillows. Located in Dax, in the heart of the Landes — a traditional region for duck farming — CASTEX has developed its expertise over generations, while adapting its marketing methods to the modern world with a focus on direct online and factory store sales. A true alliance of tradition and modernity, come and discover how these luxury duvets are manufactured to offer exceptional sleep and the unparalleled qualities of down: thermoregulation, breathability, lightness and warmth.
