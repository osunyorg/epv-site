Although the ways of working have changed over the centuries, the old techniques still remain thanks to these artisans of yesteryear who were able to pass on this ancestral know-how from generation to generation. L'Atelier Auder d'Ornements today adapts these techniques to slightly more modern machines, without sacrificing method, rigor and quality.

Passionate about roof ornaments and bringing together all his know-how, experience and expertise, Stéphane who is a Compagnon du Devoir, created in 1996 his company of ornamental craftsman. To date, Stéphane transmits within the Atelier Auder d'Ornements, the know-how of this exceptional profession. 3 ornamentalists have already been trained under his supervision and teaching, a 4th is in the process of training.
