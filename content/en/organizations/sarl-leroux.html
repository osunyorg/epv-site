It was in 1964 that the roofing framing company was founded, and Gaston Leroux directed it until 1990. At that date, his son Stéphane, «a carpenter companion», took over. After seven years spent on the Tour de France, he has acquired experience and expertise. With a team of old and young carpenters - roofers trained and supervised by the presence in the workforce of a second companion.

The company Leroux is today recognized, it acts on behalf of private and public clients in areas such as: heritage restoration, historical monuments classified or not, new construction especially timber frame (houses, workshops, buildings ...), maintenance and rehabilitation (buildings and residential or tertiary premises).
