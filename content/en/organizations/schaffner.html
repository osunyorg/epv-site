Founded in 1812 near Strasbourg, Schaffner is a seven-generation family business, now located in Duppigheim (Bas-Rhin). The company embodies excellence in metalwork, artistic ironwork, and architectural metal joinery, representing over two centuries of tradition and expertise.

Core Activities

  • Bespoke Metal Staircases: Design and manufacturing of interior and exterior staircases featuring high-end contemporary designs
  • Architectural & Artistic Ironwork: Creation of decorative works, railings, glass partitions (verrières), gates, and complex welded assemblies for various architectural projects
  • Metal Joinery & Fire-Rated Systems: Specialized installations fully compliant with current safety and fire-protection standards
  • Designer Metal Furniture: Creation of custom-made pieces (tables, benches, lighting, display cases, etc.) using noble materials such as steel, stainless steel, brass, and copper, often incorporating glass, leather, or stone
