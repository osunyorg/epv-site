Chevillard (Chevillard Frères)is founded in1850 and based in Avrillé, specialised in thework of noble metals.

She designs and manufactures high-end parts in bronze, brass, gold and silver, including luminaires, decorative objects, auxiliary furniture and silverware elements.

The creations are largely crafted, using traditional techniques (scissors, hammering, repulse), often inspired by French historical models. Recognized for its expertise of excellence, the house is labeled Living Heritage Company (VPA) and belongs to the network of houses specializing in the art and luxury trades, with a strong orientation towards restoration and custom creation for high-end architectural and decoration projects.
