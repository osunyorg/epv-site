Frédéric and Jean-Louis, both cabinetmakers, created Concept Ebénist' in 2007 in Nantes. The company has been awarded the EPV label since 2018 and has surrounded itself with a team of enthusiasts. The workshop collaborates with big names in interior architecture, but also with collectors and private individuals. The company works in Paris, in Europe and internationally.

Specialized in the design manufacturing of exceptional furniture, inspired by Art Deco and high-end design. Cabinetmaking shapes rare materials such as veneer, parchment, shad, straw, gypsum, mica, leather,...
