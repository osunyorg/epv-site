 Souchet Inspired Woodwork is a French house of contemporary cabinetmaking founded by Nicolas Souchet in 2006 and located in Liffol-le-Petit.

Awarded the "Living Heritage Company" (EPV) label, the workshop designs and manufactures high-end bespoke furniture, seamlessly blending traditional craftsmanship with digital technologies.

Specializing in luxury seating, furniture, and custom fit-outs, the company operates across interior architecture, high-end hospitality, yachting, and design publishing. Its philosophy combines design, artisanal precision, and a commitment to sustainability, with all production carried out entirely within its own workshops.

Expertise and Activities

  • Bespoke Furniture and High-End Seating: A fusion of traditional styles and contemporary silhouettes for luxury residential projects, interior design, hospitality, and the international yachting industry
  • Exclusive Design Editions (Souchet Editions): Conceived as a creative laboratory to experiment with new forms and techniques, these collections merge ancestral craftsmanship with contemporary design
  • Digital-Craft Fusion: An intelligent integration of digital tools and manual craftsmanship throughout the production process to ensure absolute precision, quality, and innovation
