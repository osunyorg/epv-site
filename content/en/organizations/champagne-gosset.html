Founded in 1584 in Aÿ, Champagne Gosset is recognized as the oldest wine house in the Champagne region. Today, it is part of the Renaud-Cointreau group while maintaining its independence and family-owned character.

Expertise

  • Blend : Gosset champagnes are crafted from a blend of Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, and Pinot Meunier, sourced primarily from Grand and Premier Crus in the Marne region
  • Distinctive Style : One of the House's hallmarks is the avoidance of malolactic fermentation, which preserves the wines' natural freshness, vivacity, and exceptional aging potential
  • Cellar Aging: Maturation takes place in 19th-century cellars stretching over 1.7 km, with an average of 4 years on the lees, ensuring aromatic complexity and the fineness of the bubbles

Range and Iconic Vintages

The House offers several prestige vintages :

  • Grande Réserve Brut (signature blend)
  • Grand Blanc de Blancs
  • Grand Rosé
  • Grand Millésime (Vintage)
