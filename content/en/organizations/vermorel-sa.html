VERMOREL specializes in restoration of built heritage, stone carving, masonry of heritage and sculpture as well as scaffolding and conservation restoration of stone sculpture. Founded in 1988, it is currently headed by Quentin Muller and has about 60 employees. She is the holder of the label Made in Aveyron and Qualibat qualifications:

  • 2183 Restoration of ancient stone and masonry
  • 2194 Restoration of Stone and Masonry of Historic Monuments
  • 6583 Attribute sculpture

Member of the Groupement des Entreprises de restauration des Monuments Historiques (GMH) and Company Partner of Companions du Devoir. Its field of action is made up primarily of the Occitanie and the neighbouring regions, but its teams can intervene as well beyond, depending on the size or particular interest of the yards. The last example is the reconstruction site of Notre-Dame de Paris, on which our team worked for 15 months in workshop and on site to restore dozens of damaged sculptures and to the pinacle size of the North of the bedside.Since its creation in 1988, Vermorel has been working on the restoration of building heritage, stone cutting, masonry and sculpture. Driven by the transmission and love of history, Dominique Vermorel and his team repair and work to ensure that the memory endures through the ages.
