Andrée Jardin is an artisanal brosserie established in the Nantes region since 1947 whose desire is to share with the greatest number of home accessories that are environmentally friendly, beautiful, functional and reflect a French way of life. Over the decades, the company has specialized in the manufacture of brushes for industry. Their products are used in various sectors, from aeronautics to metallurgy through shipbuilding.

Andrée Jardin is labeled as a living heritage company. The selection of natural raw materials and the craftsmanship necessary for the manufacture of Andrée Jardin products are based on the experience of several generations and allow obtaining an exclusive quality. Our desire is to promote objects made of natural materials, far from mass production all plastic.
