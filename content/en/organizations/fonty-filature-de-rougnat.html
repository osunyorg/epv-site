Established in 1880 on the banks of the Cher, a few kilometres from its source in the heart of the Combrailles region of Creuse, the traditional textile company Filature Fonty is one of the area's most valuable heritage sites. Labelled  Living Heritage Company (EPV) in 2017 and again in 2022, this traditional manufacturer has been perpetuating exceptional know-how for over 145 years, passed down from generation to generation.
