Atelier Meyer, located in Schiltigheim (Bas-Rhin), is a prestigious art house built upon three generations of

Best Craftsmen of France : Michel, Jean-Jacques, and Pascal Meyer.

Certified as an Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) since 2006, the workshop also holds the French Museums Accreditation, a testament to its excellence in heritage conservation.

Core Activities

  • Gold Leaf Gilding : Expert gilding services on all types of supports, including frames, furniture, decorative elements, musical instruments, and architectural features
  • Bespoke Craftsmanship : Every project is custom-handled with a highly controlled level of finish by a team of exceptional artisans
  • Conservation and Restoration : Specialized restoration of gilded wood, paintings (on wood or canvas), and sculptures. The workshop employs a minimalist approach, ensuring the reversibility of every intervention while strictly respecting the authenticity of the original work
