Maison Bianchi is a family business founded in 1978 in Maizières-lès-Metz, specializing in exceptional watchmaking and jewelry.

Recognized with the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company) label, it perpetuates a rare artisanal expertise that blends tradition and innovation.

Passed down from generation to generation, Maison Bianchi embodies excellence, precision, and a passion for the craft.

The watchmaking workshop services all types of watches and clocks, from antique pieces to contemporary models, whether they are collector's items or everyday timepieces.

Services Provided

  • Repair and servicing of mechanical and automatic movements
  • Maintenance and replacement of quartz movements
  • Complete restoration of antique watches and pocket watches
  • Refurbishment of antique clocks and pendulums
  • Quick services: replacement of batteries, crystals, crowns, and bracelets
  • Manufacturing and adjustment of specific watch parts
  • Watch transformation and customization

Each intervention is carried out with strict adherence to traditional techniques, with an absolute commitment to precision and reliability.

Jewelry and Fine Jewelry Design

The expertise of our jeweler-gem maker extends from the initial design to the meticulous restoration of precious jewelry.

Areas of Expertise

  • Custom creation: rings, wedding bands, necklaces, bracelets, and pendants
  • Ring resizing and chain or clasp repair
  • Restoration of antique and family heirloom jewelry
  • Setting of precious and semi-precious stones
  • Transformation and modernization of existing pieces
  • High-precision finishing: polishing, rhodium plating, and cleaning
  • Working with gold, silver, and platinum is guided by meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to durability

Integrated Workshops: Mastery of Artistic Craftsmanship

With over 800 m² of in-house workshops, our team of qualified professionals in the artistic crafts ensures complete control of the manufacturing and repair process:

  • Precision watchmaking and micromechanics
  • Jewelry making and gem-setting techniques
  • Restoration of historical pieces
  • Electroplating and surface finishing (polishing)

This unique organization guarantees rigorous quality control, optimal responsiveness, and the preservation of exceptional artisanal skills.
