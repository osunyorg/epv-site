From the bowels of the Earth, shaped by time and elements, the lava become stone is the PYROLAVE working base. Volvic lava stone is a material Natural, extracted in the heart of the Auvergne Volcans Regional Natural Park. It carries in it all the nobility and power of nature. In addition to its volcanic origin, if PYROLAVE is a product of character it is also thanks to the singular rendering enamelled lava: colors, shapes, finishes, ... There is a infinity of possibilities to respond to tailor-made projects where each room is unique. Synonym of luxury and refinement, PYROLAVE offers incomparable performance (permanence, extreme resistance to high heat, UV and frost) for architecture, interior design, hotels and even retail. Comptoir et banque d'accueil, kitchen, bathroom, table, wall covering, swimming pool, spa or signposting: the uses of enamelled lava Volvic is numerous. From Sydney to New York, from London in Tokyo, PYROLAVE is a showcase of French know-how that invites you to the most prestigious and exclusive places.
