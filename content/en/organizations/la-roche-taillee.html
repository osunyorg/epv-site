La Roche Taillée, a unique company
Based in Sotteville-lès-Rouen (76), La Roche Taillée has been perpetuating traditional stoneworking techniques since 1989, the year it was founded. The company's seven employees are also equipped with high-performance machines for cutting, molding and polishing the natural materials you choose. Limestone, marble, granite, slate and quartzite are just some of the materials used. Whatever your project, the La Roche Taillée team will be able to advise you on the choice of raw material best suited to your needs.
