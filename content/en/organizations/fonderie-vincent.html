Passionate foundrymen with know-how developed over more than 144 years. We are pattern-makers, molders, foundrymen, fettlers, cabinet-makers, welders, metallurgists, operators, designers, support function specialists, renovators and enthusiasts of cast-iron heritage, craftsmen and technicians. With a small, dynamic and responsive team, we develop small and medium-sized prototypes from files or parts. We work with manufacturers, local authorities and private individuals.
