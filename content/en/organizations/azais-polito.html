The Azaïs-Polito artisanal cannery is the oldest cannery of Sète specialties in the city of Sète. For almost 60 years it has been manufacturing 100% natural seafood products. All preserves are produced using artisanal cannery methods, respecting taste and traditional know-how. Azaïs-Polito offers authentic products such as Sétoise-style rock fish soup, whole fish bouillabaisse, cod brandade, small octopus marinades and original rillettes, etc.
