Moissonnier, a French luxury brand

At the crossroads of tradition and avant-garde, Moissonnier continues to shape its own style, personified by emotion, inspired by fantasy, and always with its own meticulous yet idiosyncratic character.

These values help make Moissonnier the sought-after choice for discerning customers. A brand synonymous with bold, innovative design. A complete collection and a trademark recognized by design professionals, respecting tradition and creating a timeless appeal of quality to match the originality of their design.
