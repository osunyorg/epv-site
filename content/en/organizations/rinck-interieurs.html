The professionals in our design studio, masters of the history and codes of luxury furniture, design a complete contemporary collection every year, as well as numerous bespoke pieces for connoisseurs and discerning collectors. This expertise is based on over 180 years of creative work in the most varied styles, in collaboration with our group's in-house workshops. With their in-depth knowledge of materials and finishes, our furniture creation teams are perfectly at ease designing the most complex pieces of furniture using wood, stone, leather, precious stones, wood and straw marquetry, or even metal.
