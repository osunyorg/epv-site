Founded in 1951, the company specialises in manufacture of laces on Leavers trades.

The lace is made according to the destination of the product. (band, scale, flying, gall, laize).

The company has a rare know-how on the manufacture of Leavers laces. This know-how is based on a set of manual and specific tasks: shelling, extirpation, pre-visit, load the weft wires with the wheel or reel, the pressing, pre-visiting to obtain a coil thickness conformity, ect.

The profession of tullist, rare and complex is exercised by about fifteen of the persons in the enterprise.

All production is sold to Solstiss Group, brand of distribution of articles developed by Victor Machu, the finishing is insured by the company AEG J Machu.
