Recognized for its exceptional textile know-how and labeled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV), the brand designs and manufactures high-end bed linen, bath linen and decoration, combining artisanal excellence, creativity and elegance with French.

Yves Delorme is also committed to responsible production, notably through the use of cotton from organic farming and the preservation of its historical know-how.
