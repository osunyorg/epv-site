Since its founding in Bezannes, Le Bâtiment Menuisier has stood out for its artisanal craftsmanship and passion for wood. Specializing in bespoke joinery and cabinetmaking, the company supports both private and professional clients in a wide range of projects, from interior fit-outs to the creation of unique furniture.

The workshop's expertise covers all types of woodwork, including stairs with curved handrails, entrance doors, windows, and paneling. Custom layout solutions, including the creation of dressings and kitchens, optimize living or working spaces while meeting the aesthetic and functional requirements of every client.

The company's approach combines creativity, precision, and high-quality materials to guarantee durable and aesthetic results. Every project is meticulously studied from design to installation, ensuring a result perfectly tailored to the client's needs and style.

Core Activities

  • Bespoke Manufacturing: Furniture, dressings, bookcases, doors, stairs (straight, curved, winded), paneling, parquet flooring, closets, gates, shutters
  • Interior Layout: Design of kitchens and bathrooms, as well as shop or restaurant fittings
  • Heritage Restoration: Conservation and renovation of period woodwork
  • Aluminum Joinery: Manufacturing and installation of aluminum structures

Driven by an experienced and passionate team, Le Bâtiment Menuisier prioritizes active listening and personalized advice to turn ideas into concrete, harmonious realizations.

Whether for interior renovation, modernizing professional spaces, or creating custom furniture, the company provides expertise and rigor. Le Bâtiment Menuisier is more than just a company; it is a trusted partner for all wood-based projects, where quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of every achievement.
