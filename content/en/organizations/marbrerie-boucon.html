The Boucon house is originally 5 generations of builders, 5 generations of transmission of our ancestral know-how and quest for excellence

Activities:

We put more than 175 years of expertise into all types of character projects that share the values of the house.

From the extraction of the building stone, in search of the most beautiful, Marbles, Granits, Quartzites worldwide.

We then transform these natural rocks to sublimate them in fine decoration marblery, carved ornamental art, heritage renovation and construction projects.
