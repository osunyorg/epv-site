A national meeting to structure the future of know-how

On September 30, 2025, on the occasion of the 20th
EPV label, the Burgundy-Franche-Comté region welcomes a day
dedicated to the introduction of regional channels of excellence.
This event is part of the dynamic of the Network
Excellence, in connection with the Ministries of Economy and Culture,
consular chambers, communities and businesses labeled as EPVs. He
aims to strengthen economic cooperation between EPVs and related sectors,
value the territorial impact of know-how and to co-build sheets
shared roads.
Organized in Tonnerre, a pilot city committed to
the revival by the crafts, this meeting alternates plenary
inspiring, collaborative workshops and concrete meetings
to identify action levers at 24 months. It is addressed to the EPVs of all
the regions, enterprises in connection with the sectors represented,
economic decision makers and prescribers.

Why register?

Because you're a company manager
a committed player in your territory, or simply convinced that on
local know-how is a strategic lever for tomorrow's economy .
On September 30, in Tonnerre, we gather on
living forces of the network of Enterprises of Living Heritage, sectors
of excellence, public decision makers, prescribers and territories
one day immersive, collaborative and inspiring .
On the occasion of 20 years of EPV label ,
event is the national meeting Excellence Network for:

Structure new
local sectors high potential

Radiate VTEs to
national and international levels

Valorizing triple models
impact  : economic, social and environmental

Creating cooperation
concrete , in an uncertain but alliance-bearing context


A day not to be missed! Continuous highlights:
Strategic Plenarys, Leadership Evidence, Peer Discussions
Collective intelligence workshops to build your lines at 24 months
EPV graduation ceremony of Burgundy-Franche-Comté (BFC) by the Regional Prefect*
Opening of photo exhibition dedicated to gestures and know-how
Qualified networking, BFC EPVs masterpiece exhibition and final cocktail
Sector focus An iconic place:  Covered Market & Marland Space, Thunder - A city at the heart of recovery
local trades


Take your ticket (free for EPVs Réseau Excellence members - please contact@reseauexcellence.fr if you have not received the code to register). *The EPV graduation ceremony will take place at 5.30pm and is organised by the Région Bourgogne-Franche-Comté

Prefecture, and does not require registration fees.
