This regional event will bring together the major players of living heritage, offering a unique opportunity to value your know-how, exchange and explore new perspectives. On the morning programme:

Conference - Intervention by a Director of Heritage on the stakes and opportunities of the EPV label

Round table - Sharing experiences and experiences of companies involved in the transmission of know-how

Meetings with start-ups - Discovery of innovations and initiatives related to trades of excellence

Trade Fair - An area dedicated to exchanges between labeled companies, institutions and labeling players.
Whether you're already labeled as an EVP, in the process of labelling or reflecting on this approach, this meeting will provide an opportunity to deepen your knowledge, develop your network and contribute to the spread of living heritage. Come many!
