Next graduation ceremony for the label "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" will take place on Monday 15 September 2025 at 6 p.m. At the Prefectural Hotel Place du Petit Broglie 67000 STRASBOURG in the presence of Monsieur Jacques WITKOWSKI, Prefect of the Greater East Region, and Olivier AUDEBERT, Manager of the SGS certifications and labels Certification.
