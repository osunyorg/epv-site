body: >-

Join us at Auberive Abbey to celebrate French excellence together.

In this exceptional setting nestled in the heart of a wooded park, the Abbey of Auberive unfolds all the strength of its history and the beauty of its architecture. Between centuries-old walls and vast spaces bathed in light, this unique place offers a prestigious and inspiring setting to enhance the know-how of excellence. Exhibiting at the Abbey is a unique setting in which every detail sublimes the authenticity and richness of your participating houses, which will be fully represented. The know-how here finds a framework for discovery, exchange and transmission. The Abbey of Auberive opens its doors to you to live an experience out of time, within a remarkable place, full of emotion and meaning. To live and discover your exceptional homes is to offer the public an unforgettable experience and value your know-how.

Registration before 1 September 2025
