Why set up philanthropy with  de l'or dans les mains  ?

body: >-


We invited the association 

de l'or dans les mains    to present his program "I discover manual trades". This sponsorship of competence is part of a CSR approach and enables employees to be valued, the image of their company and to inspire vocations among college students through a simple mechanism to be set up. We will be present:
President of De l'or dans les mains, Nicolas Marvelleux, also very active in the Reseau Excellence Ile-de-France, and leader of the EPV Rémy Garnier
Gabrielle Légeret, Director General of the Association, and Philippine Janssens, Director of Development
d'Édouard Dumas, CEO of the EPV La Compagnie Dumas and President of Réseau Excellence Bourgogne-Franche-Comté
Tristan De Witte, President of the RIVALEN Group and the EPV Roger Pradier, and President of Réseau Excellence.
