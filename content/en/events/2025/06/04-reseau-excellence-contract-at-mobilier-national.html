The next Dinner of Excellence will be held at the Mobilier national. On this occasion, a partnership will be signed between Réseau Excellence EPV  and Manufactures nationales - Sèvres & Mobilier national.

body: >-


This event is in line with the dinners of excellence initiated by the EPV Réseau Excellence EPV  Contract. Its aim is to have companies labeled "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" dialogue with major cultural institutions, architects, designers, schools, and more broadly with all actors involved in the transmission of exceptional gestures. This evening was made possible thanks to the competition of Fauchon Hospitality, Bollinger, and FORMAE, faithful partners who share our desire to make live and shine the art of living to French. By bringing together architects, ambassadors and defenders of living heritage, this dinner helps strengthen the links between the worlds of creation, transmission and memory, to make excellence a lever for the future. On the occasion of this exceptional dinner, a partnership agreement will be signed between the EPV Excellence Network and the National Manufactures - Sèvres & Mobilier National, affirming a common commitment to the promotion of crafts and the outreach of French know-how.
