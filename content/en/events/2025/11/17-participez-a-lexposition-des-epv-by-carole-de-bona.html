At the  January 2026 edition  Room  Maison&Object , Réseau  Excellence Contract  will deploy an unprecedented space of 400 m2, entirely dedicated to  expertise of excellence  applied to contract, hospitality and high-end interior design. Title  " Manufactures of Excellence",  this space will welcomed  20 companies labeled EPV , selected for the quality of their approach, the boldness of their vision and their ability to respond to the most demanding projects. The  curation  space is entrusted to  Carole de Bon a, founder of  Bel Ouvrage , which will deploy a sensitive and contemporary scenography, like the new expectations of the prescribers. An exceptional showcase to affirm the strategic role of  Heritage Living Compagnies in major development projects today and tomorrow. We would be delighted to include you in this exhibition.

How to participate?

If you want to integrate this exceptional presentation, just contact directly  Carole De Bona  by presenting the  Exception room  that you would like to exhibit: 06 10 11 16 86 carole2bona@gmail.com Cost of participation : 1 500 € tax excl. We strongly hope that you will be able to take part in this collective highlight, thought to celebrate the excellence, singularity and innovation of the Heritage Living companies.
