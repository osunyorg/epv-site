The Nouvelle-Aquitaine Region and the Conseil Régional du Tourisme de la Nouvelle-Aquitaine, in collaboration with the consular networks CCI and CMA Nouvelle-Aquitaine, Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine and the association Entreprise et Découverte launch a brand new regional event dedicated to the business visit:  Here our know-how, a week to visit New Aquitaine companies. From October 22 to 28, 2025, companies from Nouvelle-Aquitaine will open their doors to make the general public discover the diversity and richness of their know-how. A great opportunity to value those who shape the identity of our territory. New Aquitaine companies wishing to participate, can register for the event  here (before 14 September 2025)
