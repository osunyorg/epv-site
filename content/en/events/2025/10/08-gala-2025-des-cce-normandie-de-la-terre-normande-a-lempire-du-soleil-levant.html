After success of the United States Gala 2024 for the 80th anniversary of the Landing, the Councillors of Foreign Trade of Normandy and the club Dynamic Export have the pleasure of inviting you to a new evening on October 8, 2025 at the Giverny Impressionist Museum

body: >-


This edition will highlight Japan as a central player
and economic relations, only a few months after
our mentoring mission in Tokyo, Kyoto and Osaka.
This unique event will take place in two highlights:
Return and exchanges: testimonies, returns
and discussions on economic, cultural and
between Normandy and Japan.
Japanese-inspired thematic dinner, conducive to
meet, share and discover the Japanese art of living.
We will have the privilege of welcoming Hervé Morin,
President of the Normandy Region, and His Excellency the Ambassador of the
Japan in France

The members of ARSEN are warmly
invited to participate in this evening.
