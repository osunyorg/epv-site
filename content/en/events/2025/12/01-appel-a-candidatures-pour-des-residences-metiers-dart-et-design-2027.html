Villa Albertine renews its call for applications for exploration residences specifically dedicated to crafts and design in the United States in 2027.

body: >-


With the support of the Bettencourt Schueller Foundation, Villa Albertine renews its call for applications for exploration residences specifically dedicated to craft and design in the United States in 2027. As part of a New York-based residence, the resident's accompaniment will be carried by WantedDesign. Between January and December 2027, for a period of between one and three months, selected artisans and designers and artisans will benefit from a residence allowing to renew their reflections on their practice, to draw inspiration from the American territory of their choice, to value their work and to weave enriching links. This call is open to professionals in the arts and design sector whose project requires immersion in the United States. The ability to speak in current English is also required. Applications are open until 29 January 2026 link.
