Artisans in France: ready to present your know-how on the New York scene?

Apply for the immersion grant Yes Design organized by Villa Albertine! The immersion scholarship Yes Design! 2026 supports French or French-based artisans wishing to develop their activity in the New York interior decoration market. The program includes ten days of professional immersion in New York during the NYCxDESIGN Festival, ICFF and TEFAF in May 2026, offering participants the opportunity to discover the local ecosystem and identify new opportunities. This year, the interior architect Edgar Jayet will accompany the cohort as a mentor, sharing his advice and experience in the American market. Apply before January 15, 2026 Here This program is in partnership with the Bettencourt Schuler Foundation and the National Manufactures – Sèvres & National Furniture
