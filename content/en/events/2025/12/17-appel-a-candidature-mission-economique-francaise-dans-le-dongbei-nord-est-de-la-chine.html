With the support and coordination of the Consulate General of France in Shenyang, the CPME and CPME 93 organise a exceptional economic mission in Dongbei, strategic region of north-eastern China, January 23 to February 2, 2026.

body: >-


This mission is aimed at French companies of all sizes and sectors , wishing to explore a market with high potential, particularly receptive to French know-how and products cultural identity (regional products, handicrafts, art de vivre, emblematic brands of France).
To the programme:

Masterclass e-commerce to understand the Chinese market


Targeted B2B appointment with local partners

Meetings with authorities and professional organisations


French market in Changchun to enhance your products

Cultural highlights: Changbai Mountain , Harbin Ice Festival , networking evenings and gala

Business opportunities are varied and accessible, including for niche companies.

all informations here
https://www.shenyang2024.org/

Applications opened now
