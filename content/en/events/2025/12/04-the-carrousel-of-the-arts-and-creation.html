A special edition to celebrate 100 years of the Chambers of Crafts and Crafts
In the framework of these activities for the promotion and economic development of the craft sector, the CMA Ile-de-France will organise the "Carrousel des Métiers d'Art et de Création" from 4 to 7 December 2025 in an emblematic place in the heart of Paris, the  Carreau du Temple . This old market covered with industrial architecture of the late nineteenth century hosts 230 cultural events per year. A selection focused on creative and contemporary crafts will bring together unique pieces or small series shaped in a workshop locally, for reasoned and durable purchases of original and refined objects: 140 exhibitors are expected.


Artisan / EPV ? Join the 2025 selection and exhibit your creations in the Carreau du Temple, in the heart of Paris! What awaits you: - 3.5 days of exposure-sale against an informed audience - 140 stands available, neat scenography - Exceptional visibility thanks to a wide communication campaign - An essential appointment to enhance your unique know-how To apply:
Perform a recognized art profession
Create a workshop: single or small series pieces or services
Present a quality stand and professional visuals All information and space "candidatures" are accessible here
