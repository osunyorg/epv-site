Réseau Excellence EPV Contract Annual Closing Evening 2025
Tristan De Witte, President of the Réseau Excellence, and Tristan Rodriguez, President of the Réseau Excellence Contract, are pleased to announce the holding of the EPV Contract Excellence Network's 2025 Annual Closing Evening . This inescapable event will bring together the players of the 11 December 2025 , from 18.30 , within the prestigious framework of the Hotel of Industry located 4 place Saint-Germain-des-Prés in Paris.
This evening will be an opportunity to revisit the highlights of the past year, to discuss together the balance sheet 2025 , but also to present the outlook 2026 for the Excellence Contract Network. A privileged moment of encounter, exchanges and conviviality bringing together the companies of the Heritage Vivant engaged in the contract sector and its ambassadors. This event is accessible by ticket, the link below.
