International reference event, the Milan Salone del Mobile constitutes Each year one of the major events of contemporary design. True platform for exchange, innovation and global visibility, it brings together creators, publishers, institutions and emerging talent. Participate in this event offers a unique opportunity to register at the heart of the ecosystem and to expose his work to an audience of professionals and international prescribers.

As part of its participation in the 2026 edition, Manufactures National associate with Campus Fashion, Crafts & Design (MoMADe) and the Réseau Excellence Contract -Enterprises of the Heritage Living (EPV) around a call for projects to select pieces produced by companies for presentation in the space chosen by the National manufactures.

These pieces will accompany the presentation of the latest editions of the National manufactures. Once the pieces are selected, students of School Blue and Camondo School will work on a scenography with the accompaniment of their teachers, Manufactures and VTEs. The winning project, selected by a jury will meet in mid-February 2026, will be realized and implemented within the framework of the Salone del Mobile.

The call for projects

The call
to projects is intended for enterprises of the Réseau  Excellence having obtained the
label « Enterprise of Living Heritage  (EPV).
The
companies wishing to apply must respect the
the following conditions:
Propose
a completed and manufactured part. Furniture that is the subject of
This application must not be at the project stage. The aesthetic choices
must be definitive, including in terms of materials and finishes. Thus,
applications for unsuccessful documents will not be accepted.
Propose
one room available during the duration of the event ( early April - end
April ).
Propose
a recent exhibit, if possible, that has never been presented at the Salone del
Mobile Milan.
Present
3 different rooms maximum.

logistics and return transportation costs of their piece(s) .
The winning companies will be linked to discuss
the opportunity to consider mutualized transport.

International reference event, the Milan Salone del Mobile constitutes Each year one of the major events of contemporary design. True platform for exchange, innovation and global visibility, it brings together creators, publishers, institutions and emerging talent. Participate in this event offers a unique opportunity to register at the heart of the ecosystem and to expose his work to an audience of professionals and international prescribers.

As part of its participation in the 2026 edition, Manufactures National associate with Campus Fashion, Crafts & Design (MoMADe) and the Réseau Excellence Contract -Enterprises of the Heritage Living (EPV) around a call for projects to select pieces produced by companies for presentation in the space chosen by the National manufactures.

These pieces will accompany the presentation of the latest editions of the National manufactures. Once the pieces are selected, students of School Blue and Camondo School will work on a scenography with the accompaniment of their teachers, Manufactures and VTEs. The winning project, selected by a jury will meet in mid-February 2026, will be realized and implemented within the framework of the Salone del Mobile.

The call to projects is intended for enterprises of the Réseau Excellence having obtained the label «Enterprise of Living Heritage (EPV).

The companies wishing to apply must respect the the following conditions:

  • Propose a completed and manufactured part. Furniture that is the subject of This application must not be at the project stage. The aesthetic choices must be definitive, including in terms of materials and finishes. Thus, applications for unsuccessful documents will not be accepted.
  • Propose one room available during the duration of the event (early April - end April).
  • Propose a recent exhibit, if possible, that has never been presented at the Salone del Mobile Milan.
  • Present 3 different rooms maximum.
  • He is understood that the companies will have to take over the logistics and return transportation costs of their piece(s). The winning companies will be linked to discuss the opportunity to consider mutualized transport.

The expected typologies are:

  • Luminaires (table lamp, stage lighting, floor lamp, chandelier, suspension etc.)
  • Hangers and clocks
  • Mirrors
  • Exhibit mobilising (seats, healers, etc.) of small dimensions, little bulky
  • Sculptures, mobile
  • Vases, table arts
  • Original lithographs etc.
  • Objects Small decorative (objects of curiosity)
  • Parts Art crafts: parts of blown glass, art marquetry, works of art metal etc.
