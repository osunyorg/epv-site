During SEPEM Toulouse 2026, which will be held on September 22, 23 & 24, 2026 in Toulouse, the "Flagships of Industry" exhibition will highlight the region's industrial excellence.

We invite Living Heritage Companies (EPV) located in Occitanie to submit their applications to participate in this collective showcase dedicated to the region's expertise.

body: >-

About SEPEM Toulouse 2026

SEPEM Toulouse is the trade show for services, equipment, and processes for industry, dedicated to manufacturers in the region. For three days, it brings together decision-makers, maintenance managers, production managers, methods engineers, and industrial directors to discover concrete solutions for their production sites.

SEPEM Toulouse Website 2026
https://toulouse.sepem-industries.com/

"Les Fleurons de L'industrie" Exhibition

WHITE MIRROR


Designed as a scenographic exhibition, "Flagships of Industry" will showcase emblematic finished products from the region's industrial fabric. ⚠️This is neither a pavilion nor a collective exhibition space, but rather a non-commercial exhibition highlighting regional industrial excellence. Selected companies do not have a booth and are not expected to be continuously present in the exhibition space. The objective: to offer a collective showcase of Occitanie's industrial expertise, with a focus on regional development.

Scope and Participation Guidelines

The exhibition is open to industrial companies located in Occitanie. • It will not be exclusively composed of companies with the EPV (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) label. • Companies holding the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company) label will benefit from specific promotion, with their labeling mentioned within the exhibition. • Participation is entirely free • A finished product will be provided for the duration of the event • Exhibition in a non-commercial, scenographically designed space • Invitation to the opening visit, in the presence of institutional and political representatives from the region

Application Submission

SEPEM Toulouse 2026 - Application Form
https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=wBMATUtnBEqg2zrA4P8w7juiLe503dJFrcxSsNYpQTRUNzhQSDdBNkw1Ulg4UjNUUk9FRThURlpaMy4u&route=shorturl


Companies wishing to participate are invited to complete the registration form. ⚠️ Application deadline: July 3 at 11:45 PM. No registrations will be accepted after this date. We are counting on the mobilization of the Living Heritage Companies (EPV) of Occitanie to collectively showcase the industrial excellence of our region at SEPEM Toulouse 2026. For any further information, please contact: contact@reseauexcellence.fr
