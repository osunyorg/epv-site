Live from Paris, online:

During this meeting, we will present the second edition of the Village Manufacture d'Excellence in partnership with the Excellence Network Contract EPV and House & Object for their January 2027 edition (14-18).

Including:

The integration of a Village Manufactures of Excellence EPV of 400 m2, designed to enhance exceptional know-how, will showcase space, real staging of an exceptional interior, co-directed with an architect and partners EPV (arrangement, cabinetmaking & furniture, luminaires, floor coverings & walls, table arts...).

Various forms of participation are adapted to the ambitions and budgets of each

A VIP event on the margins of the show to create connections between EPV and International project leaders

This January 2027 edition will resolutely focus on the universe Contract & Hospitality, with a strong will to bring to Paris architects, hoteliers, designers and prescribers from around the world.

This is a structural opportunity in the deployment of our network and in the radiation of the EPV label.

We urge you to participate in this meeting: ideally as a leader, or by designating a member of your team to represent you. We are expecting many of you to build together this new stage of collective dynamics.
