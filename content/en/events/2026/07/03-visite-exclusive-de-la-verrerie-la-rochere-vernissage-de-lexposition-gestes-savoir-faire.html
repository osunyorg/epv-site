Réseau Excellence EPV of Burgundy-Franche-Comté has the pleasure of inviting its members to a shared moment of discovery and conviviality on Friday, July 3, 2026, from 4 p.m., at Glassware La Rochère, an exceptional site in operation without interruption since 1475.

body: >-

The EPVs of Bourgogne-Franche-Comté meet at La Rochère for an immersion in the heart of glassmaking know-how!

An exclusive visit to La Rochère , exceptionally reserved for network members. A rare opportunity to discover the workshops of this iconic factory, to witness a demonstration of glass blowing in the mouth, to exchange with glassmakers and to browse the shop and its contemporary creations.

Programme:

This appointment will take place in two stages: 1. An exclusive visit to La Rochère, reserved exceptionally for members of the network. For those who have not yet discovered this emblematic place, it will be a rare opportunity to enter the workshops, attend a demonstration of glass blowing in the mouth, meet the glassmakers, and then discover the shop and its contemporary creations. 2. The opening of the exhibition "Gestes & Savoir-faire" , from 18:00, which will highlight the excellence of the know-how of our companies, followed by a closing cocktail.


This visit is reserved for  Members , we invite you to confirm your presence at: arepvbfc@gmail.com specifying the number of participants.

La Rochère
