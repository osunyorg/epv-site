On 7 October 2026, the Enterprise Directorate-General organise a national event dedicated to innovation in crafts. The craft companies present will discover innovative solutions adapted to the realities of their trades. This call for expressions of interest therefore aims to select some 20 start-ups, TPEs, SMEs, ETIs, public institutions or associations offering innovative solutions for craft enterprises.

The selected structures will benefit from an exhibition booth and visibility among institutional and professional players in the sector.
