After a first edition organized in 2025 on the occasion of the centenary of the International Exhibition of Decorative Arts, the National Manufactures launched a call for applications to participate in the Salon des Nouveaux Enembliers 2027.

body: >-

Background and Event Presentation

After the success of its first edition in 2025, the new grand rendez-vous of the Mobilier National dedicated to interior architecture and contemporary creation is back in autumn. Designers, interior architects and decorators are invited to apply now to extend the reflection and reinvent the spaces of " the embassy of tomorrow " The first edition in 2025 confirmed public and professional interest in this approach. In twenty days of opening, 21,000 visitors came to discover ten decorative sets made especially for the event with the help of 138 companies from the arts and design ecosystem. Several projects continued outside France, including Hong Kong, Canton (China) and Doha (Qatar). For this new edition, the National Manufactures invite a new generation of creators to explore the evolution of lifestyles, the hybridization of know-how, ecodesign, sustainability and new expressions of decorative arts, in order to offer a renewed vision of 21st century spaces of representation. The call for applications is open to interior architects, designers, decorators and, more broadly, to all creators and creators developing a global approach to space, either individually or collectively. Following a two-phase selection, ten teams will be selected to design a real-scale decorative set presented at the Salon in the fall of 2027.


Applications are open from now until September 14, 2026. Presentation documents and the form are available on the Mobilier National website.
