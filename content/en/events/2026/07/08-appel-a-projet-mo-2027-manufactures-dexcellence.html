The Réseau Excellence Contract of the EPVs and Maison&Objet are returning the Village Manufactures of Excellence : a 400 m2 collective space dedicated to the Ateliers & Manufactures of exception, entirely oriented towards the universe of high decoration and hospitality.

After a successful first edition in 2026, this new appointment is resolutely part of the universe Contract & Hospitality, with the ambition to bring together in Paris architects, hoteliers, designers and international prescribers around French know-how.

body: >-

Description of the Offer

A collective village of 400 m2 valuing the exceptional know-how of EPV companies
A show-case space, staging an exceptional interior co-directed with an architect and partners EPV (arrangement, cabinetmaking & furniture, luminaires, coatings, table arts...)
Various forms of participation adapted to the ambitions and budgets of each company
A VIP event on the sidelines of the show, dedicated to connecting EPV with international project leaders
This new edition represents a structuring opportunity for the outreach of companies labeled EPV and the international development of know-how of excellence.
