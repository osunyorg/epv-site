From Friday 2 to Monday 5 October 2026, Premiere Classe, the must-see event of accessories designers and ready-to-wear, reinvested the Jardin des Tuileries with new energy.

On the programme: new universes like Jill and "such a boutique!" join Shoppe Object Paris, Brut Icon, and the showroom RUN x ANDAM, to redefine the boundaries of contemporary creation.

body: >-

A new edition for the development of EPVs

With nearly 300 brands gathered at the Jardin des Tuileries, Premiere Classe offers an international panorama of contemporary creation. Among the new houses and the most singular signatures of this edition, some are distinguished by their know-how, their artisanal approach or their committed vision of creation. The French state label "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) recognizes some of these companies to implement within their structure this courageous strategy and demands the mastery of these complex know-how and excellence. Lifestyle, Fashion, Childhood, these EPVs are everywhere and Premiere Classe wants to salute their commitment with this FOCUS EPV featuring 12 labelized companies, combining fashion & know-how. If you would like to join this FOCUS EPV space, please request participation by following the link below, specifying "FOCUS EPV" in the "Specific Request" section.

Premiere Classe : Curating originality, celebrating creativity

Premiere Classe is the international reference event dedicated to fashion accessories designers. Organised twice a year during the Fashion Week Femme de Paris, Premiere Classe has been celebrating the creative richness and singularity of accessories since 1989 through a unique, inspiring and resolutely new experience. Designed as an immersive setting, Premiere Classe promotes connections between professionals through a careful staging of 250 creative artists, both emerging and iconic, and an exclusive program combining talks, exhibitions and artistic installations. For four days, Premiere Classe becomes the must-see event where style and business meet, creative talents and international buyers, to shape together the accessory of tomorrow.
