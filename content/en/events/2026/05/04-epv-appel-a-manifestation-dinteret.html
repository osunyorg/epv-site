Within the framework of the national strategy for the arts and crafts, led jointly by the Ministry of Economy and the Ministry of Culture, the Directorate-General for Enterprise (DGE) is launching a call for expressions of interest dedicated to the development of the French art de vivre sector, particularly in the international arena.

body: >-


Recognized throughout the world, France has major advantages in this area: design, architecture, furnishing, table arts and decoration. The aim of this call is to identify and value French companies wishing to expand their business to new markets and customers, particularly internationally.
The selected companies will be included in a reference catalogue, intended to be distributed to prescribers and originators. Deadline for applications: 4 June 2026
