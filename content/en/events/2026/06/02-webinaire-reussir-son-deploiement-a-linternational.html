The Institut pour les Savoir-Faire Français, with the support of CIC, gives you an appointment for a new Webinar Let's Talk Savoir-Faire.

This webinar will give you concrete insight into the essential steps of an export development. From defining the strategy to financing tools, market analysis, structuring the approach and identifying the right partners, stakeholders will share methods, practical advice and vigilance points to secure their export project.

body: >-

Speakers for this webinar:

Luc Lesénécal, President of the French Institute for Traditional Know-How and President of Saint James


Marie-France Vannier, Head of International Activities - CIC Nord Ouest


Frédéric Girard, Vice-President of the Business Support Commission, President of the Mentorship Expertise Group, Secretary of the Advisory Commission to Public Authorities, and French Foreign Trade Advisor


Mohéli Becquemont, Key Accounts and Partnerships Manager, Crafts Industry Specialist, Export Department - Key Accounts and Sector Partnerships, Business France


Jean-Baptiste Drachkovitch, Director of Langlois-Martin, the last remaining manufacturer of glitter in France.
