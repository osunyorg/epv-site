Join the EPV dynamic at SIRHA 2027

The Réseau Excellence EPV is gearing up for SIRHA Lyon 2027 with two complementary spaces designed to showcase the craftsmanship of Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Companies) to professionals in the gastronomy, hospitality, design, and interior fit-out sectors. A reception area within the Sustainable Cuisine Foundation (Fondation pour la Cuisine Durable) Led by Marie-Odile Fondeur —President of the Foundation and ambassador for the Réseau Excellence EPV—this premium space will host chefs, officials, ambassadors, and partners for key networking events and showcases of exceptional craftsmanship. A space at the heart of the Hospitality & Factory zone Developed in partnership with GL events , this new zone in Hall 7 is dedicated to the art of hospitality, interior design, and excellence in craftsmanship. It will bring together key players from the tableware, gastronomy equipment, and hospitality sectors.

Offer Presentation to EPVs

By joining this initiative, you become part of a collective of Living Heritage Companies (EPV) , benefiting from enhanced visibility, valuable networking opportunities, and a shared programme at the world's leading trade show for the foodservice, hospitality and hospitality equipment industries. Three complementary participation formats are available, allowing EPV companies to choose the option that best matches their business activity and objectives. 1. EPV Trail - Throughout the exhibition - for EPV companies exhibiting at the show. 2. EPV Inspiration Village - Hall 7 - Hospitality Factory area - for EPV companies in the tableware, equipment and contract sectors, featuring:
A curated collective showroom (12 m²)
Turnkey Hub Inspiration individual stands (6 m²), exclusively reserved for first-time exhibitors (12 spaces available)
3. VIP Partners Village - Hall 6 - Foundation for Sustainable Cuisine area (application required)Each participation format is designed to meet different needs. The table below will help you identify the option that best suits your business.  


The financial contributions collected through these registrations will be used exclusively to support t he Réseau Excellence EPV Gastronomie's presence at SIRHA Lyon 2027 and to further the initiatives led by the Réseau Excellence EPV Gastronomie . Be among the first EPV-certified companies to join this unprecedented collective initiative at SIRHA Lyon 2027. Increase your visibility, create new business opportunities, and showcase your expertise as part of a community of excellence . As several participation options are available in limited numbers, registrations will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
