On June 25, Réseau Excellence will participate in The Grand Assembly of Entrepreneurs, a major event in the French economy that will bring together more than 4,000 leaders at Parc des Princes.

body: >-

Participate in La Grande Assemblée des Entrepreneurs alongside the Alliance du Manufacture in France

On this occasion, we will be present alongside the Alliance du Fabriqué en France (Made in France Alliance), a collective committed to promoting French expertise and developing a strong and innovative industry, of which the national CPME (Confederation of Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises) is also a member. This day of discussions and reflection will highlight the major challenges shaping the future of our businesses and our economy: - Artificial intelligence, - Business financing, - Made in France, - Reindustrialization, - The various forms of economic and industrial sovereignty. Entrepreneurs, SME and mid-sized company leaders, policymakers, and experts will share their experiences and visions to build the solutions of tomorrow together. Among the expected participants are Arnaud Montebourg, Vincent Luciani, Sophie Lacoste, Thomas Huriez, Jonathan Cherki, Olivier Lluansi, Natacha Polony, Philippe Aghion, Sylvain Tesson, and Stéphane Séjourné.

For Réseau Excellence, this participation reinforces its mission to accompany, promote and spread French know-how

For Réseau Excellence, this participation is fully part of its mission: to support entrepreneurs, to promote the excellence of French know-how and to contribute to the influence of an innovative, responsible and sovereign economy. We invite all our members and partners to join this essential event and to share with us this collective dynamic at the service of French companies.


Join us and participate in this must-see French entrepreneurship event!
