Les Rencontres du Made in France - 11 June 2026 By Sacred French

On June 11, 2026, live the 3rd edition of the Rencontres du Made in France à la Friche la Belle de Mai - Marseille. A national event to inspire, meet and act: 4 conferences on the challenges of Made In France, a village of companies bringing together 40 exhibitors, innovation, exchanges and collaborations around the major issues of Made in France.
