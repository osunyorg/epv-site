Business France invites you to meet international buyers in search of exceptional know-how, during the various stages of theTour Professions d'Art 2026.

The Tour Métiers d'Art 2026 will take place onthree stages in France: toLyon 16 June,Paris on 17 JuneandBordeaux on 18 June.

body: >-

Diarys B2B

The Tour Professions d'Art 2026 will take place over three stages in France, offering a proximity to local ecosystems and a variety of meetings with professionals from the sector specialized in high-end projects:
to  Lyon 16 June ,
to  Paris on 17 June and
to  Bordeaux on 18 June .
On this occasion, Business France offices abroad will identify and invite up to 10 international buyers/prescriptors (architects, interior designers, top-of-the-range distributors), selected for their interest in French crafts and excellence. Prior to the event, a matchmaking platform will be put online to allow you to organize your appointments and optimize your participation. You will have the opportunity to meet these buyers during the various stages of the Tour, in dedicated spaces or directly on site.

Why participate?

A privileged opportunity to meet qualified international buyers, especially mobilized to discover French know-how
SEO on a networking platform to initiate and develop international business contacts

Possible grants : Plan Métiers d'Art and/or EPV

Companies eligible for the Plan Métiers d'Art and/or EPV can receive a contribution of up to 50% of the amount taxed, up to a payment of €3,666.
This aid is subject to the de minimis Regulation and can be combined with regional aid and France 2030.
Support from Franc blûr for the watchmaking, jewellery and table arts sectors.

Members paying the assigned and up-to-date Tax on their admission to the Groupement d'Exporteurs de Meubles (GEM) are eligible for a 50% subsidy linked to the CODIFAB Convention.
