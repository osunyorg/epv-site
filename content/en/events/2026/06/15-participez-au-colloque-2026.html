The Confédération des Arts de la Table, with the support of Franc brilliance, invites retailers, manufacturers, distributors and professional organizations to its annual conference. A day of exchange and reflection, punctuated with conferences, a round table and interventions to better understand market developments.

body: >-

The market does not change at the margin, it changes logic

The day will come
around several highlights:


Understanding of new
economic power relations
Industrial transformation and
competitiveness issues
Changes in
consumption
New
distribution, including live shopping
Visibility in the engine age
and artificial intelligence assistants
The role of marketplaces in
trade balances
Safety and security issues
confidence in purchasing routes
Beyond the analyses,
several interventions will highlight concrete examples
companies that are already adapting. The aim is not to be
comprehensive.
The aim is to deal with
topics that will have a real impact in the coming years.
