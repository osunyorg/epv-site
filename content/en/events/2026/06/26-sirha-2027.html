Discover the first collective mobilisation of the EPV Gastronomy & Hospitality

Friday 26 June 2026 between 9.30am and 10.30am

Sign up for this webinar
https://app.livestorm.co/chambre-de-metiers-et-de-l-handicrafts-auvergne-rhone-alpes/les-epv-preparent-leur-rdv-au-sirha-2027


Speakers:

Tristan DE WITTE  Président  Réseau Excellence ­

Carole DOIRE Maison Giraudet et déléguée Réseau Excellence Gastronomie

Marie-Odile FONDEUR Présidente de la Fondation pour la cuisine durable

François THIERY Directeur Commercial  GL Events ­

Philippe MOREAU Animateur du Réseau Excellence Gastronomie­

What you will discover during this webinar

Why the Excellence Network Today Develops Vertical Trades And how the Contract sector has become a model of collective mobilisation for the EVPs. Tristan De Witte - President of the Excellence Network The birth of vertical Gastronomy and its first national collective action Why the Sirha 2027 is a unique opportunity to unite the expertise of excellence around a common project. Carole Doire - Excellence Gastronomy Network The support of the great ambassadors of French gastronomy How the Fondation Cuisine Durable d'Olivier Ginon is working alongside the EPVs to bring French know-how to life. Marie-Odile Founder - President of the Fondation Cuisine Durable VTEs at the heart of Sirha 2027's strategic framework Hospitality Factory, EPV course, visibility, speaking, networking: discover the concrete opportunities offered by GL Events. François Thiery - Sales Director GL Events & Milica Savic Head of GL Events Affairs And tomorrow? The next steps in the collective mobilization and major meetings of the Excellence Network, including the national gathering of VTEs scheduled for the end of the year. Philippe Moreau Chevrolet - Moderator of the Excellence Gastronomy Network


With the participation of:

Do you wish to receive information in real time?

The  Gastronomy sector or Réseau Excellence has created a WhatsApp group dedicated to the members of the Network Excellence Gastronomy, table art and equipment manufacturers to enhance communication, keeping you informed in real time about targeted actions of your sector.

Join the group WhatsApp Gastronomy
https://chat.whatsapp.com/IRtjnZrnprzBhODzzZfFy7

Join EPV Excellence Network

To join the Network, you must join the regional association from which your company originates. Of course, your membership gives you the right to all our actions throughout the country! The annual membership amount is calculated based on your turnover.
