Assemblée Générale de Réseau Excellence Nouvelle Aquitaine et ceremoniale de labeles Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant aux entreprises labeled on the first half of 2026 at the CCI de Limoges Haute-Vienne in Limoges on Monday 22 June 2026 afternoon. Participation only on invitation.

body: >-


The Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine, Association régional des Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) de la Nouvelle-Aquitaine,and its partners: the New Aquitaine Region, consular networks CCI and CMA Nouvelle-Aquitaine, and the New Aquitaine Region Prefecture, have the pleasure of inviting all New Aquitaine companies holding the EPV label, or in the process of renewal, to:

General Assembly of the Network Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine

Date: Monday, June 22, 2025 From 3pm Location: CCI de Limoges Haute-Vienne, 16 place Jourdan 87011 Limoges, Panoramic Room (7 nd floor) Agenda:
Moral report - vote
Financial report - vote
Action plan and budget
Governance update: Bureau elected for 3 years - call for applications to join the office; office renewal in 2027
Presentation of the regional operation "Here our know-how, a week to visit the companies of New Aquitaine"

EPV label award ceremony for companies labeled on the first half of 2026

Monday, 22 June 2026 From 6pm In the presence of the representative of the New Aquitaine Region Prefecture Location: CCI de Limoges Haute-Vienne, 16 place Jourdan 87011 Limoges, Panoramic Room (7 nd floor) Access : Gare de Limoges : 15 minutes walk ; Parking nearby


A friendly exchange time will close this meeting. Please reply before 17 June 2026 via the following link :
