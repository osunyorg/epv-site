Speak on the Cook & Share space by La Filière France

The EPV Excellence Network , space partner Cook&Share by La Filière France , invites its member companies to Home & Object - September 2026 propose an intervention within the show's animation program. After the success of the first edition, this collective space returns to the heart of the Hall 4 - Cook & Share to highlight the know-how, innovations and expertise of the Art de vivre sector.

Learn more about the project
https://confederation-des-arts-de-la-table.fr/les-rendez-vous-cookshare-by-la-filiere-france-2026/

Two formats of participation

Share your expertise and value your business gestures through: Conferences & round tables To share a point of view, a feedback or a useful topic for visiting professionals: trends, CSR, regulation, retail, social networks, transmission, know-how, etc.

Propose a Conference topic
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfO8MU6NBuxSoC6PNl6MeA0NV35BdaEoMze2F6c3n1NSx_Ptw/viewform


Ateliers de démonstration Pour valoriser un usage, un geste métier, une collaboration entre exposants : cuisine, coutellerie, œnologie, mixologie, dressage, arts de la table, verrerie, céramique, etc.Possibilité de collaborer avec  Monbana,  et  Terre Exotique  présent sur M&O

Propose a topic Workshop topic
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe0LJ-tELfEAaWUWcnjn7P-BJymgekI3NkiRJLTZUphHft-aA/viewform


Conditions of participation: - Participation is free - Only for companies exhibiting at Maison & Objet and members of the EPV Excellence Network or partner organisations Please complete the questionnaire(s).
