Description
Principle  : A short format of 3 minutes to highlight the history of your EPV, your exceptional know-how and enhance your visibility, with a perspective of collective project and media dissemination. Webinar facilitated by: Almandine Ibled , Director & Journalist specializing in corporate films Guillaume Fischer , Economic journalist at The Tribune , specialist in Made in France and patrimony Guillaume and Amandine will present their proposal, its concept, its format, rates and visibility opportunities for members of the Réseau Excellence.

Biography of presenters

Guillaume Fischer is an economic journalist
especially for La Tribune and author of several books on brands
French. He is particularly specialized in
promotion of French sovereignty through the promotion of Made in France. He
also regularly intervenes to safeguard and enhance the
heritage, including artisanal and industrial, and the art of living
French. Finally, he is also committed to the issues of
promotion of community trade and markets in
Hexagon.

Réalisation de Guillaume Fischer


Amandine Ibled : Director & Journalist
specialized in corporate films Amandine Ibled is a director and journalist
experienced, founder of IBLED PRODUCTION, a production company
the creation of institutional films, reports and
Captations for companies and organisations . After training
in Journalism and Experiences in Television and Media, Amandine has chosen to
putting his mastery of narrative and image at the service of companies,
providing them with videos that tell their stories, values and
know-how with clarity, emotion and impact . Double expertise
- Journalist ( ability to structure a relevant message)
(from shooting to mounting, including
drone) - allows it to build powerful corporate films that
capture the attention of key audiences : clients, partners,
institutions and the media. What distinguishes Amandine is her approach.
human and creative, combined with a capacity to understand in depth
the identity of a company - essential for EPV labeled brands, which
to develop a unique heritage, exceptional know-how or
craft tradition on all communication media. By entrusting your
video projects in Amandine Ibled, you choose a capable director
to visually translate the soul of your company, strengthen your image of
brand and touch your audiences with authenticity and elegance .
