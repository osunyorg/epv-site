Save the date! The French Craft, a partner association, launches a new meet in 2026 to promote and foster innovation through know-how

body: >-


PROTOTYPE
is a project accelerator allowing strategic meetings
between creators, businesses and investors. Materials,
uses, forms, processes: participate in the avant-garde of Craft the 5 & 6
February 2026 at the Hôtel de l'Industrie in Paris. Réseau Excellence EPV will be
partner of the event, with already beautiful EPVs: Kramer Group, Rinck,
Cainerie 91, Procédés Chénel, Maison Alivon, Daumet... All will put in
light the richness of their legacy at the service of new developments!

Discover the event

https://prototype.frenchcraftguild.fr/


Craftsmen, designers,
manufacturers, researchers or industrialists: join a community
to create the know-how of tomorrow.
