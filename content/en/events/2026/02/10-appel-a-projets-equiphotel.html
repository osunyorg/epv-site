Equiphotel ( 2-5 November 2026 - Paris Porte de Versailles ) , hotel-restaurant reference room, proposes " Exclusive Collection », a new exposure area Multisectoral of nearly 1,000 m2 dedicated to premium and luxury hotels. Exclusive Collection present in his heart a space dedicated to EPV - Enterprises of Living Heritage.

body: >-

Discover the Equip Hotel offer for the EPV

This space is designed in partnership with the network
of excellence of VTEs. A unique opportunity to promote business
French craft and industrial with rare and exceptional know-how
to hotel and restaurant professionals. This space includes:


An exposure area : different
stand formulas, including an innovative panel display offer (from 4,605
€ excluding VAT)
A series of conferences and events
meetings and networking .
Unpublished exhibition « Don't
Disturb, praise the intimate » giving white card to four
creative talents with complementary profiles - Wilmotte & Associates, Maison
A&G, Emilieu Studio and Mathilde de l'Ecotais.

The exhibition will highlight the products and materials of the space exhibitors « EPV@Exclusive Collection »

Included in your participation: Access to the "EH Connect" appointment platform with hoteliers
and hospitality decision makers present at the show
Discover the Business Builder Pack : our tools
digital to generate leads.

Further information:

For more information about Exclusive Collection : juanita.torres-ceron@rxglobal.com
