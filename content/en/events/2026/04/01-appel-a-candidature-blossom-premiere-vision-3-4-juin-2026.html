The Excellence Network, in partnership with Blossom Première Vision is pleased to announce the launch of a dedicated call for applications to companies labeled Enterprises du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV).

body: >-

Blossom Première Vision

Blossom Première Vision is a bi-annual event (June & December) exclusive dedicated to creative fashion brands, luxury, top of
range and with expertise of excellence. Located in the heart of Paris, the salon welcomes for 2 days at the Carreau du
Temple in the heart of the Marais weavers, tanners, accessories manufacturers and
manufacturers all specially selected for quality and creativity
of their products.
A unique opportunity to value your know-how and
launch your collection with more than 1000 international visitors
representing nearly 600 companies such as: AZZEDINE ALAIA, CELINE, CHANEL,
CHRISTIAN DIOR, CHRISTIAN LOUBOUTIN, HERMES, SCHIAPARELLI and many others
Again.

Blossom Première Vision offer for EPV

Print and digital visibility upstream of the show Specific digital communication will be
deployed on our digital tools to showcase our labeled exhibitors
EPV (newsletters, website and social networks)
Presence and visibility in the lounge Recognition of your label via a
dedicated identification on the show

Applications opened now
