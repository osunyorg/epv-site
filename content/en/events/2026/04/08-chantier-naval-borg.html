Each boat has a particular history in it.

Renewing, after long
years, with the construction of wood at the Shipyard Borg and in Marseille, the
that we are about to hand over to its owners is no exception. After months of drawings,
assembly, adjustment, construction and shared passions, our
New Fifty, the first copy of the name, is ready to take off.
Inspired by the iconic
marseillaise trays, it fits into this filiation with a touch of
modern, while fully embodying the know-how of carpenters of
Marine that make the story of our Building.
So we have the
pleasure to invite you to the Outing of Building that will be held the Wednesday
8 April at 12:00 , at the Borg Shipyard, at the Pharo Cove
Marseille.
