We are pleased to share with you the invitation to our next meeting between  Companies labeled EPV of Île-de-France , to be held in a place dedicated to the preservation of the written heritage :  Coralie Barbe Workshop . Specialized in  restoration and preservation of old papers, works and heritage documents  The workshop perpetuates rare know-how for libraries, archives, institutions and collectors. A careful work where technical expertise and artistic sensitivity meet to revive the works of the past.
