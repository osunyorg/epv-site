A national event to promote French excellence

During the previous edition, almost 10,000 visitors had discovered more than 120 products made in France , selected in all metropolitan and ultramarine departments. This national showcase makes it possible to radiate emblematic, innovative or strong territorially based productions.

Apply until May 15, 2026

Applications open 31 March to 15 May 2026 to 11:59 .
All enterprises meeting the "Made in France" can file a file: start-up, TPE, SME, ETI or large groups.
The files will be evaluated according to several criteria:

share of value added achieved in France,

labels and certifications held,

environmental and social commitment,

relocation of the activity,

innovation,

Export strategy.

After a regional pre-selection, one product per department will be selected for exposure to Elysée .

Why participate?

Participating in this exhibition is:

enjoy exceptional national visibility,

value its know-how to the general public,

strengthen its brand image,

represent its territory at the highest level.

How to apply ?
