body: >-

CSR - A subject at the heart of your labelling

Strengthen
and value your CSR actions in your company to anticipate your application
labeling or renewal of your EPV label! The
Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is a fundamental criterion for
obtaining or renewing your EPV label. To accompany you,
we offer 2 complementary webinars to better understand the
CSR issues, both for your labelling approach and for your
business. Discover
Social issues:
team management,
training and integration of
staff,
communication and promotion with your customers and
partners.

Speakers

Eric ANGIBOUST - Vice President of the Excellence Network, President Muzéo
Sylvie BENARD - CSR expert - President of GPEX (EPV Label Group of Experts)
Nathalie BOUTET - Chief Executive Ducaroy Grange
Madeline FARDET - Human Resources Expert CMA AURA
Laurent CAVEROT - Expert CMA AURA environment

Webinar - CSR Social component 27/03 from 11am to 12pm
