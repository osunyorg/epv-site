Application until 25/03/2026

body: >-


Beautiful work Venezia - Il Corpo della Materia is a curatorial exhibition presented from May 4 to July 20, 2026 in the historic Madonna dell'Orto cloister in Venice, on the margins of the Biennale of Art. Scenographed by Studio Andrée Putman, it brings together a narrow selection of exceptional pieces around a central theme: matter as language, gesture and presence.
Applications are open to houses, manufacturers (including EPV) and studios with strong creative identity wishing to register in an institutional, non-commercial curatorial setting, before an international audience of collectors, architects and prescribers.
Deadline: 25 March 2026. Contact: carole2bona@gmail.com


Présentation Bel Ouvrage Venise 2026

Applications before 25/03/2026

To apply, please send your file to Carole de Bona - curator and founder of Bel Ouvrage - accompanied
a presentation of your home (4 to 5 lines),
parts envisaged (name, materials, dimensions, short description)
2 to 4 HD visuals per room.
