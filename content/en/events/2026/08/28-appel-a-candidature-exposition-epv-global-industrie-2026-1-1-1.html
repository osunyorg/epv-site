About SEPEM Grenoble 202

SEPEM Grenoble is the trade show for services, equipment, and processes for industry, dedicated to manufacturers in the region. For three days, it brings together decision-makers, maintenance managers, production managers, methods engineers, and industrial directors to discover concrete solutions for their production sites.

SEPEM Grenoble 2026 Website
https://grenoble.sepem-industries.com/

"Les Fleurons de L'industrie" Exhibition

WHITE MIRROR


Designed as a scenographic exhibition, "Flagships of Industry" will showcase emblematic finished products from the region's industrial fabric. ⚠️This is neither a pavilion nor a collective exhibition space, but rather a non-commercial exhibition highlighting regional industrial excellence. The selected companies do not have a booth and are not expected to be continuously present in the exhibition space. The objective: to offer a collective showcase of the industrial expertise of the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, with a focus on regional development.

Scope, and Participation Guidelines

The exhibition is open to industrial companies located in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region. • It will not be exclusively composed of companies with the EPV (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) label. • Companies holding the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (Living Heritage Company) label will benefit from specific promotion, with their label being mentioned within the exhibition. • Participation is entirely free • A finished product will be provided for the duration of the trade show • Exhibition in a non-commercial, scenographically designed space • Invitation to the opening ceremony, in the presence of institutional and political representatives from the region

Application Submission

SEPEM Grenoble 2026 Registration - Complete the form
https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=wBMATUtnBEqg2zrA4P8w7juiLe503dJFrcxSsNYpQTRURUZKREUzN1VLVDNQWFlVQlk0UkpSRkUwRC4u


Companies wishing to participate are invited to complete the registration form. ⚠️ Application deadline: August 28th. No registration will be possible after this date.


We are counting on the mobilization of the Living Heritage Companies (EPV) of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes to collectively showcase the industrial excellence of our region at SEPEM Grenoble 2026. For any further information: contact@reseauexcellence.fr
