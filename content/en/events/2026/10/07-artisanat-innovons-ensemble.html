the Directorate-General for Enterprise organises a the morning devoted to innovation in handicrafts entitled "Artisanat : Let's innovate together » at the Pierre Mendès France conference centre Ministry of Economy and Finance. This event will bring together companies craft and innovative solutions to show how innovation can effectively meet the needs of artisans.

body: >-

The objective of this event

Bring the craft sector to innovation by showing concrete benefits (gain of time, reduction of penitibility, improvement of profitability, preservation of know-how)
Accelerate the adoption of innovative solutions by artisans (technical, environmental, organisational)
Enhancing the attractiveness of craft trades, in particular by valuing tools that facilitate business day-to-day life and mix in the sector
