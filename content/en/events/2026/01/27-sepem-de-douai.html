On the occasion of SEPEM Customs 2026, to be held from 27-29 January 2026 at Gaitant Expo, Excellence Network joins the SEPEM fair to enhance regional industrial excellence through a new edition of the exhibition "The Fleurons of Industry".

body: >-


This exceptional display showcases the emblematic industrial know-how of Hauts-de-France through the presentation of finished products representative of excellence, innovation and regional industrial creativity. Each selected company presents a product illustrating its expertise and the value of its achievements.
Free and non-commercial, the exhibition is part of a heritage and collective approach aimed at providing visitors - industrial, institutional, students and prescribers - with a global and concrete vision of products and industrial skills of the Territory.
This partnership illustrates the joint commitment of the SEPM and Excellence Network in favour of the outreach, attractiveness and recognition of the Hauts-de-France industry.
