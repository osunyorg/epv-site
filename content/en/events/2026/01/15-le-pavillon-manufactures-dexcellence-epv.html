The village Manufactures of Excellence come to life House & Object January 2026, offering a unique experience where French know-how shines in all its splendor. Thought as a response to the exceptional aspirations of 25,000 interior architects and decorators present, this pavilion unfolds like a real box, a recomposed baroque where the ornament is subtle and precise.

body: >-

Handicrafts except in honour

At the heart of the pavilion, Master glassmakers, cabinetmakers of art, weavers, tapesters, staffers, locksmiths of art, marbriers , and designers of furniture and luminaires meet to share their know-how. Each gesture gives form to matter, sublimated in detail, telling stories of excellence and passion .
Weared by the State label EPV - Living Heritage Companies , this collective of French houses reveals another anatomy of luxury: far from the ostentation, the extraordinary lies in precision, refinement and the contemporary twist that surprises every discovery.

A walk among the treasures of know-how

Among the artisans and houses present:


La Maison du Vitrail and La Verrerie de Biot  for the magic of glass


Focus Creation and Agencement Paul Champ for unique layouts


TIssages de la Lys - Jules Pansu and Le Jacquard Français for exceptional textiles


Eurocave , Dumas Paris , Bronzes de France & Rings , Creze , Horus , Cogitech , Garabos , Blanc  Carrare  ...

Each of these names hides a rare expertise and a story to discover, a know-how capable of sublime the worlds of hospitality and decoration. The pavilion Manufactures of Excellence EPV is more than an exhibition: It's a immersive travel , a meeting with the exception and the thoroughness of the French artisanal gesture. Between Back to Traditions and contemporary creativity , let yourself be surprised by the beauty of materials, the ingenuity of creations and the poetry of details.
Maison&Objet, January 2026 - Come and discover the luxury reinvented by French craftsmanship.
