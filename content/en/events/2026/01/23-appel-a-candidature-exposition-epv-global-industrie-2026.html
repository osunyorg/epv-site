The Réseau Excellence, in partnership with Global Industry, are pleased to announce the launch of a call for applications dedicated to labeled companiesEnterprises of the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant  (EPV).

body: >-

A unique opportunity to value your expertise

This call for applications offers you the opportunity to display your flagship products during Global Industry 2026 , the largest French industrial gathering, to be held 30 March to 2 April 2026 to Paris Nord Villepinte .
The aim of this exhibition is to highlight the excellence and diversity of French industrial know-how to a high value-added public:

60,000 industrialists expected
8 000 young people in training
Many national media (BFM, La Tribune, RMC, etc.)

Why register?

Participating in this EPV exhibition is:

Valorize your exceptional know-how on a major industrial scene

Gaining visibility among tomorrow's policy makers, prescribers and talents

Contribute to a collective exhibition representative of the EPV label

Benefit from a recognized framework and strong media coverage

How to participate?

To apply, we invite you to complete the following online form before February 15, 2026

Registration Global Industry Exhibition 2026 – Fill out the form
