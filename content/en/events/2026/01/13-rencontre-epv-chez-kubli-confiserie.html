Come and participate in our next EPV meeting in Ile-de-France

We will have the opportunity to discover Kubli Confiserie, a family-owned company that has been making candy for more than 120 years. Kubli perpetuates a rare artisanal know-how: the work of cooked sugar, the hand-made manufacture of laminated or praline candy, and the creation of tailor-made recipes to the tastes and colors desired by their customers. Monday 13 January at 9 a.m. Confectionery Kubli - 17 rue Gustave Eiffel 91420 Morangis (91)
