WHO'S NEXT : Celebrating fashion, shape tomorrow

What Next is the benchmark for fashion and lifestyle professionals in Europe. Since 1994, the show has brought together the new Femme de Prêt-à-Porter collections, accessories, jewellery, beauty and lifestyle. Along with its 1200 exhibiting brands, Whos Next organizes a program rich in conferences, masterclasses, workshops and forums. The opportunity to decipher major trends, explore tomorrow's challenges and take part in innovation. Always organized with Bijorhca and Shoppe Object Paris, Whos Next is accompanied by Interfilière Paris in September, and in January at the International Lingerie & Interfilière Paris. This organization is designed to stimulate the synergies of the different universes proposed by the events of the WSN Group. Each edition of Who的s Next gathers about 38,000 visitors, 70% of whom are retailers, multi-brands, department stores and e-commerce buyers, all meet to discover upcoming trends and experience an immersive experience combining business and emotion.
