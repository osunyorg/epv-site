On September 30, 2026, the Excellence Network EPV Île-de-France will hold its General Assembly at 5 p.m., followed by an EPV meeting within the Art Shop of MuzeoAt 6:00.

body: >-

Agenda of the General Assembly

From 5pm Location : 31 avenue Léon Gambetta, Montrouge 92120

EPV meeting at Muzeo

Muzéo fabrique avec soin des reproductions de tableaux, des photographies et affiches d'art sur mesure, imprimées et encadrées à la main dans un atelier français labellisé EPV.

ART STAGING & ART SHOP


The Excellence Network gives you an appointment for a meeting within the art premises of Muzeo . A specialist in art publishing, high-end printing and the manufacture of custom wall decors, Muzeo will open the doors of its Art Shop in Montrouge companies in the network. This visit will also provide an opportunity to exchange between EVPs, share and strengthen links within the network. We hope to find you many for this time of exchange and conviviality, dedicated to the promotion of French living heritage.
