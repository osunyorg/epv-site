The Speed Dating Design Object makes its great return on Tuesday 29 September nextHey!

An opportunity exceptional offer to French companies (manufacturers, publishers, craftsmen, galleries, etc. to initiate new collaborations and develop new products, reinventing themselves by integrating design at the heart of their strategy.

body: >-

Are you a manufacturer, publisher, craftsman, gallery owner or head of a furniture brand? Do you have a project?

Developing a new collection
Reissue an iconic piece of your catalog
Valuing fall or production waste
Explore new materials or processes
Reinvent an existing range
Respond to new uses or markets
Strengthening your brand image
Imagine a new project

Whatever your project is, the FRENCH DESIGN identifies the designers most able to accompany you.

Why participate?
Meet multiple designers in one day
Discover creative profiles tailored to your issues
Save valuable time with a match made upstream
Bringing out new product ideas and collections
Initiate concrete and sustainable collaborations
Join the FRENCH DESIGN Incubator
Qualified meetings to identify the right partners and
opening up new perspectives.
