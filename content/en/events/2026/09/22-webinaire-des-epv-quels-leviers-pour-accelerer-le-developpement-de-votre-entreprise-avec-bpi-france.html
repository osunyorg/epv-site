V ous have financing needs to innovate or accelerate your development? Do you already export, or do you want to go international? This seminar, co-hosted by Bpifrance, will help you identify concrete levers to accelerate the development of your business.

Summary of the Seminar

Synthetic presentation of Bpifrance

Questions and Answers: privileged exchange time to answer your questions and provide concrete solutions.
