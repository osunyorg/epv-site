The aim of the "Rendez-vous d'Exception" program is to enable you to discover excellent craftsmanship and know-how, in workshops focusing on restoration, conservation or creation, in emblematic heritage or cultural sites that are usually little or not accessible to the public.

This exceptional program, for a limited number of visitors, is organized by appointment and requires registration. It's a unique opportunity to discover the diversity of the arts and crafts, and their contribution to the construction or restoration of prestigious heritage or cultural sites.

The program will be available on March 8 at this link.

Booking conditions, specific to each event, will be detailed there.
