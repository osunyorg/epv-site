  • Create the Grand-Est regional network in liaison with the existing national network of companies awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label by the French government
  • Participate in the actions of regional associations pursuing the same goals, contributing to the expression of the needs/difficulties of companies in terms of recruitment and training, and to the implementation of the most appropriate solutions
  • Contribute to initiatives aimed at safeguarding, conserving and passing on the rare craft and industrial skills of excellence in the Grand-Est region
  • Enlist the support of all industrial and craft companies in the Grand-Est region that possess rare and exceptional know-how
  • Organize actions for the benefit of certified companies in the Grand-Est region, in particular meetings, information exchanges, sponsorship, sales promotion and communication campaigns
  • Set up partnerships with professional organizations, public organizations, local authorities, training bodies, economic sectors (financial partners in particular) and the media to support the development of EPV Grand-Est companies
  • Promote the EPV label (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) in the Grand-Est region, in France and internationally
