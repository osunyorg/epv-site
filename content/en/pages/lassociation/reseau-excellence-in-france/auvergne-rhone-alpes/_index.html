body: >-

Missions

To encourage all industrial and craft companies in the region with rare and exceptional expertise to join the association

Contribute to initiatives to safeguard, conserve and pass on the region's rare and outstanding craft and industrial skills

Organise initiatives to benefit certified companies in our region, in particular meetings, information exchanges, sponsorship and sales promotion and communication initiatives.

Promote the EPV label (Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant) in the region, in France and internationally
