The Réseau Excellence organization is closely linked to France's craft and industrial ecosystems, of which it is now a key player.

body: >-

GENERAL ASSEMBLY
The Ordinary General Meeting is held at least once a year and comprises all paid-up members of the association. It deliberates on future orientations. It appoints or renews members of the Board of Directors. It also sets the annual membership fee. After deliberation, the General Meeting votes on the annual report or activity report, and on the accounts for the financial year. Decisions are taken by a majority of members present.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The association is managed by a Board of Directors made up of :
from 2 to 10 members who are directors of a living heritage company, elected for 2 years by the general meeting

2 ex-officio members: a representative of external personalities, and the President (or his representative) of the Institut pour les savoir-faire français.

FOUNDING MEMBERS
Arnaud MARION, Artistic Director, Pianos PLEYEL, certified company

Olivier MELLERIO, President of Mellerio dits Meller jewelry, certified company

Michael WAGNER, CEO of WAGNER S.A., certified company

Hervé BOULLANGER, Rapporteur for the EPV label award committee

MEMBERSHIP
To join Réseau Excellence, you must :
have been awarded the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (Living Heritage Company) label by the French government, or be, or have been, a member of the label award commission defined in article 4 of decree no. 2006-595 of May 23, 2006; or belong to a service or communications company involved in supporting EPVs;

have been approved by the Board of Directors;

adhere to the articles of association ;

and pay the membership fee set by the General Meeting.

The Board of Directors may refuse membership, giving reasons for its decision. Membership fees are set each year by the General Meeting on the recommendation of the Board of Directors.
