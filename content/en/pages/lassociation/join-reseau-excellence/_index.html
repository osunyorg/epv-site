Are you an EPV? To join Réseau Excellence, join the association in your region!

body: >-


To join Réseau Excellence, you need to become a member of the regional association from which your company originates. Of course, your membership entitles you to all our activities throughout France! The annual membership fee is calculated on the basis of your sales figures. To find out more, click on the link for your region (in French).
