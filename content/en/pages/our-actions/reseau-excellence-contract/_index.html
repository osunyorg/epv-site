Réseau Excellence Contract, the new joint initiative launched by the French companies of exceptional know-how, to spread out their innovation potential in France and internationally. A unique collective at the service of hospitality and luxury projects in the field of interior decoration and architecture.

body: >-


Initiated by Réseau Excellence, "Réseau Excellence Contract  is a bold initiative aimed at promoting exceptional French know-how while responding to contemporary economic and cultural challenges. This ambitious project embodies the commitment to the preservation, recognition and revitalization of French art professions and industrial heritage.

" We see this group as a circle of excellence, where each member contributes to a collective vision of growth, mutual support and shared success. Our goal is to create unprecedented opportunities for our companies by providing them with training, visibility and networking needed to thrive in a competitive and globalized environment. Like the Réseau Excellence Contract, other sectors will emerge within the Réseau Excellence, such as gastronomy, fashion and beauty... the "Contract" sector prefigures this approach. "
Tristan De Witte
President of Réseau Excellence, Founder of Rivalen Group
