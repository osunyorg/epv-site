The Editorial Committee of the Reseau Excellence brings together a representative of the Board of Directors for each region. Together, they ensure a common vision, harmonization of content and consistency of communication across the country.

Since the adoption of the name Reseau Excellence, the committee accompanies the construction of a shared identity: common graphic charter, unified tools and coherent editorial line. On 19 September 2024, the official launch of the website marks a key step, reflecting the network's desire to provide clear, homogeneous and valuable information.

The EPV Label Communication Kit is available on request from:

  • SGS, the EPV Label Manager: FR.LabelEPV@sgs.com

This includes:

  • The graphic charter EPV.
  • The regulations for the use of the EPV mark;
  • One brochure in French and English to enhance your labelling;
  • One Presentation of the EPV label to better communicate your commitment.

and to go further, the Excellence Network provides you with:

  • Recommendations to highlight your EPV Label;
