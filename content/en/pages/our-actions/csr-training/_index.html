Transmission & formation
The EPV label is a key tool for the conservation and transmission of excellence know-how. Thanks to its financial support, the training of apprentices and the documentation of techniques, it ensures the preservation of living heritage and of artisanal and industrial traditions. Artistic residences can complement this system by promoting intergenerational exchange and artistic creation nourished by ancestral know-how. By joining the EVP label, you are involved in the preservation and transmission of exceptional know-how, guaranteeing a unique cultural and economic richness.
