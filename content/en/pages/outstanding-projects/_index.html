Prescription Club
Our Prescription Club regularly brings together EPV expertise by market sector, or for exceptional, customized projects. The "Club Prescripteur" hence offers a unique platform to highlight and develop French companies with exceptional know-how, connecting them with influential networks and strategic growth opportunities in the architecture, design and luxury industries worldwide.
