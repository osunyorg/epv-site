The luthier and/or restorer of rubbing stringed instruments creates and restores violins, violas, cellos, double basses, violas de gamba. With the help of planks, gouges and canifes, he works the maple wood for the head, bottom, locks and horse; Spicea for the table of harmony and soul; Ebony for the touch and ebony, the palisander, the box for ankles, ropes and chin straps. It uses varnish for finishing.
