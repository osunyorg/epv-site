In the workshop, the organ maker and/or restorer works the wood of the buffets and mechanisms, it flows and forms the tin plates to make the pipes, it works leather, paper and various metals. It installs and restores all parts of the instrument on the site, regulates its sounds and grants according to various temperaments.
