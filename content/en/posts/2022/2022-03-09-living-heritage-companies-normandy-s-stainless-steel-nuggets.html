Normandy Living Heritage Companies
Filt commission nets, Saint-James knitwear, Berger lamps, Bohin needles, Cherbourg umbrellas, Mauviel cookware, Laudescher wood architectural solutions, Michel Cluizel chocolates, Dehondt machine tools, Isigny-Sainte-Mère, Kiplay, Les Biscuits de l'Abbaye... Their common denominator: these companies have a specific, often ancient, "gesture". Most of them have been awarded the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label. Their activity is closely linked to the region.
