It's at Brasserie 3 Monts, itself labeled Enterprise of Living Heritage (Entreprise du patrimoine vivant) that Our General Assembly of the Hauts-de-France EPVs was held. A perfect setting to celebrate the craftsmanship of our region!

body: >-


Pierre Marchica  welcomed us with exceptional generosity and passion. After the AG, he offered us a  tasting its iconic beers  and one  visit of the brewery , allowing everyone to dive into the heart of exceptional brewing know-how, transmitted with passion. The meeting ended in the whole new  bistronomic restaurant The Smaak , where a delicious buffet - accompanied, of course, by 3 Monts beers - extended the exchanges in conviviality. A nice demonstration that the label  EPV , they are above all  passionate women and men , which bring out the artisanal and industrial excellence of our territories. Thanks to Pierre and the entire Brasserie 3 Monts team for this welcome!

Pierre Marchica welcomed us with exceptional generosity and passion.

After the AG, he offered us a tasting its iconic beers and one visit of the brewery, allowing everyone to dive into the heart of exceptional brewing know-how, transmitted with passion.

The meeting ended in the whole new bistronomic restaurant The Smaak, where a delicious buffet - accompanied, of course, by 3 Monts beers - extended the exchanges in conviviality.

A nice demonstration that the label EPV, they are above all passionate women and men, which bring out the artisanal and industrial excellence of our territories.

Thanks to Pierre and the entire Brasserie 3 Monts team for this welcome!
