EPV IMPACTS program: turning your CSR and Carbon issues into performance levers

CSR and the Carbon Balance Sheet are no longer mere regulatory obligations - today they are real competitive assets. That's the bet of the IMPACTS programme , a semi-collective accompaniment over 12 months, designed specifically for the EPV and subsidized via Bpifrance and the region.
A hybrid, concrete and operational programme
The programme combines collective training (groups of 8-10 companies, 3h/month for 6 months) and 1 Individual support to choose from two routes:


CSR course  : maturity diagnosis, strategy, roadmap and CSR report

Carbon course  : emissions analysis, decarbonation action plan and recovery
All in hybrid format - face and visio - to adapt to the constraints of the leaders. Measurable results
Accompanied companies average +38% increase in their CSR/Carbone knowledge, +20 points on their EcoVadis ratings, and a 20% reduction in their GHG emissions at 5 years. 87% of them believe that accompaniment generates a direct ROI.
Accessible cost through grants
Depending on the size of the company, the remainder can be reduced from 30 to 70%. For example, for a company with less than 20 employees, the CSR Impacts Programme is 3 850€ excluding VAT after Bpifrance grant.
Why join this program? Winning tenders, reducing energy costs, anticipating regulations, bringing together teams around a meaningful project The IMPACTS Program addresses all these issues in one structured path.

CSR and the Carbon Balance Sheet are no longer mere regulatory obligations - today they are real competitive assets. That's the bet of the IMPACTS programme, a semi-collective accompaniment over 12 months, designed specifically for the EPV and subsidized via Bpifrance and the region. A hybrid, concrete and operational programme

The programme combines collective training (groups of 8-10 companies, 3h/month for 6 months) and 1 Individual support to choose from two routes:

  • CSR course : maturity diagnosis, strategy, roadmap and CSR report
  • Carbon course : emissions analysis, decarbonation action plan and recovery

All in hybrid format - face and visio - to adapt to the constraints of the leaders.

Measurable results

Accompanied companies average +38% increase in their CSR/Carbone knowledge, +20 points on their EcoVadis ratings, and a 20% reduction in their GHG emissions at 5 years. 87% of them believe that accompaniment generates a direct ROI.

Accessible cost through grants

Depending on the size of the company, the remainder can be reduced from 30 to 70%. For example, for a company with less than 20 employees, the CSR Impacts Programme is 3 850€ excluding VAT after Bpifrance grant.

Why join this program?

Winning tenders, reducing energy costs, anticipating regulations, bringing together teams around a meaningful project

The IMPACTS Program addresses all these issues in one structured path.
