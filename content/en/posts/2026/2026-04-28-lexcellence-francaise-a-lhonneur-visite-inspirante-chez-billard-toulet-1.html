An immense Thanks toHubert Coisnefor his welcome and explanations passionate about every stage of manufacturing, from design to realization.

Your billiards tailored to your needs:
Choose according to your style (design, contemporary, classic, relaxing or or your favorite game (American, French, English, snooker, mixed).
Total customization: wood, fabric, finishes... We create pieces unique, in harmony with your interior.
Life guaranteeon the plane,manufactured in Francedelivered and installed worldwide.

Next stage: the preparation of ourInternational exhibition at the trade fair Italy, where Billard Toulet will once again represent the best of French know-how.
