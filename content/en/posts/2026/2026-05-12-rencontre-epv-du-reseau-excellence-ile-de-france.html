A visit to the heart of a territory of excellence

After the discovery of Charles Paris, the visit to Le Lit National extended this immersion into French artisanal excellence. Between historic workshops and the iconic Trocadéro boutique, the EPVs as  Atelier Pictet, Daumet, Dissidi, Kubli Confectionery, La Maison du Vitrail, Maison Alivon and Papier d'Arménie  have discovered a factory where each gesture bears witness to a living heritage and a constant desire to preserve rare know-how. A huge thank you to Nicolas Lévy and Nicolas Beaufour for their welcome to their emblematic houses that contribute to the spread of French living heritage.
