body: >-

The excellence of Occitanie's know-how in honour

In the presence of Pierre-André Durand , Prefect of the Occitanie region and Prefect of the Haute-Garonne, the newly labeled Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) companies received their diploma at an official ceremony organized at the Occitanie Region Prefecture.
Exceptional know-how in the heart of the region
Each distinguished company contributes to the economic, cultural and human wealth of the territories through rare know-how and excellence. The EPV label recognizes their daily commitment to the preservation, transmission and development of exceptional professions that contribute to the influence of Occitanie and France.
A ceremony under the sign of excellence
Lore Camillo, President of the Association Regionale des EPV Occitanie, stressed the essential role of these companies in valuing French living heritage. The ceremony, bringing together many emblematic companies from the region, including Les Délices d'Aliénor, Papeterie Martin, App Bois and Jean Alran, marks a moment of recognition and shared pride.
