The Artist and know-how program at the Frac-Artothèque in Limoges is a research and artistic creation program that connects artists and heritage companies living in New Aquitaine, around know-how of excellence. In partnership with the Chambre de Commerce et d'Industrie de la Haute-Vienne in connection with Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine, he encouraged the hybridization of techniques and materials in collaborative projects.

Julie Chaffort - Skin from 22 January to 28 March 2026 Exposure For the skin explores the unique place of the animal in the artist's work. By interacting with other forms of presence at stake in his films - human, plant... - the animal participates in a shift of gaze and the opening of new perceptions. Through this presence, Julie Chaffort invites us to question our markers and to move the angle from which we envisage otherness.

Laureline Galliot - Linen decorations From 22 January to 23 May 2026 Laureline Galliot designed a hybrid installation designed for the "balcony" of the Frac, the work unfolds the metaphor of a skeleton under the skin thanks to textiles, impressions, 3D shapes and mapping. Julie Chaffort - Skin from 22 January to 28 March 2026

Exposure For the skin explores the unique place of the animal in the artist's work. By interacting with other forms of presence at stake in his films - human, plant... - the animal participates in a shift of gaze and the opening of new perceptions. Through this presence, Julie Chaffort invites us to question our markers and to move the angle from which we envisage otherness.

Laureline Galliot - Linen decorations From 22 January to 23 May 2026

Laureline Galliot designed a hybrid installation designed for the "balcony" of the Frac, the work unfolds the metaphor of a skeleton under the skin thanks to textiles, impressions, 3D shapes and mapping.

Participating companies[all EVCs at the time of program conduct; Since then, some are no longer labeled EPV, see asterisk]: Agnelle (Haute-Vienne), Ateliers des Facs Similes* (Dordogne), Barris* (Haute-Vienne), C2000 (Haute-Vienne), Chapal* (Creuse), J.M. Weston (Haute-Vienne), Laine et Compagnie (Haute-Vienne), Rémy Martin (Charente), Tannerie Bastin (Haute-Vienne)

Frac-Artothèque, located 17bis Rue Charles Michels in Limoges
