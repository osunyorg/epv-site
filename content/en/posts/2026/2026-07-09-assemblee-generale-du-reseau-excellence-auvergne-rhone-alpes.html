The Réseau Excellence Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes brought together its members at its Annual General Meeting, held under the chairmanship of Gregory Laurent, in the presence of Renaud Durand General Secretary for Regional Affairs of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes representingÉtienne Guyot, Prefect of the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, Prefect of the Rhône.

body: >-


This day was marked by the official graduation ceremony of the Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label newly labeled or renewed undertakings. In total, 31 enterprises in the region of which: 10 First labeled have been honoured for the excellence of their artisanal and industrial know-how. The Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region thus confirms its place as the first French region in the number of members of the Excellence Network of companies labeled EPV. After the general meeting and a lunch cocktail with the opening of a photographic exhibition, the participants attended a round table devoted to a future issue: "What promises do EPVs want to keep in the AI era? » . Witnesses and experts shared their thoughts on how companies of excellence can combine innovation, artificial intelligence and the preservation of know-how. This meeting once again illustrated the strength of the network of EPVs: to make life an exceptional heritage while preparing the future of the professions of excellence.Find also the report dedicated to this ceremony broadcast by Tonic Radio.
