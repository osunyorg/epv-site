La Châtre, 11 April 2026

On the occasion of the European Days of the Arts
2026, the Castle of Ars hosted the official launch of the Réseau Excellence EPV Centre-Val de Loire, chaired by Stéphane Parrain also head of
the EPV Jourdant et fils.
The event was also marked by a
graduation of the Label Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant, in the presence of
Minister Delegate for Foreign Trade and Attractiveness
Nicolas Forissier, Mayor of the Châtre and honorary member of the Réseau
Excellence EPV Centre-Val de Loire.

Roger Pradier
Un temps fort pour l’excellence des savoir-faire français

Several companies have been distinguished or renewed
their EPV labeling, a national reference that values craft excellence
France.

Among them, Roger Pradier received his diploma of
renewal, welcoming the requirement of its manufacturing processes and
the commitment of its teams.
" Receive the renewal of the EPV label is a great
pride. It recognizes the work of our teams and our commitment to preserve
and the development of exceptional know-how " points out Sophie Mussche.

Roger Pradier
Transmission of know-how at the heart of the challenges

Beyond recognition, this day has brought to light
a strategic issue for labeled companies: the transmission of
skills.

Often rare and few present in the
training, these know-how needs to be structured and
to ensure their sustainability.
" The transmission of know-how is now an issue
key to ensuring the continuity and competitiveness of our businesses
excellence", Reminds Stéphane Parrain.

Roger Pradier

A highlight for excellence... and its international outreach

At the same time, the international
EPV companies was illustrated by the distinction obtained by Messigerie Bodin Joyeux, winner of the Best of APLF Award - Hong Kong at the Best of APLF
Awards 2026.
This award, awarded at the APLF international fair
Leather and Materials+, rewards the originality of the design and quality of the
know-how of exhibitors.

According to the jury, " high-end leathers to the touch
exceptional offer both sensual and sophisticated experience, with
rich and refined colours. The collection is for high-rise houses
sewing looking for materials combining quality and high performance.  » learn more
