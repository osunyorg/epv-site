Manufactures of Excellence: EPV at the heart of contract and hospitality

On nearly 400 m2, this space brought together 20 companies
labeled Live Heritage Enterprises, illustrating wealth and
diversity of French know-how applied to the worlds of contract,
interior architecture and hospitality.

Thought as a true immersive scenography, the
curation of this space was entrusted to Carole de Bona, founder of Bel
Book. His curatorial approach revealed all the power
and to highlight their ability to dialogue
with contemporary projects.

Architects, decorators, prescribers and actors
international design companies have discovered
the alliance between heritage, innovation and industrial excellence.

A presence that confirms the vocation of the Network Excellence
Contract: Positioning EPV as Strategic Partners for Large
development and hospitality projects.
