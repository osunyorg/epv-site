body: >-


This national map highlights more than 350 companies open to the public throughout the country, including 84 companies labeled EPV , with specific identification. Included in the Michelin thematic map collection (including "Les Plus Beaux Villages de France" or "Vélo en France"), this edition constitutes the 20th thematic map published by the publisher. It is a structuring tool to increase the visibility of excellent know-how and support the tourism of know-how. One pre-purchase opportunity is offered to companies wishing to distribute the card to their audiences.
