The Réseau Excellence EPV Grand Est is proud to announce the release of its first Catalogue of Excellence, a new work at the national level that brings together the affiliated companies labeled Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) and highlights the exceptional know-how that make the region famous.

Designed as a real showcase of French economic heritage, this catalogue presents the EPV Houses of the Great East through eight universes: Fashion & Beauty, Furniture & Decoration, Gastronomy, Architecture & Heritage Building, Leisure & Transport, Table Arts, Medical & Mechanical Industrial Equipment, Culture & Communication.

body: >-

Through this publication, the Réseau Excellence EPV Grand Est affirms a strong ambition:

Promote the artisanal and industrial excellence of its territory, foster collaborations between companies and strengthen the influence of the Manufactures du Grand Est in France and internationally. More than just a simple directory, this catalogue is the flagship of collective pride. It showcases companies that perpetuate rare know-how, innovate daily and actively contribute to regional economic dynamism.

A Territory of Excellence

The Great East occupies a major place in the national network of Living Heritage Enterprises.
4th national ranking in terms of labeled companies
2nd national rank in terms of membership
110 Houses labeled, representing 8% of the national total
6 462 non-relocable jobs
EUR 1.1 billion of combined turnover
These figures reflect the richness of an ecosystem where heritage, innovation, transfer of know-how and economic performance combine on a daily basis.

National and international broadcast

Available in both print and digital versions, the Catalogue of Excellence will benefit from an important distribution plan. It will be relayed to professionals via e-mailing campaigns, the social networks of the Réseau Excellence EPV Grand Est as well as at the main trade shows in which the network participates. A targeted prospecting approach will also be carried out with French and international prescribers and decision makers, including:
Major hotel and catering groups
Architects, interior architects and design agencies
The actors in the restoration of heritage and historical monuments
Public and private contractors in France and abroad.
The aim is to make this catalogue a real reference tool for all donors looking for exceptional companies capable of intervening on demanding projects, both in France and abroad.
