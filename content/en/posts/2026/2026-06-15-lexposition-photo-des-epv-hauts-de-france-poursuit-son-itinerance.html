After being exposed on the grids of the Lille Prefecture, our photographic exhibition honouring all companies Members of the Association Régionale des EPV Hauts-de-France pursues its route and move to the sub-prefecture of Bethune.

body: >-


Through these portraits and know-how, this exhibition celebrates the artisanal and industrial excellence of our territory and highlights companies that perpetuate exceptional trades while making them live in the present. A big thank you to our partners and the institutions that accompany this approach of valuing Enterprises of Living Heritage and their talents. Go to Bethune to discover this exhibition and go to meet those who make the Hauts-de-France shine.
