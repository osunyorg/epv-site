body: >-

A beautiful day for the heritage living in Île-de-France

The EPV Excellence Network of the island of France represented by its President, Mrs Emmanuelle Andrieux had the pleasure of participating in this ceremony, alongside the Prefecture of the Île-de-France region , the Enterprise Directorate-General represented by Mr Geoffroy Cailloux , the CMA Île-de-France represented by its President, Mr Francis Bussière and our partners as Enterprise and Discovery , to celebrate the companies that bring out the crafts of excellence of our territory.
This highlight brought to the spotlight companies committed to the transmission, innovation and preservation of rare know-how, fully embodying the spirit of French living heritage.
The EPV label is more than just a distinction: it rewards a daily commitment to the quality, creativity and sustainability of exceptional professions. Our congratulations again to the companies labeled and renewed!


The entire ceremony was hosted by Mr. Antoine Terrasson (SGS), Manager of the EPV Label

Among the labeled, congratulate our members!
