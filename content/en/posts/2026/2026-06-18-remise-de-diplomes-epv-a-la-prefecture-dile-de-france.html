We warmly congratulate the new companies labeled as well as those whose label Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) has been renewed, symbol of the excellence of French know-how.

body: >-

A beautiful day for the live heritage in Île-de-France

We had the pleasure of participating in this ceremony, alongside the Prefecture of the Île-de-France region , the Enterprise Directorate-General , the CMA Île-de-France and  Enterprise and Discovery , to celebrate the companies that bring out the crafts of excellence of our territory.
This highlight brought to the spotlight companies committed to the transmission, innovation and preservation of rare know-how, fully embodying the spirit of French living heritage.
The EPV label is more than just a distinction: it rewards a daily commitment to the quality, creativity and sustainability of exceptional professions. Our congratulations again to the companies labeled and renewed!

Ceremony program and speakers

The ceremony was opened by a PSGAPP welcome note provided by Madame the Prefect , then by the intervention of Monsieur Francis Bussière , President of the Chamber of Trades and Crafts of Ile-de-France. Madam Emmanuelle Andrieux , President of the Île-de-France Excellence Network, and Mr. Geoffroy Cailloux The Directorate-General for Enterprise then recalled the importance of the label and the value of the know-how of excellence, highlighting the issues of transmission, recognition and outreach of exceptional professions.


The ceremony was animated by Mr. Antoine Terrasson (SGS), in charge of the audit of the label.
