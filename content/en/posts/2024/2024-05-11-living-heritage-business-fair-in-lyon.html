Organised by the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes EPV association and the Région Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, with the support of the Chambre régionale de métiers et de l'artisanat and the Chambre de commerce et d'industrie of the region, the first exclusively French exhibition of the Enterprises du Patrimoine Vivant was held in Lyon on 24 and 25 October.

body: >-


The ambition of these two days was to promote the excellence of French know-how and to make the EVPs shine. This fair was intended for professionals as well as the general public. It brought together the companies labeled EPV, created synergies between the regional actors, and made it possible to discover exceptional occupations and job opportunities.


About 30 companies with a living heritage have exhibited in 4 different sectors of activity: • Crafts: materials processing • Architecture & built heritage Gastronomy • Fashion & Accessories
