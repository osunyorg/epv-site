body: >-


In the vitrine:

the Madeleines Bijou, 170 years of existence

Sarah Linda , young art mark of the table inspired by nature
The Porcelains Jacques Pergay, Living Heritage Company

Works from ENSAD , the National School of Art and Design of Limoges
Limoges Metropole has redesigned its tourist, physical and mobile reception "outside the walls" and develops showcases and information points in places of great passage. Limoges Metropole seized the opportunity offered by the SNCF and invested the former reception point of the Benedictin station, of nearly 40m², with an annual flow of 1.5 million people, to develop a showcase valuing the tourist excellence of the territory.
