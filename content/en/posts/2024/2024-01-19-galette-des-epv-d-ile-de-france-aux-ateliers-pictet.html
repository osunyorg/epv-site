body: >-


A good start to the new year, in good company, in a magical setting in the heart of Paris: the galette (cake of kings) of the local association of EPVs' from Île-de-France brought together its members and future members in the Ateliers Pictet for a warm and convivial occasion. It was an opportunity to discover exceptional glassmaking skills through a "live" demonstration of the transformation of the material. In the superb showroom unveiled by Aurélie Kostka, guests were able to get to know each other better, share their expertise and learn more about the challenges facing their profession.

A good start to the new year, in good company, in a magical setting in the heart of Paris: the galette (cake of kings) of the local association of EPVs' from Île-de-France brought together its members and future members in the Ateliers Pictet (external link) for a warm and convivial occasion. It was an opportunity to discover exceptional glassmaking skills through a "live" demonstration of the transformation of the material. In the superb showroom unveiled by Aurélie Kostka, guests were able to get to know each other better, share their expertise and learn more about the challenges facing their profession.
