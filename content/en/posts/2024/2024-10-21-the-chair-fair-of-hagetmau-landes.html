The town of Hagetmau (based in Landes) organised the Chair Fair  ‘ Salon de la chaise’ from 18th to 20th October 2024 ,
the Landes trade fair for made-to-measure furnishings and decoration.
On Sunday 20th October, a round-table discussion was held on the ‘Living
Heritage Company, a label of excellence’ with the participation of the
following companies: Agglolux , La Chaiserie Landaise , Sièges Bastiat , Tissage Moutet .

The town of Hagetmau (based in Landes) organised the Chair Fair  ‘Salon de la chaise’ from 18th to 20th October 2024 (external link), the Landes trade fair for made-to-measure furnishings and decoration. On Sunday 20th October, a round-table discussion was held on the ‘Living Heritage Company, a label of excellence’ with the participation of the following companies: Agglolux (external link), La Chaiserie Landaise (external link), Sièges Bastiat (external link), Tissage Moutet (external link).
