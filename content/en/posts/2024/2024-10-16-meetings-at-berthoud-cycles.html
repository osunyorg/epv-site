Members of the Réseau Excellence EPV Bourgogne-Franche-Comté had the pleasure of meeting on Wednesday at the Berthoud Cycles factory, specialized in the manufacture of custom bicycles and bicycle accessories.

body: >-


These moments of sharing and meeting are the foundation of our network, which proudly brings together the best of our region's know-how under the Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) label. Many thanks to Berthoud Cycles and Philippe Marguet for their warm welcome! Their workshop, with its exceptional cachet, bears witness to remarkably well-preserved know-how and a passion for artisanal excellence.
