Osaka 2025, here we go!

ANEPV is particularly proud to be taking part in the success of the French Pavilion at the World Expo, through the mobilization of French craft and industrial ecosystems.

By signing the partnership agreement with the Compagnie Française des Expositions (COFREX ) and theInstitut National des Métiers d'Art (INMA ) at the Ministry of Europe and Foreign Affairs on January 9, Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) will be officially associated with this superb project, unveiled on this occasion under the banner of sustainability... and love!

Love of self, love of others, love of nature: the theme will run from April 13 to October 13,2025, in a building that will welcome over 28 million visitors from all over the world to discover how France "conceives the society of the future and imagines our life tomorrow" through the juxtaposition of art, nature and innovation.

Exhibitions, B-to-B events or events for the general public, boutiques, bistros, bakeries... the French art of living will be offered in a sensory experience sublimated by the excellence of our know-how in every field.

With 160 countries taking part, this event will carry loud and clear the values of expertise, boldness and commitment that are also the foundation of our EPV identity!
