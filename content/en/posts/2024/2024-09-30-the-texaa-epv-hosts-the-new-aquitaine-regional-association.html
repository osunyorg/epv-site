Thursday, September 26, 2024, the Texaa (external link)company, located in Gradignan in Gironde, labeled Heritage Company Living (EPV) in 2023, hosted the very young regional association and its partners, including the Leather Sector Luxury Textile and Crafts of Art of the Region New Aquitaine and consular networks.

The Enterprises of Living Heritage (external link)of the entire New Aquitaine Region had made the displacement, to meet and exchange on the EVP label, their expectations, projects of action of the regional association.

This meeting was an opportunity to present the new banner of communication, Réseau Excellence, carried out by the National Association Réseau Excellence, which becomes the Federation of 12 regional associations of EPVs.

Thanks to an exciting visit led by Matthieu Demptos, head of the EPV Texaa (external link), and its teams, all participants were able to discover the know-how of the company Texaaa, specialized in design, development and manufacture of materials, panels and articles to improve the acoustic comfort of all types of spaces Interiors.
