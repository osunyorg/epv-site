To mark the European Heritage Days, the Association EPV Grand-Est is organizing a themed exhibition, “Grand-Est, Grand'Art”.

body: >-


Dedicated to the rare expertise of Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV) in the Grand-Est region, at the invitation of Josiane Chevalier, Prefect of the region, the exhibition will be held in the heart of Strasbourg, in the emblematic Hôtel de la Préfecture, the former Hôtel Klin, known as Petit Broglie. Two magnificent exhibition spaces, rich in architecture and décor, as well as the inner courtyard, will provide a prestigious historical backdrop for this event. A captivating scenography will showcase the excellence and diversity of the talents of the EPVs of the Grand-Est region.

Practical information
Exhibition “Grand-Est, Grand'Art” | European Heritage Days September 21-22, 2024 Hôtel Klinglin Place du Petit-Broglie, 67000 Strasbourg Visits at 2:00 pm and 4:00 pm By reservation only
