Here's a look back at our last meeting between the EPVs of the Ile-de-France region, held on March 14 at the REMY GARNIER workshops in the heart of the Bastille district.

body: >-


In addition to Nicolas Merveilleux's visit to Rémy Garnier 's workshops in Paris to discover the skills and techniques of bronze-making, we also had the pleasure of welcoming Gabrielle Légeret from the De l'or dans les mains association to present her outstanding project for young people. These moments are very important for forging close links between EPVs, but also between associations working together on issues that help to ensure that exceptional expertise continues to thrive and shine.
