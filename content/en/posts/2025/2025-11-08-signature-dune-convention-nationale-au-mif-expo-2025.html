8 of  November 2025 , during the MIF Expo in Paris  a strong moment marked the shared commitment of Réseau  Excellence  and Association de l'or dans les mains  : the signature of a national convention to strengthen the transmission of expertise of excellence to younger generations.
Around this signature, were gathered:


Eric Angiboust , Vice-President of Réseau Excellence,

Nicolas Merveilleux , President of REMY GARNIER, President of the Association 

de l'or dans les mains   and Treasurer of Réseau Excellence Île-de-France,


Gabrielle Légeret , Director General and founder of 

de l'or dans les mains   .

All recalled how the preservation of French know-how is strategic for our territories, businesses and the future of young people .

A convention for the transmission of trades of excellence

This Convention formalises a new national partnership , enabling the Enterprises of the Heritage Living (EPV) - ambassadors of French know-how - with the educational device worn by the association  de l'or dans les mains. Objective:
To discover Grade 5 students the passion for artisanal and technical gestures, thanks to the intervention of a voluntary employee made available in partner schools. This action enhances the philanthropy of the competences of the Enterprises of the Heritages Living (EPV).

The regions concerned by the scheme

Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes

Burgundy-Franche-Comté

Centre-Val de Loire

Upper France

Île-de-France

Normandy

New Aquitaine
Country of the Loire

A structuring partnership for youth and living heritage

This reconciliation between Entreprises of Heritage Living and the association 

de l'or dans les mains   marks one major step in raising young people's awareness of crafts, crafts of excellence and French industry .
By pooling their strengths, the two organizations affirm a common conviction:
The transmission of know-how is a key issue for the future of our territories and businesses.
