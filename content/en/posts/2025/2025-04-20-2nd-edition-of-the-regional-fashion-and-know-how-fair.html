On 11 and 12 April 2025, twelve regional fashion players were
present at the 2nd edition of the fashion and know-how fair
territory at the Pavillon du Verdurier in Limoges including companies
labeled Heritage Company Living like: Cheri Bibi , manufacture of luxury hats and hats in Combressol (Corrèze); House Broussaud : manufacture of socks for 3 generations in Les Cars (Haute-Vienne);
J. Mr. Weston : shoe manufacture since 1891 in Limoges Haute-Vienne°.

"This show represents more than just a merchant event.
It is a showcase of authenticity and know-how that characterizes
the Limousin, says Alexandra Broussaud, president of the
Trade Union for Textiles, Clothing and Leather whose company is a member of Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine. In
bringing together these passionate actors in the heart of Limoges, we make
to our heritage and highlight the talents of our
region. »
