Discover the practical guide for artisans wishing to export their business to the United States summary: >-

A new guide accompanies French artisans who want to develop their activity in the United States, a booming market driven by the attraction for "French taste".
It highlights the opportunities, cultural keys and methods needed to succeed in sustainable implementation.
Designed by the National Manufactures and the Villa Albertine, it brings together expert advice and inspiring testimonials to guide designers step by step.
