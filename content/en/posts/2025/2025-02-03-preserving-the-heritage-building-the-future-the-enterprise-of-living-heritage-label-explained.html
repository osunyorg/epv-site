The Creuse Chamber of Commerce and Industry, in partnership with the Prefecture of the Creuse and Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine, organized an event to raise awareness among local businesses about the Enterprise Label of Living Heritage (EPV), symbol of excellence and unique know-how.

body: >-


This Thursday, January 30, 2025, at the initiative of  Creuse CCI Jean-François Tixier, in partnership with  Prefecture of Creuse and Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine, held the meeting "Preserving the heritage, building the future: the label Enterprise of Living Heritage explained" at the Cité de la Tapisserie , in Aubusson. It was addressed to the companies and partners of Corrèze, Creuse and Haute-Vienne. The participants were welcomed by Emmanuel Gérard, Director of the City of Tapestry and his teams. The meeting discussed:
Label Enterprise of Living Heritage
The mobilization of consular networks of New Aquitaine, CCI and CMA, to accompany companies on the EPV label (point on the company's strategy and the reasons for the company's willingness to ask for the label, impact of the label ...)
Support for the   Leather Sector Luxury Textile and Crafts of Art of the Nouvelle-Aquitaine Region


Excellence Network New Aquitaine, the all-new regional association of the Companies of the Heritage Vivant Nouvelle-Aquitaine, represented by two members of the office, Karine Loiseau and Benoit de Laruzière

Geographical Indications of Aubusson Carpets and Tapestry with the intervention of Géraldine Cauchy, director of Lainamac
The testimonials of Creuse's EPVs:

Atelier A2 : Odile Perrin-Crinière

Ateliers Pinton : Liveta Coibion

Fonderies Fraisse : Emmanuel Déhé


At the end of the meeting, Anne Frackowiak-Jacobs, Prefect of Creuse, officially presented the company label of the Heritage Living 

Fonderies Fraisse  labeled in 2024.

This Thursday, January 30, 2025, at the initiative of Creuse CCI (external link)Jean-François Tixier, in partnership with Prefecture of Creuse (external link)and Réseau Excellence Nouvelle-Aquitaine, held the meeting "Preserving the heritage, building the future: the label Enterprise of Living Heritage explained" at the Cité de la Tapisserie, in Aubusson. It was addressed to the companies and partners of Corrèze, Creuse and Haute-Vienne.

The participants were welcomed by Emmanuel Gérard, Director of the City of Tapestry (external link) and his teams.

The meeting discussed:

  • Label Enterprise of Living Heritage (external link)
  • The mobilization of consular networks of New Aquitaine, CCI and CMA, to accompany companies on the EPV label (point on the company's strategy and the reasons for the company's willingness to ask for the label, impact of the label ...)
  • Support for the  Leather Sector Luxury Textile and Crafts of Art of the Nouvelle-Aquitaine Region (external link)
  • Excellence Network New Aquitaine, (external link)the all-new regional association of the Companies of the Heritage Vivant Nouvelle-Aquitaine, represented by two members of the office, Karine Loiseau and Benoit de Laruzière
  • Geographical Indications of Aubusson Carpets and Tapestry (external link)with the intervention of Géraldine Cauchy, director of Lainamac
  • The testimonials of Creuse's EPVs:
    • Atelier A2 (external link): Odile Perrin-Crinière
    • Ateliers Pinton (external link): Liveta Coibion
    • Fonderies Fraisse (external link): Emmanuel Déhé

At the end of the meeting, Anne Frackowiak-Jacobs, Prefect of Creuse, officially presented the company label of the Heritage Living  Fonderies Fraisse (external link) labeled in 2024.
