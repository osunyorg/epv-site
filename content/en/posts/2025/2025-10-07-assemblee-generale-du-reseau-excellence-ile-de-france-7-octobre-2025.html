The 7 October 2025 , the Excellence Network EPV Île-de-France held its Annual General Meeting in a symbolic and inspiring place: Goldsmithing , former emblematic site of the house Cristofle , now an exceptional space at the service of crafts and welcoming many residents labeled EPV. A framework in perfect harmony with Network values: transmission, excellence, territorial anchoring and innovation . The collective of the Orfèvrerie is chaired by Romain Gazzola and Camille Weyl for communication, which we would like to thank for their welcome and help in organizing our gathering. The General Assembly was led by Emmanuelle Andrieux , President of the Excellence Network EPV Île-de-France, surrounded by the Board of Directors :

Valentin Goux Vice-President

Nicolas Wonderful Treasurer

Benoît Béguin Secretary-General

Franck Isel , Alternate Secretary-General
The Assembly was an opportunity to: actions carried out in 2024 and 2025 , to mention the development prospects of the network, and especially of strengthening links between members on joint projects. Thanks to the companies for their mobilisation! This AG has brought together many  Enterprises of Living Heritage , outstanding know-how carriers in Île-de-France, including:  REMY GARNIER · BRUGIER Workshops · RINCK · CARRAFONT · Potel et Chabot · FRAMEX · ARTMETAL-COINDEROUX · Maison Rennotte · DAUMET · WORKSHOP LOUIS DEL BOCA · Cornu · Atelier THIERY · La Maison du Vitrail · Maison HERINK · MALEVILLE · Atelier Pictet · Espacestaff Volumes Thanks to each of them for their presence, their commitment, and their active contribution to The influence of the artisanal and industrial excellence of France . "Together, let's keep our exceptional know-how alive Through this General Assembly, the Excellence Network EPV Île-de-France confirms its commitment to federate talent , of support collective initiatives and promoting the trades and know-how of excellence that make our territory rich.
