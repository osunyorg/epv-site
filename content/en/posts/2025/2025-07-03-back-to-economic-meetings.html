An afternoon of exchanges for the 20 years of the EPV label

On 3 July, as part of the 20th anniversary of the "Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV) label, the Bercy Forum hosted a strong time of economic exchange from 15:30 to 18:00, bringing together companies, institutions and partners.
A dozen booths allowed participants to engage with key players in the development of VTEs:

SGS ICS, label manager,

INPI, for intellectual property,

Excellence Network, with testimonials from companies like Gold in your hands ,

CMA France and CCI France,

Business France, on export,

Company and Discovery, around the tourism of know-how,
and several branches of the Ministry of Economy: DGE, DAE, DGDDI, DGCCRF.
The event took place in the presence of Mrs Véronique Louwagie, Minister Delegate responsible for Enterprise, testifying to the strong commitment of the public authorities and their unwavering support for the EVP label and the companies it represents. Finally, visitors were able to discover the photo exhibition "Richesse", carried out by Jérôme Poulalier for the AURA region, highlighting the know-how of the EPVs through a series of sensitive and inspiring portraits.
A moment of encounter and valorisation that prolongs the collective dynamics around the EPV label, at the service of French excellence.
