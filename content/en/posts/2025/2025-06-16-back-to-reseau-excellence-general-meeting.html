On the morning of June 16, Tristan de Witte, President of Réseau Excellence, gathered the members of the Board of Directors of each Regional Réseau Excellence network to hold the Ordinary General Assembly. This event took place in a prestigious setting: the National Assembly, a strong symbol of our Republic.
This meeting was made possible thanks to the support of the Member of Parliament, Mr.Fait, whom we warmly thank for his commitment to Living Heritage Enterprises. The presence of Minister Louwagie's office also marked the importance attached by the public authorities to the exceptional know-how we represent.
This moment of exchange and governance allowed us to take stock of the actions carried out at national and regional level, to validate the main directions to come, and to reaffirm our common will to value and bring to light French artisanal and industrial excellence.
