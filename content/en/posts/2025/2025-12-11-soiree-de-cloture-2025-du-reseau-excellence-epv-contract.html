On December 11, 2025, the Réseau Excellence EPV Contract organized its annual closing evening, bringing together all of its ecosystem around the founding values of Life Heritage Enterprises (EPV): excellence in know-how, transmission and innovation for contemporary projects.

body: >-


This meeting allowed us to take stock of a year marked by the strengthening of the links between the companies certified EPV and the actors of prescription - architects, designers, decorators, hoteliers, partners and institutions - in a structured and sustainable collective dynamic.

A structured community serving French excellence

Throughout 2025, the Réseau Excellence EPV  Contract has pursued its objective of federation and of valuing exceptional know-how. The closing evening illustrated the maturity of a committed ecosystem, united by a common requirement of quality, meaning and sustainability.
The Réseau Excellence EPV Contract thus affirms its role as a platform for dialogue and cooperation between EPV companies and prescribers, contributing to the outreach of contemporary French excellence, anchored in the territories and oriented towards the future.

Presentation of the Réseau Excellence EPV Contract 2026 Ambassadors'

The evening was also an opportunity to introduce the new Ambassadors of the Réseau Excellence EPV  Contract . Their appointment is part of a desire to structure and amplified the actions carried out, in order to strengthen the bridges between the EVPs and the actors of today's and tomorrow's projects.
New Ambassadors 2026: Guillaume de Saint Lager, Arnaud Lonent, Anastasia Andrieu, Michel Cottray, Valérie Bensoussan (Studio Friedmann & Versace), Mélanie Leroy Rapoport, Julien Sebban, Soline Rouland (Wilmotte & Associés Architectes), Patrick Jouin, Jacques-Olivier Chauvin, François Azambourg, Fabien Roque, Oscar Lucien Ono, MAISON A&G avec Gabrielle Joinau Tavernier, Laurent Delporte, Dorothéée Delaye, Marc Hertrich, Sarah Hamon, Gilles Bonan, Enzo Storti, Carole de Bona-Védrine, Sylvie Leroy, Cyril Meaudre, Armelle Weisman.

Presentation of strategic planning 2026

Along with Tristan De Witte, the Réseau Excellence EPV Contract  Contract revealed the main directions of its 2026 planning. This ambitious and structured roadmap aims to step up the networking, transmission and accompanying actions of the companies labeled EPV, while developing new forms of collaboration and visibility.
The year 2026 is thus a strategic step to consolidate the achievements and accelerate the deployment of the Network's initiatives.

Tanguy de Montesson
Presentation of the HR and Training survey with the Campus de Versailles

Réseau Excellence, in partnership with the Versailles Campus , launches an investigation dedicated to HR skills and issues of companies EPV . Campus, real know-how campus , intervenes on five channels of excellence : gastronomy, built heritage, crafts and design, hospitality and tourism, horticulture and landscape throughout France .
The purpose of the questionnaire is to identify recruitment and training needs, to better understand the challenges of talent and to develop common approaches to training and transfer of know-how. Lead in a framework strictly confidential and in accordance with GDPR  The survey will run from January to April 2026, with a collective return of the results to the members of the Excellence Network.

Human at the heart of collective performance

Au-delà des perspectives stratégiques, cette soirée a rappelé une conviction essentielle : l'excellence des savoir-faire repose avant tout sur l'humain. La maîtrise du geste, la transmission intergénérationnelle, l'ancrage territorial et l'engagement collectif constituent le socle des Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant et de l'action du Réseau Excellence EPV Contract .
Le Réseau Excellence EPV Contract remercie l'ensemble des participants pour leur présence et leur engagement, ainsi que ses ambassadeurs et partenaires pour leur confiance renouvelée
