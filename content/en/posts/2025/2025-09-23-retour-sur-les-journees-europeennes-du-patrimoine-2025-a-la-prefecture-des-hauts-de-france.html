On Sunday 21 September, the Prefecture of Hauts-de-France in Lille has exceptionally opened its doors to host a unique event dedicated to Enterprises of the Heritage Living (EPV), in partnership with Excellence Network.

This meeting brought to light the rare and precious know-how that make our region rich.

body: >-

Outstanding artisans and businesses

Throughout the day, visitors were able to meet those who passionately perpetuate the artisanal and industrial excellence of Hauts-de-France. A big congratulations and thanks to the companies present:

Atelier Patrice Bricout (restored furniture)
Art)

Brasserie 3 Monts  (Flemish guard beers)

Chicorée du Nord  (traditional and
sustainable)

Etablissement J. Toulemonde (high-end textile threads)

Maison Meert (gaufers and gourmets Heritage)

Tissage de la Lys - Manufacture Jules Pansu (textiles
Jacquard)

Stoelzle Masnières Parfumerie  (luxury glass bottles)
Sophie
Hallette, Peignage Dumortier, Parqueterie de la Lys ...

A rich and inspiring program

A stand dedicated to the label EPV improved understanding of the excellence criteria and values of this network of companies: transmission, innovation, French know-how . The event highlighted the dynamism and diversity of the living heritage in all sectors: craft, industry, textile, gourmet, wood, glass...

A day of sharing

We extend our sincere thanks to Mr. Prefect and the Prefecture teams for their welcome and valuable collaboration. Thanks also to all the passionate visitors who have come in numbers to celebrate with us this living heritage which is the pride of the Hauts-de-France.
