The Friday, 5 December, the Prefecture of the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region hosted the official ceremony of award of diplomas Living Heritage Company (EPV), a highlight of excellence, transmission and collective pride.

body: >-


21 companies in the region their EPV diploma, a national distinction which recognizes the exceptional know-how often rare, innovative and deeply rooted in the territories. These companies embody a living heritage , actively contributing to the economic, cultural and artisanal influence of the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region.
Through their long-term commitment, their ability to pass on skills and develop skilled trades, the EPV winners are fully involved in regional dynamism and in the valorisation of fine crafts, industry and crafts.
The Réseau Excellence   EPV PACA  was represented at this ceremony by Guillaume Fievet , it itself renewed EPV, proud to carry the voice of the network and to support these companies that make the richness and singularity of our regional economic heritage. We warmly thank the Prefecture of the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region for its reception and support, as well as all the partners mobilized for this ceremony, marked by inspiration, sharing and collective recognition.

The companies EPV 2025 in Provence-Alpes-Côte

Atelier de la Boiserie - Cassis (13)

Atelier Marcel Carbonel - Marseille (13)

Santons Campana - Saint-Chamas (13)

Atelier Bidal - Marseille (13)

Ferronnerie d'Art Gaudin - L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue (84)

Rampal Patou - Gardanne (13)

Le Garde Temps - Apt (84)

Callistorea - Saint-Laurent-du-Var (06)

La Verrerie de Biot - Biot (06)

La Compagnie du Midi - Carros (06)

Ragni - La Trinité (06)

Blachère Illumination - Aubagne (13)

Méhari Club Cassis (MCC) - Marseille (13)

Ravel Decroix et Fils - Aubagne (13)

Marius Bernard - Gargas (84)

Conserves Guintrand - Salon-de-Provence (13)

D. Maffren - Carpentras (84)

Laboratoire LAC - La Garde (06)

Chantier Borg - Marseille (13)
Savonnerie Marseillaise de la Licorne - Le Poët (05)
