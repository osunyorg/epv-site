Three intense days, rich in encounters, discoveries... and emotions! A big thank you to our 16 committed companies that have brought the excellence and know-how of our territory to life: Alélor De Laine En Rêves Literie Laine Du Grand Est E.BOEHM - Art Textile LA FABRIQUE MARTINELLI Henryot et Cie 1867 HERVE MAYON Lalicorneverte La Maison Luquet LE BATIMENT ASSOCIE Les Ateliers Sacker et Massé LES CONFITURES DU CLIMONT PARQUETERIE JANOD 1930 Nogent 3 étoiles NORKI Oller- Philippe CHARPENTIER Ridremont & Brosserie Nouvelle SOUCHET │ INSPIRED WOODWORK More than 575 visitors responded present to discover an exhibition that aroused a real enthusiasm. Friday highlight: the labelling of a century-old tree by the association A.R.B.R.E.S., with the support of the State Secretariat for Biodiversity. The evening continued with the signing of a strategic partnership between MADEiN Grand Est and the Excellence Network EPV Grand Est, in the presence of: Sonia LINARD  President of the EPV Grand East Reseau Excellence  Dominique Roitel  Chairman of MADEiN Grand-Est Christophe Richard  Chairman of the Grand East CMA Sophie Delong , Regional Councillor of the Haute-Marne A major step forward in valuing our know-how and a dynamic that we intend to amplify! We would like to extend our sincere thanks to Mr. Jean‐Claude Volot, owner of the Abbey of Auberive, and to his daughter, Mrs. Alexia Volot, for their warm welcome. See you next year, where we expect you even more!

Three intense days, rich in encounters, discoveries... and emotions!
A big thank you to our 16 committed companies that have brought the excellence and know-how of our territory to life:

Alélor
De Laine En Rêves Literie Laine Du Grand Est
E.BOEHM - Art Textile
LA FABRIQUE MARTINELLI
Henryot et Cie 1867
HERVE MAYON Lalicorneverte
La Maison Luquet
LE BATIMENT ASSOCIE
Les Ateliers Sacker et Massé
LES CONFITURES DU CLIMONT
PARQUETERIE JANOD 1930
Nogent 3 étoiles
NORKI
Oller-Philippe CHARPENTIER
Ridremont & Brosserie Nouvelle
SOUCHET │ INSPIRED WOODWORK

More than 575 visitors responded present to discover an exhibition that aroused a real enthusiasm.

Friday highlight: the labelling of a century-old tree by the association A.R.B.R.E.S., with the support of the State Secretariat for Biodiversity.

The evening continued with the signing of a strategic partnership between MADEiN Grand Est and the Excellence Network EPV Grand Est, in the presence of:
Sonia LINARD President of the EPV Grand East Reseau Excellence 
Dominique Roitel Chairman of MADEiN Grand-Est
Christophe Richard Chairman of the Grand East CMA
Sophie Delong, Regional Councillor of the Haute-Marne

A major step forward in valuing our know-how and a dynamic that we intend to amplify!

We would like to extend our sincere thanks to Mr. Jean‐Claude Volot, owner of the Abbey of Auberive, and to his daughter, Mrs. Alexia Volot, for their warm welcome.

See you next year, where we expect you even more!
