On February 6, 2025, Tristan de Witte and Eric Angiboust, President and Vice-President of the Réseau Excellence, had the honour of being received by Senators Annick Billon and Martine Berthet to discuss the crucial issues of the EPV label, and in particular the joint actions undertaken to restore the budget line of the 2025 Finance Bill currently under discussion by the Joint Joint Committee.

body: >-


It was thanks to an unprecedented mobilisation that the EVP label was saved from a threat to its sustainability:
The 2025 Finance Bill threatened to remove the credits allocated to the label, questioning its existence
These budget cuts jeopardized not only the management of the label, but also its promotion, which was essential for our companies and their competitiveness.


Many thanks to all the senators who supported us and worked in defence of the EVP label: Mr. Jean-Baptiste LEMOYNE and its co-signatories: Ms. Valérie Boyer, Mr. Buis and Mr. Foussin, Ms. Phinera-Horth, Mr. Levi, Ms. Cazebonne, Ms. Havet, Ms. Devésa, Mr. Jouve, Mr. Saint-Pé, Mr. Ramia, Ms. Sollogoub and Ms. Romagny, Mr. Wattebled, Buval and Longeot, Mrs. Frédérique Espagnac , Mrs. Annick Billon Mr Delcros and Mr Jean-Michel Arnaud, Ms Perrot, Mr Duffourg, Ms Vermeillet, Ms Olivia Richard and Mr de La Provôté, Mr Jean-Michel Arnaud, Mr Duffourg and Mr De La Provôté, Mr Jean-Michel Arnaud, and his co-signatories. Perrion, Parigi and Capus, Mrs. Martine Berthet . We also take advantage of this message to thank the strengths of our EPV Excellence network, our partners and colleagues for this wonderful mobilization of recent months!
