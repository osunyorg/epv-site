The 17 June 2025, 26 companies from Occitanie were honoured at an official graduation ceremony organized by the prefecture of the region.

body: >-


In the presence of  Pierre-André Durand , Prefect of the Occitanie region and Prefect of the Haute-Garonne, these companies have obtained their diploma of label "Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV). Exceptional know-how in the heart of the region Each labeled company embodies rare know-how, transmitted from generation to generation. The EPV label rewards their craft and industrial excellence, their commitment to the territory and their contribution to the preservation of trades that make the cultural and economic richness of Occitanie. A ceremony of pride and excellence The ceremony on 17 June was held in a convivial and solemn atmosphere.  LORE CAMILLO , President of Réseau Excellence  Occitanie , and the Prefect  Pierre-André Durand , both committed to the recognition of regional talents. Their mobilization contributed significantly to the success of this event, which was marked by the  French know-how .

In the presence of Pierre-André Durand, Prefect of the Occitanie region and Prefect of the Haute-Garonne, these companies have obtained their diploma of label "Enterprise du Patrimoine Vivant" (EPV).

Exceptional know-how in the heart of the region

Each labeled company embodies rare know-how, transmitted from generation to generation. The EPV label rewards their craft and industrial excellence, their commitment to the territory and their contribution to the preservation of trades that make the cultural and economic richness of Occitanie.

A ceremony of pride and excellence

The ceremony on 17 June was held in a convivial and solemn atmosphere. LORE CAMILLO, President of Réseau Excellence Occitanie, and the Prefect Pierre-André Durand, both committed to the recognition of regional talents. Their mobilization contributed significantly to the success of this event, which was marked by the French know-how.
