On March 5, 2025, the Hôtel de l'Industrie hosted an exceptional evening for the launch of Réseau Excellence Contract, an event that brought together passionate and committed actors to highlight the exceptional know-how of French companies labeled EPV in the areas of hospitality, luxury, decoration and interior architecture.

body: >-


The warm and friendly atmosphere allowed all guests, actors of architecture, design and luxury, to share moments of exchange and networking in a spirit of collaboration. This new initiative, led by Réseau Excellence , is intended as a platform to connect companies with the EPV label to development opportunities. We would like to thank Tristan Rodriguez, President of

Réseau Excellence

Contract, for his vision and commitment, Jean-Michel Wilmotte, Honorary Ambassador of

Réseau Excellence Contract, and Tristan De Witte, President of Réseau Excellence, for their unwavering support and key role in the success of this initiative. We would also like to thank all the leaders of the EPVs and our guests for making this evening a memorable and promising moment for the future. We look forward to seeing this network grow and offer EPV companies unique opportunities to shine. Thank you to all those who helped make this evening unforgettable. Together, let's shine French excellence!

How to join Réseau Excellence Contract

I join Réseau Excellence . Your company is labeled EPV but not yet a member of the network? Join the association in your region.


I subscribe to a Contract  pack . Are you a member of the network? Subscribe to one of 3 Contract packs (the launch cocktail of March 5 is included in the price).
