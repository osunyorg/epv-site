This international recognition salutes close to twenty years of efforts to transform the site and offer an experience always richer to our visitors.

In 2005, Eric Papon, 7th generation, chose to reinvent the image of petrification. The site is transformed into a depth: the route is entirely redesigned to showcase a natural know-how unique to world. Today, the "Petrifying Fountains" impose themselves as a must in Auvergne, where science, nature and art unite in a spectacular setting.

The regional association of EPV Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes is proud to have among its members this exceptional company, now crowned by the Michelin Guide.

Further information: Michelin Guide Petrifying Fountains
