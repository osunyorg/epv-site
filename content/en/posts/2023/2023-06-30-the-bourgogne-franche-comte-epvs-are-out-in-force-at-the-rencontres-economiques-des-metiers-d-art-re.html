A partnership has been formalized with the Campus des Métiers et des Qualifications Maroquinerie et Métiers d'Art to align the training courses offered by our establishments in the arts and crafts sectors with the needs of companies in these sectors. This synergy is essential to enhance the value of the sector and facilitate recruitment.

The EPVs then headed off to the Rencontres Economiques des Métiers d'Art for a day devoted to the promotion and development of know-how in France, of which the EPVs are major players. This was an opportunity to attend highly instructive round tables and to meet a number of industry personalities.
