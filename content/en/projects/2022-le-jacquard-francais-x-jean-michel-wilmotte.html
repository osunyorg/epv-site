An exclusive collaboration Architect Jean-Michel Wilmotte's research into Jacquard weaving has drawn on his artistic sensibility to create a resolutely timeless collection. Jean-Michel Wilmotte's work on composition and the balance of shapes and colors has focused on rediscovering the self-evident character of the object and the sensuality of the material. New "Bath Club" collection For the Autumn-Winter season, the Club by Wilmotte collection joins the world of bathrooms and spas with the new Bath Club range. Lightweight, technical 100% cotton creations, woven in double honeycomb in the Vosges. An architectural collection where fabrics come to life like the sketchbook dear to Jean-Michel Wilmotte. The constructive gesture of architectural structures and weaving weaves respond and harmonize to give rise to a confidential collection whose contemporary lines and deep colors lend sophistication and elegance. Sleek and intimate, Bath Club is an invitation to relaxation and well-being. Let's join the Club

An exclusive collaboration

Architect Jean-Michel Wilmotte's research into Jacquard weaving has drawn on his artistic sensibility to create a resolutely timeless collection.

Jean-Michel Wilmotte's work on composition and the balance of shapes and colors has focused on rediscovering the self-evident character of the object and the sensuality of the material.

New "Bath Club" collection

For the Autumn-Winter season, the Club by Wilmotte collection joins the world of bathrooms and spas with the new Bath Club range.

Lightweight, technical 100% cotton creations, woven in double honeycomb in the Vosges. An architectural collection where fabrics come to life like the sketchbook dear to Jean-Michel Wilmotte.

The constructive gesture of architectural structures and weaving weaves respond and harmonize to give rise to a confidential collection whose contemporary lines and deep colors lend sophistication and elegance.

Sleek and intimate, Bath Club is an invitation to relaxation and well-being. Let's join the Club
